She regularly works for [[Maryland Championship Wrestling]]. ==Early life and career start== During her teens years, Heiser was overweight; never imagining of one day stepping into the ring herself. With courage, discipline, motivation and help from a local professional wrestler, who was signed to a developmental contract with the WWE, she was able to lose over 60 lbs and become an athlete.
