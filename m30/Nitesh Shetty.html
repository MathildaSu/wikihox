He has studied in St. Josephs Boys’ High School in [[Bangalore]]. He has played professional [[tennis]] and was ranked top 10 in India in the juniors and the state of [[Karnataka]]. He has also represented his school for 3 years in [[cricket]]. ==Entrepreneurial ventures== Nitesh started his first business in September 1997 at the age of 19, with a capital of INR 12,000 (USD 300) borrowed from his mother. He started a billboard business for advertising in Bangalore under ‘Serve and Volley’ brand. In 2004 he got into property development and founded Nitesh Estates Limited.{{cite news|url=http://www.icicitrinity.com/biography_nitesh_shetty.aspx|title=Nitesh Shetty taking real estate to the next level with Nitesh Group of Companies|publisher=[[ICICI]] Trinity|date=9 April 2013}} He first developed a property on MG road (Bangalore), where he already had a billboard and relationship with the landowner. He did a joint venture and developed an office building.{{cite news|last= Pinto |first= Jackie| url=http://indulge.newindianexpress.com/the-game-changer/bangalore/cover-story/2344|title=The game changer|publisher=[[The New Indian Express]]|accessdate=8 December 2014|date=15 November 2013}} In 2006 he bought two land parcels in Race-Course road and Kasturba road from a German MNC and HNI family in New York and built a residential development.
