Perr&Knight provides insurance support services consisting of [[Actuarial science | actuarial]] consulting, [[competitive intelligence | competitive intelligence]], data services, insurance technology, [[regulatory compliance | regulatory compliance]] and risk services. The firm maintains offices in Santa Monica, [[Boca Raton | Boca Raton]], the [[New York metropolitan area | New York Metro Area]] and [[Ft. Worth | Ft. Worth]]. Perr&Knight is among the top 20 [[Property insurance | property]] and [[Casualty insurance | casualty]] actuarial firms in the United States.{{cite journal|title=Top Audit and Actuarial Firms|journal=Best's Review|date=January 2015|issue=9|page=31-32|url=http://viewer.zmags.com/publication/6292b9a8#/6292b9a8/34}} Among the firm’s professionals are more than 25 Fellows and Associates of the [[Casualty Actuarial Society | Casualty Actuarial Society]] and members of the [[American Academy of Actuaries | American Academy of Actuaries]]. == History == Perr&Knight was founded in 1994 in [[Pacific Palisades, California| Pacific Palisades]], California by Tim Perr and Scott Knight. The firm's actuarial consulting services were limited to property and casualty lines of insurance until 2009 when the firm added their first accredited health [[actuary | actuary]]. This addition expanded Perr&Knight's capabilities to include accident and health actuarial consulting services.{{cite web|title=Perr&Knight Adds Texas Actuary|url=http://www.insurancejournal.com/news/southcentral/2009/09/28/104115.htm|website=Insurance Journal|publisher=Wells Media Group, Inc.|accessdate=2015}} In 2011, the firm relocated its headquarters from Pacific Palisades to the downtown district of [[Santa Monica, California|Santa Monica]], California.{{cite web|title=Perr&Knight Relocates California Headquarters to Santa Monica|url=http://www.insurancejournal.com/news/west/2011/10/24/221318.htm#|website=Insurance Journal|publisher=Wells Media Group, Inc|accessdate = 2015}} === RateFilings.com === RateFilings.com, an online database of competitor rate, rule and form filings, was created and launched by Perr&Knight in 1998.{{cite web|title=Perr & Knight Launches RateFilings.com 2.0|url=http://www.lexdon.com/article/perr_&_knight_launches_ratefilings.com/229956.html|website=Lexdon|publisher=Lexdon Business Library|accessdate=2015}} The website became the largest online source of competitor filings and a market leader in providing [[competitive intelligence | competitive intelligence]] to insurance companies.
