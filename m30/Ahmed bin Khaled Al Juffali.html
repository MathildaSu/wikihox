Ahmed began his schooling at the [[British International School of Jeddah]] and then at the Dar al Fikr Schools. He then continued his studies in the U.S where he will be graduating from [[The Winchendon School]]. ==Personal life== He is known as a car enthusiast, he dives in [[Jeddah]], and is an amateur [[Jet Pack]] flyer, where he was taught as a beginner in [[Southern France]]. He is known for his strong values of entrepreneurship, and modesty, due to the teachings of his father.
