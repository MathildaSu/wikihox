July 19, 2012]] '''Benjamin Charles "Ben" Fankhauser''' (born May 11, 1990)[http://www.famousbirthdays.com/people/ben-fankhauser.html] is an actor and singer. He's been performing in the theatre since middle school and continued throughout college. He made his Broadway debut in the 2012 [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] musical ''[[Newsies (musical)|Newsies]]'', as newsboy "Davey," and also originated the role in the 2011 [[Paper Mill Playhouse]] production. ==Early life and education== Ben Fankhauser was born in [[Zürich|Zurich]], [[Switzerland]] and was raised in [[Cleveland]], [[Ohio]]{{Cite web|title = PLAYBILL.COM'S CUE & A: Newsies' Ben Fankhauser|url = http://www.playbill.com/news/article/playbill.coms-cue-a-newsies-ben-fankhauser-209282|accessdate = 2015-09-01}} with his parents and older brother.{{Cite web|title = Playbill's Cue & A|url = http://www.playbill.com/news/article/playbill.coms-cue-a-newsies-ben-fankhauser-209282|website = Playbill|accessdate = 2015-09-01}} He attended [[Ithaca College]] and graduated in 2011, after taking off a year to be a part of the first national tour of [[Tony Award]]-winning musical ''[[Spring Awakening (musical)|Spring Awakening]].''{{Cite web|title = All the Worlds His Stage - Main Stage Theater - Ithaca College|url = http://www.ithaca.edu/hs/depts/theatre/mainstage/news/all-the-worlds-his-stage-17803/#.VeT54M745UQ|website = www.ithaca.edu|accessdate = 2015-09-01}} He then returned to school in fall of 2010 in order to graduate on time with his class. ==Broadway== Ben made his [[Broadway theatre|Broadway]] debut in 2012 as part of the original cast of Disney's ''[[Newsies (musical)|Newsies]]''.{{Cite web|title = Ben Fankhauser Theatre Credits|url = http://www.broadwayworld.com/people/Ben-Fankhauser/|website = www.broadwayworld.com|accessdate = 2015-09-01}} The show won Best Score and Best Chroeography at the 2012 [[Tony Awards]].{{Cite web|title = Disney NEWSIES {{!}} Official Site for NEWSIES On Tour {{!}} About the Show|url = http://www.newsiesthemusical.com|website = www.newsiesthemusical.com|accessdate = 2015-09-01}} He originated the role of "Davey," which he also played in the 2011 [[Paper Mill Playhouse]] production prior to the Broadway transfer of the show. ===Other theatre=== Fankhauser has performed in various shows in the theatre since middle school. One of his first roles was playing "Crutchie" in a children's production of ''Newsies'' at his theatre day camp.{{Cite web|title = PLAYBILL.COM'S CUE & A: Newsies' Ben Fankhauser|url = http://www.playbill.com/news/article/playbill.coms-cue-a-newsies-ben-fankhauser-209282/P2|accessdate = 2015-09-01}} He was involved in shows during college at Ithaca, including ''[[A Midsummer Night's Dream|A Midsummer Night’s Dream]], [[A New Brain]], Metamorphoses'' and ''[[Chicago (musical)|Chicago]]''. He then played the role of "Ernst" in first national tour of ''Spring Awakening'' from 2009-2010. In York Theatre's off-Broadway production of [[Stephen Sondheim|Sondheim's]] ''Saturday Night'', Ben played "Gene." The show was a limited run and ran November 8–16, 2014. Ben has also performed regionally in shows including ''[[Next to Normal]]'' at The North Carolina Theatre. ''Next to Normal'' details the story of a family struggling as mother, "Diana Goodman" battles bipolar disorder. He played the role of "Henry," who is the love interest of daughter, "Natalie Goodman" in the show. The show ran from May 1–10, 2015 at the A.J. Fletcher Opera Theater in [[Raleigh, North Carolina|Raleigh]]. In the upcoming first national tour of ''[[Beautiful: The Carole King Musical]]'', Fankhauser will play the role of [[Barry Mann]], alongside Abby Mueller as [[Carole King]], Liam Tobin as [[Gerry Goffin]] and Becky Gulsvig as [[Cynthia Weil]]. The tour launches September 2015 in [[Providence, Rhode Island]].{{Cite web|title = Liam Tobin, Becky Gulsvig & Ben Fankhauser Join the National Tour of Beautiful: The Carole King Musical|url = http://www.broadway.com/buzz/181724/liam-tobin-becky-gulsvig-ben-fankhauser-join-the-national-tour-of-beautiful-the-carole-king-musical/|accessdate = 2015-09-01}} ==TV/Web series appearances== Ben has appeared on television series including "[[Submissions Only]]," created by Kate Wetherhead and [[Andrew Keenan-Bolger]] (2012) and "It Could Be Worse," created by [[Wesley Taylor]] and Mitchell Jarvis (2014).{{Cite web|title = Ben Fankhauser|url = http://www.imdb.com/name/nm5025768/|website = IMDb|accessdate = 2015-09-01}} ===Other=== Ben is a frequent singer/guest at [[54 Below]], [[Birdland (New York jazz club)|Birdland Jazz Club]] and other cabaret venues in [[New York City|New York]].
