He's currently attending high school at [[Westside Secondary School]] in [[Ontario]]. ==Background== Vandervaart knew he wanted to act when he saw his first professional play at the age of 5. He has performed on screen and stage including ''[[Our Town]]'' and ''[[A Month in the Country (play)|A Month in the Country]]'' with the [[Soulpepper|Soulpepper Theatre Company]] in Toronto.  Television credits include appearances in ''Lifetime's At Risk'', ''Disney's [[Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam]]'', ''Global's [[Bomb Girls]]'', ''MuchMusic's [[The L.A. Complex]]'', ''BravoFACT'' short film ''Miskate'', CBC's {{Cite news|url=http://www.cbc.ca/murdochmysteries/|title=Murdoch Mysteries|access-date=2017-07-06|language=en}}[[Murdoch Mysteries]] and a pilot episode for a new CBC family Drama ''Port Hope''. He has also appeared in a number of national commercials including, a career highlight for him, working with Jarome Iginla, captain of the Calgary Flames, on a ScotiaBank commercial.
