OYO Rooms currently operates in more than 120 Indian cities including Delhi, Gurgaon, Jaipur, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Goa, Chennai, Kolkata and others. OYO is present in major metros, regional hubs, leisure destinations and pilgrimage towns. According to a research carried out by CB Insights for ''The New York Times'', OYO Rooms is among the companies that may be the next start-up unicorns.{{Cite web|title = 50 Companies That May Be the Next Start-Up Unicorns|url = http://bits.blogs.nytimes.com/2015/08/23/here-are-the-companies-that-may-be-the-next-50-start-up-unicorns/?_r=0|accessdate = 2015-08-25|first = The New York|last = Times}} The company is backed by investors like the Softbank Group, Greenoaks Capital, Sequoia Capital and Lightspeed India.{{Cite news|title = OYO Rooms raises Rs 630 cr in funding led by SoftBank|url = http://www.thehindu.com/business/oyo-rooms-raises-rs-630-cr-in-funding-led-by-softbank/article7495373.ece|newspaper = The Hindu|date = 2015-08-03|access-date = 2015-08-25|issn = 0971-751X|language = en-IN}} OYO Rooms provides standardized hotel rooms with features such as an air-conditioned room, complimentary breakfast and Wi-Fi with 24x7 customer service support. Guests can use the OYO Rooms App for booking rooms on the go. They can also order beverages and request room service through the app. Hotel owners who partner with OYO Rooms are connected via world’s first tablet based property management App and become part of the OYO brand.{{Cite web|title = Why A 21-Year-Old Is Building OYO As An Uber (And Not An Airbnb) For Hotels In India|url = http://www.forbes.com/sites/saritharai/2015/08/06/why-a-21-year-old-is-building-oyo-an-uber-and-not-an-airbnb-for-hotels-in-india/|accessdate = 2015-08-25|first = Saritha|last = Rai}} ==Business Model== OYO Rooms is an online aggregator of budget hotels. OYO Rooms partners with hotels with the aim of standardization on various measures in each room including free wifi and breakfast, flatscreen TVs, spotless white bed linen of a certain thread count, branded toiletries, 6-inch shower heads, a beverage tray and so on. The standards are audited every few days so that the customers are assured a quality experience. OYO’s budget stays range from 999 ($16) to 4,000 rupees ($66). OYO provides property owners with support such as quality standardized supplies and service training. OYO boasts of a 24x7 in-house customer service center. However recently OYO has changed its policy and allow you to spend only 25% of referral money when you book a hotel, the rest must be paid by the guest from his pocket, a complete "U-turn" from their initial model.{{Cite web|title = Why A 21-Year-Old Is Building OYO As An Uber (And Not An Airbnb) For Hotels In India|url = http://www.forbes.com/sites/saritharai/2015/08/06/why-a-21-year-old-is-building-oyo-an-uber-and-not-an-airbnb-for-hotels-in-india/|accessdate = 2015-08-25|first = Saritha|last = Rai}} ==Founder== 21-year-old, Ritesh Agarwal, is the founder & CEO of OYO Rooms. Being an avid traveler, he soon realized that the budget hospitality sector lacked predictability.
