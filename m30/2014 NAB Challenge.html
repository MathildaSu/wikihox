xml:space="preserve" bytes="7691"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=2014 NAB Challenge|timestamp=20140328073629|year=2014|month=March|day=28|substed=yes}} The '''2014 NAB Challenge''' is the [[Australian Football League]] (AFL) [[Australian Football League pre-season competition|pre-season competition]] that was played before the [[2014 AFL season|2014 home and away season]]. It featured 18 practice matches played over 18 days, beginning February 12 and ending March 1.{{Cite news|title=Eighteen games in 18 days|url=http://www.afl.com.au/news/2013-10-31/eighteen-games-in-18-days|accessdate=31 October 2013|publisher=AFL.com.au|date=31 October 2013}} Unlike previous years, the 2014 competition did not have a Grand Final or overall winner.{{cite web|last=Bilton|first=Dean|title=AFL preseason tournament signals the start of footy season with new rules and new
