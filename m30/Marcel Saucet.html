He obtained his PhD ES Management Science with highest honors in 2007. He completed his doctoral thesis on Marketing Innovation in the cosmetics industry. In his research, he invented the concept of micro innovation,Cova Bernard, Fuschillo Gregorio and marcel Paul Saucet, Attualità e prospettive dello Street Marketing, Micro & Macro Marketing, 2014, issue 1, pages 145-166 http://econpapers.repec.org/article/muljyf1hn/doi_3a10.1431_2f76503_3ay_3a2014_3ai_3a1_3ap_3a145-166.htm which applies [[chaos theory]] to marketing. During his studies, he held academic positions which included associative Vice President of the University of Nice from 2005 to 2007. At the end of the term he created the Alumni Association of the University in Nice and became the president in 2010 and 2011. == Professional career == Product Manager and Marketing Manager of a small business, Cosmetics applied from 2002 to 2006, he led the development of products Biosiris, Vitalithé, Bakea for France, Canada, Belgium, Brazil, Europe and Southwest Asia. Following his PhD, he decided to create his consulting and training laboratory in marketing and managerial innovation, LCA Conseil (Consultants Associates). Through the formation of LCA Consulting & Training division, Marcel Saucet provides initial training in many business schools in France and abroad. Associate professor and researcher at the University of San Diego at California since 2010, he was brought to speak at USC in Los Angeles, University of Miami, University of California at Berkeley, HEC and BIMTECH Institute in India.Marcel SAucet Website. http://marcel-saucet.fr/conseil-et-formation/cours-et-conferences/ In 2013, he launched his agency "Street Marketing Communication", of which he is custodian of the brand in Europe and North America.
