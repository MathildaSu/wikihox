In 2014, he began to gain major recognition following the release of his debut visual album, ''[[Maek Pandamonium Visual Album]]''. He also is the founder of [[Maek]]. ==Background== Jason was born on September 20, 1987, and grew up in the [[Chatham, Chicago|Chatham]] neighborhood on the South Side of [[Chicago]], [[Illinois]].
