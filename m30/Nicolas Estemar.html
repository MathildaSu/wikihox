The suspect was then moving towards a building complex where he was running around and testing doors.{{Cite news|title=Två personer dog efter knivdramat i Varberg|url=http://www.expressen.se/gt/tva-personer-dog-efter-knivdramat-i-varberg-1/|accessdate=3 July 2017 |newspaper=Expressen |date=7 July 2013 |deadurl=no}}{{Cite news|title=Pascal, 18, överlevde knivmannen i Varberg|url=http://www.expressen.se/gt/pascal-18-overlevde-knivmannen-i-varberg/|accessdate=3 July 2017 |newspaper=Expressen |date=27 July 2013 |deadurl=no}} First, he found a way in to a young man that was asleep in his bed and later an elderly lady whom he both stabbed. ==Shootout== After the attack Nicolas was confronted by police and was first shot in the leg, afterwhich he got up to his knees and started crawling towards the police; still equipped with a knife. The police then shoot him a second time; and this time it's lethal. The perpetrator dies later in hospital. ==Perpetrator== '''Nicolas Estemar''' (born 1989), known as the '''Zombie Killer''', was not known by police before the attack. He originated from the Stockholm area but was in Varberg as part of a boot camp.
