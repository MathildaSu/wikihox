Every year they conduct the Manhunt and Megamodel Contest, it is a unisex pageant in India. At the contest most appealing male and female models are selected as winner and runner ups and the magazine promotes them. The current winners of Gladrags Manhunt and Megamodel India are 26 years old Ravi Awana of [[Noida]], [[Uttar Pradesh]] and Charlotte Clarie of [[Mumbai]]. They first conduct a round of selection from the regions and then post all the elimination rounds for the final contestants. The group undergoes vigorous training including physical fitness, grooming, catwalk, diction, speech and so on. ==Gladrags Manhunt Contest== ===History=== The first ever Gladrags Manhunt Contest India was held in 1994, [[Rajat Bedi]] of [[Punjab, India|Punjab]] was the first ever winner of Gladrags Manhut Contest. He represented India at [[Manhunt International 1994]] held in [[Greece]] and was awarded 4th Runner Up in the finale. ===International Achievements=== In 2001, [[Rajeev Singh]] of [[Rajasthan]] became the first Asian and Indian to win [[Manhunt International]] contest. He also won two sub-awards at the contest including Sharmoon Best Dressed Gentleman Award and Best in National Costume. After his victory he established himself in the Indian fashion industry as a successful model. He is often touted as one best models that India has ever produced. India has produced many runner ups at the pageant most notably [[Dino Morea]], [[Zulfi Syed]] and [[John Abraham]] all of them have established themselves in the [[Bollywood]] industry afterwards. It has also given India some of the finest models ever seen by Indian fashion industry like supermodel [[Muzamil Ibrahim]], [[Actor]] and model [[Siddharth Shukla]], model [[Farhad shahnawaz]], actor and model Abhimanyu Jain, 4th runner up [[Manhunt International 2006]] and Arry Dabas, semifinalist in the year 2012. Other notable winners include Romeo Gates, Ahran Chaudhary and Ravi Awana. In 2005, [[Siddharth Shukla]], winner of Gladrags Manhunt 2004 represented India at Best Model Of The World contest held in [[Turkey]] and became the first Indian to win the title.
