xml:space="preserve" bytes="7965"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=AXM Futsal Manado|timestamp=20170731170055|year=2017|month=July|day=31|substed=yes|help=off}} '''AXM Futsal Manado''', also known as AXM, is a local Futsal Team in [[Manado]], [[Indonesia]]. Founded in April 2015 by Alventura B. Pangemanan with the original name of Alveyro FC, the name was taken from the name of the founder's son, Alveyro Xaverius Maldini Pangemanan. The founder decide to change the name from Alveyro FC into AXM Futsal Manado because of the poor performance in some local tournaments, AXM won their first local tournament{{Cite web|url=http://www.suluttoday.com/2015/07/09/axm-team-jawara-alveyro-1st-anniversary-open-tournament-futsal/|title=AXM Team Jawara ALVEYRO 1st Anniversary Open Tournament Futsal {{!}} Suluttoday.com|access-date=2017-07-31}} in the early July and announced their official team name and logo in July 11, 2015{{Cite web|url=http://www.suluttoday.com/2015/07/14/axm-football-club-launching-skuat/|title=AXM Football Club Launching Skuat {{!}} Suluttoday.com|access-date=2017-07-31}}. AXM Jersey Manufacturer is Elastico7, an Indonesia Football Apparel. Under the management of Alventura B. Pangemanan, Fhey Lumunon and Calvin Panginda, AXM successfully hosted a Coaching Clinic with Bonsu Hasibuan{{Cite web|url=http://www.vamos-academy.com/|title=About Vamos - Vamos Futsal Academy|website=Vamos Futsal Academy|language=en-US|access-date=2017-07-31}}, an AFC Licensed and [[Vamos Mataram]] Coach, and Andriansyah{{Cite news|url=https://bolalob.com/read/36046/andriansyah-adom-terima-kasih-banyak-vamos-mataram-dan-semeton|title=Andriansyah Adom: Terima Kasih Banyak Vamos Mataram Dan Semeton|last=Anggoro|first=Dwi|work=Bolalob - Situsnya Anak Futsal!|access-date=2017-07-31|language=id-ID}}, an [[Indonesia national futsal team|Indonesia National Futsal Team]] Player and Ex-[[Vamos Mataram]] Captain, as the main speaker and trainer that was held on GOR Politeknik Manado in the early of June 2017. ==History== === Early Years === AXM Futsal Manado were founded in April 2015 by Alventura B. Pangemanan as Alveyro FC, but renamed to AXM Futsal Manado 3 Months Later. The team first tournament was AUS-Finnest Cup and reaching the 3rd Place. After 3 Months without winning any tournaments some players went out from the team and joining some other rival teams. With the team captain, James Giroth, and some players that stayed, AXM won their first local tournament after beating their rival 3-0 on the Group Stage in July 2015. Aldair Makatindu, a [[Pusamania Borneo]] player, was on a holiday before Jend. Sudirman Cup{{Cite news|url=http://bidhuan.id/piala-jenderal-sudirman/31034/ini-kekuatan-baru-skuad-pusamania-borneo-fc-di-piala-jenderal-sudirman/|title=Ini Kekuatan Baru Skuad Pusamania Borneo FC di Piala Jenderal Sudirman|date=2015-11-04|work=Berbagi Cerita, Opini dan Berita Farmasi|access-date=2017-07-31|language=en-US}} also took part on the winning team in July 2015. === Birth of a New Generation === New Players like Risto Taduminggir and Andre Manuty brought AXM some trophies including Pa'dior Cup with Risto Taduminggir scored a last minute goal against El Ragnarok and AXM won on a penalty with Maryo Luhukay, AXM's Goalkeeper, played an outstanding performance. In late 2015, AXM sent some players and the team coach, Ronny Soputan, to be joining North Sulawesi Futsal Team for the Pre-PON(Pekan Olahraga Nasional){{Cite web|url=http://manado.tribunnews.com/2015/10/30/tim-futsal-sulut-lolos-ke-16-besar|title=Tim Futsal Sulut Lolos ke 16 Besar|website=Tribun Manado|access-date=2017-07-31}} and the [[Sam Ratulangi University]] POMNAS(Pekan Olahraga Mahasiswa Nasional){{Cite web|url=http://www.suluttoday.com/2015/11/12/tim-futsal-unsrat-tampil-di-pomnas-aceh/|title=Tim Futsal Unsrat Tampil di POMNAS Aceh {{!}} Suluttoday.com|access-date=2017-07-31}} Team that also includes Risto Taduminggir and Andre Manuty, making AXM has one of the most player sent by a local North Sulawesi team to a National Competition. === AXM Jr. === Acting like AXM's second team made new and less experience players played and fight for their place in the main team, and players such like Risto and Andre were once played for the AXM Jr. and gained promotion to the AXM Main Team. In some local tournaments AXM are allowed to register two teams in one tournament, and AXM Jr. once played against AXM in a local tournament but eventually lost to their main team with a breathtaking score of 9-3. In Sam Ratulangi University Tournaments, AXM annd AXM Jr. met in the finals and get the 1st and 2nd Rank.
