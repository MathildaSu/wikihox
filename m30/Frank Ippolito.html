'''Frank Ippolito''' is a makeup artist, special-effects artist, and director based in [[Burbank, California]]. He has appeared on two seasons of the SyFy competition ''[[Face Off (TV series)|Face Off]]''. Ippolito is known for his work helping fellow special-effects artist [[Adam Savage]] create his [[Admiral Ackbar]] costume for the 2013 [[San Diego Comic-Con International]].{{cite web | url = http://www.tested.com/art/makers/456811-making-adam-savages-admiral-ackbar-costume/ | title = The Making of Adam Savage's Admiral Ackbar Costume | date= July 19, 2013 | first=Adam | last= Savage | authorlink= Adam Savage | publisher= [[Whiskey Media#Tested|Tested.com]] | accessdate= October 13, 2013}} ==Face Off== During the first season of ''Face Off'', Ippolito was the third contestant eliminated.
