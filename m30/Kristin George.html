[[Miss USA 2006]] | birth_place= [[Casper, Wyoming]], [[United States|U.S.]] | birth_name= Kristin Ann George | birth_date={{Birth year and age|1982}} | height={{height|ft=5|in=6}} | eye_color= Blue | hair_color= Blonde | nationality= American | ethnicity= White/Caucasian | alias= }}'''Kristin Ann George''' (born 1982) is a beauty queen from [[Casper, Wyoming]] who has competed in the [[Miss Teen USA]] and [[Miss USA]] pageants. George won the [[Miss Wyoming USA]] 2006 title in a state pageant held in September 2005. She competed for the title of [[Miss USA 2006]] in [[Baltimore, MD]] on April 21, 2006 but failed to place in the top 15. The pageant was won by [[Tara Conner]] of [[Miss Kentucky USA|Kentucky]] George has been involved in a number of other major pageants. She previously the title [[Miss Wyoming Teen USA]] 2000 and competed at [[Miss Teen USA]] 2000, which was won by [[Jillian Parry]] of [[Miss Pennsylvania Teen USA|Pennsylvania]]. In both 2004 and 2005 George placed 1st runner-up to the title of [[Miss Wyoming USA]]. George graduated with honours from Kelly Walsh High School in 2000. She went on to attend the [[University of Wyoming]] where she completed associates degrees Psychology and being a Paralegal. She also earned a [[Bachelor's degree]] in Psychology and Social Science. Her academic awards include Legal Assistant of the Year and making the President's List at her University. She is currently a Professional Healthcare Representative for [[Pfizer Pharmaceuticals]].
