He currently lives in [[Birmingham]], [[England]].[http://www.rankinghero.com/en/players/207172/Peter.Evans/about.html#block_information Full list of LateNight performances] ==Poker career== A player highly valued in the poker community of England. He is constantly playing both in small and big events, mostly taking place in different UK venues. He is also sometimes playing in the United States. His biggest cash is £21,000, coming from a festival, that took place at G Casino Walsall. At the Euro Finals of Poker 10th Edition, held in Paris, he was playing at a €3000 EFOP Diamond Championship Event - No Limit Hold'em. It was held from Saturday 9th - Monday 11 December. He was the only British person to make it to the final table and finished in second place winning €79,940 (£50,288). It was won by Frechman Jérome Zerbib who won just over €140,000 (approx. £90,000). He was the only non-French person who finished in the top ten. His latest cashes comes from a tournament, held in the United States, he finished ITM for a small cash.
