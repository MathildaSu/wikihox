xml:space="preserve" bytes="1534"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Crazy Eyes Crew|timestamp=20160411142534|year=2016|month=April|day=11|substed=yes|help=off}} '''Crazy Eyes Dance Crew (C.E.C)[Meaning: youth, temper, dancing ]''' is an Azerbaijani Hip-Hop dance crew, founded by Arif Mammadov in 11 December 2012, best known for being the winners of all dance competitions in Azerbaijan in 2013. The Crazy Eyes Crew do not have a group leader; choreography for their performances, as well as music and design choices, are made as a collective unit. Members: Emin "Evan" Aghayev, Javid Mammadov, Malikmammad "Wild Man" Abdullayev, Ildirim "B-Boy Makvin" Mirmammadov, Murad "Loss" Dadashov, Tamras Abasov, Babek "MadBeaT" Gulubeyli. First competition C.E.C take part in was the Baku championship, organized by the Association of social and modern dance and Youth Union (less than three months after the formation of the group). In February 2013 Crazy Eyes Crew won the title among the 4 teams in Baku. April 21, C.E.C got Republic Title. The Crazy Eyes Crew take part in the competition "Baki Geceleri". December 24 Crazy Eyes group has performed at the Palace of Heydar Aliyev. Later it received a proposal from the singer and composer Rahim Rahimli. [[File:Cecrew.jpg|thumb]]
