International Young Audience Film Festival]]''' was held from 29 November to 6 December 2015. A number of 157 movies, released between the years of 1965 and 2015 took part.[http://2015.alekino.com/filmy/ List of movies] at the Official Homepage The movies were presented in Multikino 51 cinema and [[Imperial Castle, Poznań|Imperial Castle Cinema]].
