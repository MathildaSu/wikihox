The organization was sparked by a 13-year-old's idea of bringing some extra love to [[Senior citizen|senior citizens]], whom he claimed are "an amazing group of people so often forgotten in their final and most precious stage of life," {{Citation|last=TEDx Talks|title=14 Year Old Starts Movement to Touch the Lives of the Elderly {{!}} Jacob Cramer {{!}} TEDxYouth@Cincinnati|date=2015-09-08|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayOmOaXC0Z0|accessdate=2016-08-20}} Love for the Elderly has since transformed into a global organization that has a wide array of programs and initiatives.
