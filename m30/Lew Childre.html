He works on child development and strategies for dealing with stress. == Early years == After serving in the [[National Guard of the United States|National Guard]], Childre started a recording studio. In his early twenties, he developed some health problems that caused him to look for alternative treatments and that is when he began researching [[stress (psychological)|stress]]. He also did a lot of reading and [[meditation|meditating]], and came to realize that ultimately the answers he was looking for were in his own heart. He then began to research "heart intelligence".
