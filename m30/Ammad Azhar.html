xml:space="preserve" bytes="2796"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Ammad Azhar|timestamp=20150811222938|year=2015|month=August|day=11|substed=yes|help=off}} {{Infobox person | image = [[File:Ammad Azhar.jpg|thumb|Screenwriter/Director]] | birth_date = {{birth date and age|1977|2|23|df=y}} | birth_place = [[Depalpur]], [[Punjab]], Pakistan | residence = [[Islamabad]], Pakistan | spouse = [[Shafaq Kureshi]] | children = [[Aalyane Azhar]], [[Zuryane Azhar]], [[Arhaam Azhar]] | yearsactive = 1997-present | website = {{URL|www.meditationproductions.com}} | occupation = Film director, Screenwriter }} '''Ammad Azhar''' ({{lang-ur|{{Nastaliq|عماداظہر}}}} ) is a film director and screenwriter working under the umbrella of [[Meditation Production]]. == Early life == Ammad was born on 23rd Feb 1977 in [[Depalpur]], [[Punjab]]. He completed his bachelors from [[University of the Punjab]], [[Lahore]] and did his Masters in Urdu Literature from [[Bahauddin Zakariya University]]. He was a student of Spiritual Sciences and has been teaching Meta-physics from a very young age; as a result he was invited onto Talk shows as a guest speaker. He has written Urdu poetry and columns and his first book came out in the year 2002 by the title "Moujood" [presence], its prolusion was written by [[Ashfaq Ahmed]]. [[File:Moujood.png|thumb|Moujood - published in year 2002]][[File:Moujood2.png|thumb|prolusion written by Ashfaq Ahmed]] == Career == Initially Ammad directed dramas like [[Sanwari]], [[The Last Picture]], [[Kaitli]] etc. and wrote plays including [[Yazeed Hazir ho]] and [[Nabz]]. He got his diploma and certification in screenwriting from [[The New Zealand Film & Television School]]. He has conducted trainings & workshop on the "Art of documentary & filming" for the National Channel and is a mentor at the PTV Academy. == Personal life == Ammad's family includes his wife and 3 sons. He is the eldest among his siblings. == Film work == Ammad aims to make films that involve human psychology and are subjective in nature. His current projects include a film on the life of the [[Prophet Muhammad]] at Makkah called [[Ya-Sabaha]] and a mystery/thriller, [[Route#8]].
