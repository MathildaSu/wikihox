xml:space="preserve" bytes="1190"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Cumulative DVH|timestamp=20150621054815|year=2015|month=June|day=21|substed=yes}} {{Underlinked|date=May 2015}} '''Cumulative DVH''' (or integral) is the volume graph from a given structure which receives at least a certain amount of [[radiation dose]]. It is calculated as the integral of the differential [[Dose-volume histogram]] (DVH) so it always begin at 100% (100% of the volume receives at least 0 dose) and ends at the maximum dose.{{cite web|last1=Sambasivam|first1=Sasikumar|title=Dose Volumes Histogram|url=http://www.slideshare.net/sasikumars/dose-volume-histogram|website=slideshare|format=slides}} It is built calculating and plotting (on y-axis) the [[cumulative frequency]] corresponedent to the dose-bin on x-axis. ==See also== * [[Differential DVH]] ==References== {{Reflist}} [[Category:Radiation therapy]] [[Category:Medical physics]]
