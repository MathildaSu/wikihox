({{lang-rus|Обама - чмо}}, a possible English translation: "Obama is [[Schmuck (pejorative)|schmuck]]"[http://www.thetimes.co.uk/tto/news/world/europe/article4488623.ece Russia’s young and poor crave war with the West] or ''Obama is a [[Amorality|amoral]] person''[http://mockvanews.ru/?p=2485 Обама ЧМО – человек без морали]) is an [[Russian language|Russian]]-language slogan used in [[Russia]] to mock USA President [[Barack Obama]]. Growing as answer to Ukrainian slogan "[[Putin khuilo!]]". It is usually used as a [[sticker]]s for [[car]]s[http://tvrain.ru/teleshow/lobkov_vechernee_shou/mozhet_hot_tina_kandelaki_nadenet_shtany_na_patriotov_obama_chmo_i_drugoj_adskij_kreativ-381546/ Может, хоть Тина Канделаки наденет штаны на патриотов. «Обама — чмо» и другой адский креатив ][http://naklejka.ru/car_stickers/sanksii-obama/obama-chmo/ Обама - чмо] [[File:Barack Obama California visit.jpg|thumb|left|[[Barack Obama]]]] ==Etymology== The ''[[Mat (Russian profanity)|mat]]'' (Russian [[vulgarity]]) [[wikt:чмо|чмо]] is variously transliterated as ''tshmo, tsmo'' (t͡ɕmo). The origin is unclear. Some find the word "chmo" to be of [[Yiddish]] origin[http://languagehat.com/chmo/ CHMO] ({{lang-yi|שמאָק}}, [[penis]]). There is also a number of [[folk etymology|folk etymologies]], such as ChMO - '''ch'''elovek ({{lang-rus|человек}}, man) '''m'''oralno ({{lang-rus|морально}}, morally) opushchenny ({{lang-rus|опущенный}} put dowm through the oral homosexual sex).
