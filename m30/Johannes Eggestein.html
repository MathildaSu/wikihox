Joahnnes Eggestein managed to make huge impact in the U17 League with Havelse at the age of 14, scoring 28 goals in 19 games. It was a huge achievement considered he was 2 or 3 years younger than most opponents. Johannes Eggestein was described as a "Jahrhunderttalent" (once in a century talent) by professional scout from Bundesliga. He then attracted interests from clubs like Bayern Munich, Dortmund, Schalke, Leverkusen, Werder Bremen or even Liverpool and Real Madrid from overseas. He chose Werder Bremen at the end because his brother, [[Maximilian Eggestein]], was also in the team. Johannes Eggestein managed to finish as the B-Jugend Bundesliga's top scorer in the first two seasons since he has joined Werder Bremen.
