She was born on 27/09/1979 in an [[adivasi|tribal]] family, in Garjana Palli Village, Ellareddy Mandal, Karimnagar Dist, Telangana state. Chandrakala secured the 409th rank in the Civil Services exams whose results were announced by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) in 2008. A mother of 9 year old girl, her husband A Ramulu works as deputy executive engineer in Sriramsagar Project (SRSP) at Lower Manair Dam (LMD). She joined Koti Women's College in Hyderabad to study BA but was married off in the second year. Her high aspirations prevented her from giving up studies and she completed MA (Economics) through distance education and prepared for Group I with the help of her husband who took care of their child.She has been the student of esteemed TS Study circle {{cite news|title='studied Story|url=http://www.fapcci.in/APSC_1/Download/1980%20with%20names.pdf}} {{cite news|title='Success Story|url=http://iasexamportal.com/civilservices/Success-Story/Tribeswoman-scripts-story-of-success|date=18 December 2014}} {{cite news|title='IAS Officer Biography|url=http://news.desigoogly.com/blog/2014/09/02/ms-b-chandrakala-ias-biography/|date=18 December 2014}} She was posted as CDO Allahabad and in April 2012 shifted as DM Hamirpur.{{cite news|title='70 IAS officers transferred in Uttar Pradesh|url=http://www.rediff.com/news/report/seventy-ias-officers-transferred-in-uttar-pradesh/20120413.htm|date=18 December 2014}} After this, her post was again shuffled and she was made the Mathura DM on 8 June 2014.{{cite news|title='Major IAS reshuffle in UP|url=http://www.igovernment.in/news/40196/major-ias-reshuffle|date=18 December 2014}} She was only the second female District Magistrate of Mathura, after Anita Meshram.
