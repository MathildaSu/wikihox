xml:space="preserve" bytes="1630"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Gliese 809|timestamp=20150627163656|year=2015|month=June|day=27|substed=yes|help=off}} {{starbox short |constell= [[Cepheus (constellation)|Cepheus]] |name= Gliese 809 |type= M2.0V |ra= {{RA|20|53|19.79051}} |dec= {{DEC|+62|09|15.8028}} }} '''Gliese 809''' is a [[red dwarf]] in the constellation [[Cepheus (constellation)|Cepheus]]. It is part of the [[Gliese Catalogue of Nearby Stars]] and is only 23 light years away from Earth. Its [[apparent magnitude|visual magnitude]] is 8.55. Gliese 809 is the primary component of a multi-star system. Gliese 809 has a metallicity is -0.11 (77.6% of the Sun). It is going at a velocity of 31.1 km/s relative to the Solar System. Its galactic orbit is 21,300 Ly at its perigee and 30,600 ly at the apogee in its orbit of the center of the galaxy.
