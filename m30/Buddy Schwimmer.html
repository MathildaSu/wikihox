The studio, which holds both private lessons and classes with from 5 to 25 students, resumed full operations in September 2006.Garcia, Catherine (2006-09-07). 5-6-7-8 reopens, Benji considers offers. Buddy appeared in the 13th season to help Lacey's partner [[Chaz Bono]]. Buddy also taught [[Jared Murillo (dancer)|Jared Murillo]], who has won national titles with Lacey Schwimmer.
