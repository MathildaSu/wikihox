''SOS ma famille a besoin d'aide'' ([[NRJ 12]]) |spouse = |partner = |children = |website = }} '''Pascal Soetens''' (born October 18, 1969), better known as '''Pascal le Grand Frère''', is a [[France|French]] [[sports instructor]] and [[television personality]]. == Biography == Pascal Soetens was born in [[Paris]], but grew up in [[Creil]], a town located in the department of [[Oise (département)|Oise]]. He is married and has two children, born in 1997 and 1998 respectively. == Early career == After a number of experiences in several municipalities and federations, he joined the town hall of [[Senlis]] located in the same department, where he was in charge of the coordination of the youth structure of the town (entertainment centers, clubs, etc.) as a member of the youth service. == Television career == He began his television career in 2006 in the reality and education program ''Pascal, le grand frère'' broadcast on [[TF1]] from January 2006 to June 2012, where he helps teenagers with difficult behaviors and solve the problems with their family. The title of this program later became his nickname. He then featured in ''Opération Tambacounda'', a similar program where he goes with other teenagers in another country, where they have the opportunity to live again in community. Between December 2012 and February 2013, he is a sports coach in the [[Star Academy 9 (France)|ninth season of ''Star Academy'']] broadcast on [[NRJ 12]].
