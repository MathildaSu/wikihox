xml:space="preserve" bytes="1524"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Beat Drop|timestamp=20140815034831|year=2014|month=August|day=15|substed=yes}} '''Beat Drop''' is a music production & DJing school located in the [[Beltline, Calgary|beltline]] area of [[Calgary]], [[Alberta]], [[Canada]]. It is currently the only [[Ableton]] Certified Training Centre in Canada.{{cite web|title=Ableton Certified|url=https://www.ableton.com/en/education/certified-training/canada/|website=Ableton.com}} Beat Drop offers more than a dozen courses and also hosts a variety of community events & workshops during it's semesters and throughout the year. It also hosts teen summer camps each July. The faculty is comprised of professional musicians & DJs who make their living in the music business, in and around the Calgary area. ==History== Founded in 2011 by Ableton Certified Trainer Mitch Lee, the school was originally housed inside rental space at [[SAIT Polytechnic|SAIT]] untill September 2013 when the facility relocated to it's current location at 1120 17th Ave SW. ==Instructors== DJ C-Sik Sandro Petrillo Mitch Lee Brandon Smith Chris Maric Timothy Allan DJ Reece Miche Stirling Scott Fuhrmann ==References== {{reflist}} [[Category:Music schools in Canada]]
