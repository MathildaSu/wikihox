xml:space="preserve" bytes="2225"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Lima Publishing|timestamp=20140128002117|year=2014|month=January|day=28|substed=yes|help=off}} {{Orphan|date=February 2009}} '''Lima Publishing''' Ltd. is a UK-based commercial and contract publishers which produces the bi-monthly ICT for Education magazine.1 Specialising in multimedia production and consultancy, the business was founded in January 2007 by Directors: Joseph Devo, Ian Loosemore and Andrew Metcalfe,2 all of whom had previously worked together on various publications for [[VNU]]. Its flagship title, ICT for Education,3 was purchased from the previous owners, Crimson Publishing, in early 2007. It has a current total circulation of 12,000 individuals predominantly school ICT coordinators and senior teachers and reaching one of every two UK schools.4 On July 3, 2008, Lima Publishing hosted the first annual ICT for Education Conference entitled ‘Improving Today, Excelling Tomorrow’ at the Birmingham Metropole Hotel.5 The event was launched by Ultralab Founder, Professor Stephen Heppell.
