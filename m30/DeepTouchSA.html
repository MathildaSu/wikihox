He began his career around 2013 and has released two singles{{cite web |url=http://itunes.apple.com/za/album/movements-deep-single-afro/id1032987569 |work=ITunes |title=Movements of Deep - Single by Deep Touch |accessdate=September 28, 2016}}{{cite web |url=http://itunes.apple.com/za/album/forbidden-note-single/id1095681459 |work=ITunes |title=Forbidden Note - Single by Deep Touch |accessdate=September 28, 2016}}under his [[Polokwane]]-Based [[record label]], Deep Elements Music.{{cite web |url=https://deeptouchsa.wordpress.com/ |work=WordPress |title=About Deep Elements Music Records |accessdate=September 28, 2016}} == Early life and career == Simbarashe Musingarimi originates from [[Bulawayo, Zimbabwe]]. He started producing music at an early age of fourteen, having been influenced by his uncle who was a music producer at the time.{{cite web |url=http://www.deeptouchdj.com/LATEST_NEWS |work=deeptouchdj.com |title=10 Things You Didn’t Know About Deep Touch |accessdate=September 28, 2016}} He eventually learnt and [[Mastered]] the process of [[music production]] and began adding [[soulful]] sounds and his own synthesizer [[plugins]], later he had decided to start his own record label. The name ''Deep Touch'' was given to him by the first music producer he ever worked with. He looks up to fellow [[South African]] Music Producers{{cite web |url=http://www.discogs.com/label/6323-House-Afrika-Records |work=HouseAfrikaRecords |title=Deep Touch signs to House Afrika Records |accessdate=September 29, 2016}}[[Black Coffee]], DukeSoul and Vinny Da Vinci, and stated that he was motivated by their work [[ethic]].{{cite web |url=https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=2lLqeoZXxpU |work=Local Afro Deep Charts |title=Afro Deep House Chart |accessdate=September 28, 2016}} Deep Touch released his first [[solo album]] titled "More than memories - EP"{{cite web |url=https://twitter.com/DeepTouchSA/status/728604383546593282 |title=Deep Touch announces album release |accessdate=September 28, 2016}}which was released on 28 April 2015.
