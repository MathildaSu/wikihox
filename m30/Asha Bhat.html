(Most Beautiful Smile) | Website: }} '''Asha Bhat''' (born 1992) is an [[India|Indian]] [[Model (person)|Model]] and beauty pageant winner who was crowned Miss Supranational 2014 at [[Krynica-Zdrój]], [[Poland]] on December 5th, 2014. She is the first Asian to get crowned by another Asian winner in the history of the pageant. ==Personal life== Asha is a model in [[India]]. Previously she was awarded as NCC-Cadet ambassador of India at SAARC in 2009. ==Education== Asha did her schooling from St. Charles School, Bhadravathi and [http://primeacademypune.com/ Prime Academy Pune], a leading IIT JEE coaching institute in Pune. She also studied in Alvas pre university college, moodbidri and currently pursuing B.E from R.V College of Engineering, Bangalore ==Pageantry == ===Miss Diva 2014=== Asha represented [[Karnataka]] at [[Miss Diva - 2014|Miss Diva 2014]] and was crowned Miss Diva Supranational at the pageant. She won three sub-awards at the pageant including Miss Congeniality, Miss Fascinating and Miss Beautiful Smile.{{cite web|url=http://beautypageants.indiatimes.com/missdiva.cms|title=Noyonita Lodh won the Miss Diva-Universe 2014 |publisher=beautypageants.indiatimes.com|date=2014}} ===Miss Supranational 2014=== Asha was crowned as Miss Supranational 2014 at Hall of Sport,[[Krynica-Zdrój]], [[Poland]] on December 5th, 2014. 70 contestants from around the world competed at the pageant.
