His music has been released by a variety of different [[record label]]s, of which the most notable are Deep Medi Musik, Boka Records & WX/WL white label series. ==Career== Compa's first major release was his 2012 single on Boka Records,{{cite web|url=http://bokarecords.co.uk/?tag=boka038pt2|title=Boka Records|publisher=Bokarecords.co.uk|accessdate=28 November 2014}} followed by a well-received remix of Mavado's 'Dem A Talk' track, which was noted to have sold out worldwide in its first week on-sale{{cite web|url=http://www.discogs.com/Compa-vs-Mavado-Dem-A-Talk/release/3361500|title=Compa vs. Mavado – Dem A Talk|publisher=Discogs.com|accessdate=28 November 2014}} which was released by WX/WL white label series. In 2012, he signed to Mala (Digital Mystikz)'s Deep Medi Musik label, but he did not release his debut single{{cite web|url=http://trusik.tumblr.com/post/68564134925/review-compa-narabeh-alpha-medi075|title=TRUSIK - Underground Bass Music|work=TRUSIK - Underground Bass Music|accessdate=28 November 2014}} for the label until December 2013. Compa has played DJ sets at notable worldwide clubs and events such as Fabric, London, where he played every room of the club in 2013, TBA Brooklyn in New York, NY, Cervantes' Masterpiece Ballroom in Denver, CO, Solar Beach in Istanbul, Turkey, The Warehouse Project in Manchester, UK, Seminal Dubstep club night FWD in London, UK and Outlook Festival{{cite web|url=http://www.outlookfestival.com/compa/|title=Compa|publisher=Outlookfestival.com|accessdate=28 November 2014}} in Pula, Croatia. ==Discography== ===EPs and singles=== *''Sentence''/''Beginning'' (BOKA Records, 2012) *''Afraid'' (Redshift One, 2012) *''Security''/''Cold Weather'' (Area Recordings, 2012) *''Kalindi''/''Antact'' (Kokeshi, 2013) *''Narabeh''/''Alpha'' (DEEP MEDi Musik, 2013) *''Outer Lines''/''Timelapse'' (TUBA Records, 2014) *''Let Them''/''Earths Orbit'' (BOKA Records, 2014) ===Remixes=== *''Dem A Talk'' (WX/WL, 2012) *''Fear'' (WX/WL, 2012) *''Porta -
