Katie Vahle | launch_date = {{start date and age|2011}} | alexa = | current_status = Active }} '''CoPatient''' is a [[Medical-bill advocacy|medical bill review]] company established in 2011 and based in [[Boston]], [[Massachusetts]] in the United States.
