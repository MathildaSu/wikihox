xml:space="preserve" bytes="6227"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Rape jihad|timestamp=20150224045716|year=2015|month=February|day=24|substed=yes}} '''Rape jihad''' is a neologism which refers to the abduction, gang rape and enslavement of non-Muslim women by jihadists. The sexual abuse carried out to spread terror with the intention of controlling the government or the society.{{cite web|url=http://www.issafrica.org/iss-today/sexual-terrorism-in-africa-a-case-of-two-crimes-in-one|title=Sexual terrorism in Africa: a case of two crimes in one|last=Salifu|first=Uyo|date=2 October 2013|publisher=Institute for Security Studies|accessdate=16 December 2014}} [[Robert Spencer (author)|Robert Spencer]] used the term as early as 2004 to describe the Beslan massacre, where many captive women and children were subjected to gang rape by the hostage takers and the genocidal rape during the war in Darfur.{{cite web|url=http://archive.frontpagemag.com/readArticle.aspx?ARTID=11278|title=The Rape Jihad|last=Spencer|first=Robert|date=24 September 2004|publisher=FrontPageMagazine.com|accessdate=13 December 2014}} In recent years sex grooming cases in UK have been referred to as rape jihad.{{cite web|url=http://www.gatestoneinstitute.org/3846/britain-child-grooming|title=Britain: "Rape Jihad" Against Children|last=Kern|first=Soeren|date=11 July 2013|publisher=Gatestone Institute|accessdate=13 December 2014}} The captivity and rape of Yazidi, Turkoman and Christian women by ISIS has been termed as rape jihad.{{cite web|url=http://www.frontpagemag.com/2014/dgreenfield/rape-jihad-inside-isis-harem-for-captured-non-muslim-women/|title=Rape Jihad: Inside ISISâ€™ Harem for Captured Non-Muslim Women|last=Greenfield|first=Daniel|date=28 August 2014|publisher=FrontPageMag.com|accessdate=16 December 2014}} Rape jihad has been reported from West Bengal in India, where the perpetrators circulated a rape video that went viral.{{cite news|url=http://www.afternoonvoice.com/after-love-jihad-now-rape-jihad.html|title=After love jihad, now rape jihad|date=17 October 2014|work=Afternoon Voice|accessdate=16 December 2014}} == Origins == According to Andrew McCarthy, rape jihad is a form of sexual slavery which is sanctioned by the scripture. The Quranic verse 4:24 says that sex is permitted with [[Ma malakat aymanukum]] which refers to captive women.{{cite web|url=http://www.nationalreview.com/corner/391814/isis-and-rape-jihad-andrew-c-mccarthy|title=ISIS and the Rape Jihad|last=McCarthy|first=Andrew|date=3 November 2014|publisher=National Review Online|accessdate=16 December 2014}} === Rape in Darfur === {{Main|Rape during the Darfur genocide}} In Sudan, the war in Darfur has been commonly termed as "jihad".{{cite journal|last=Ulph|first=Stephen|date=19 August 2004|title=Jihadist Overtones In Sudan|journal=Terrorism Focus|publisher=The Jamestown Foundation|location=Washington|volume=1|issue=2|url=http://www.jamestown.org/programs/tm/single/?tx_ttnews%5Btt_news%5D=26758&tx_ttnews%5BbackPid%5D=237&no_cache=1#.VJMaMCuUfpd|accessdate=18 December 2014}} In a manner similar to the earlier conflict in present day [[South Sudan]], the Sudanese establishment publicly declared its jihadist objective. President Omar Al Bashir praised the Janjaweed with the words "Long live the Mujahideen". When the UN Security Council Resolution called for disarming the Janjaweed, he went on to declare jihad in Darfur.{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/africa/3527310.stm|title=Sudan army's anger over UN 'war'|date=2 August 2004|publisher=BBC|accessdate=18 December 2014}} During the war in Darfur, the Janjaweed resorted to [[genocidal rape]] on the women and children, the government forces including the paramilitary groups were involved in mass rapes and the military airlifted women to [[Khartoum]] to serve as sex slaves. === Beslan massacre === {{Main|Beslan massacre}} In September 2004, the Chechen jihadist group [[Riyad-us Saliheen Brigade of Martyrs|Riyadus Saliheen]] took pupils and guardians hostage inside their school the southern Russian town of Beslan. The jihadists murdered 385 people mostly teenage pupils of the school. Survivors recounted that during the hostage crisis, the jihadists raped 15 year old girls.{{cite news|url=http://www.mirror.co.uk/news/world-news/raped-forced-to-eat-flowers--1601732|title=RAPED, FORCED TO EAT FLOWERS & DRINK URINE|date=5 September 2004|work=Mirror|accessdate=17 December 2014}} === Grooming cases in UK === Rape jihad has been reported in UK since 2006, where teenage white girls have been drugged, pimped, gang raped, enslaved and forced into prostitution by men of Pakistani origin.{{cite web|url=http://www.udayindia.in/english/content_27sept2014/cover-story.html|title=MYSTERY OF LOVE JIHAD|last=Dutt|first=Vijay|date=27 September 2014|publisher=Uday India|accessdate=14 December 2014}} According to the International Centre for the Study of Radicalisation, the growing concern in UK over rape jihad has been exploited by the English Defence League to stir up anti-Muslim sentiments.{{cite news|url=http://www.theguardian.com/uk/2013/mar/13/english-defence-league-sex-grooming|title=English Defence League exploiting sex-grooming fears, says report|last=Smith|first=Mark|date=13 March 2013|work=The Guardian|accessdate=16 December 2014}}{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-22522232|title=Uncomfortable truths of child exploitation in Britain|last=Easton|first=Mark|date=14 May 2013|work=BBC.com|publisher=BBC|accessdate=16 December 2014}} == References == {{Reflist}} [[Category:Neologisms]] [[Category:Violence against women]]
