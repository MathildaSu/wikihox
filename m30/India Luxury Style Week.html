xml:space="preserve" bytes="3493"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=India Luxury Style Week|timestamp=20150611095755|year=2015|month=June|day=11|substed=yes|help=off}} [[File:ILSW - India Luxury Style Week.png|thumb|Men's Fashion week]] '''India Luxury Style Week''' (ILSW) is an annual fashion event, which will debut in August, 2015 in Bangalore. Catering primarily to men who have been long avoided on the Fashion scene, (ILSW) will fill the need for a pure Men’s- only [[Fashion week|Fashion Week]]. ==Activities== It is positioned to be a premium fashion and entertainment event. Conceptualized and helmed by ace designer, Mr. [[Ramesh Dembla]]. [[Akshay Kumar]] lends his super stardom and style in the capacity of the [[brand ambassador]], to be the face of India Luxury Style week. The event will take place from 12th -16 August 2015 at Movenpick, [[Bangalore]]. The event will witness talents and works of renowned fashion designers like Manoviraj Khosla, Vivek Karunakaran, Samant Chauhan, Sagar Tenali, Debarun, Aslam Khan, Rocky S, Ken Ferns, Rohit Kamra and [[Ramesh Dembla]] and many more, along with GenY and emerging designers. ==ILSW Launch Event== A grand launch was organized on 28 April 2015 at La Ruche, Mumbai. It saw mainstream Media covering the event and taking quick bytes from Fashionista personalities from across the entertainment industry. The charismatic [[Madhurima Tuli]], hosted the event and kept the audiences engaged before the showstopper act by superstar, [[Akshay Kumar]]. Mr. [[Ramesh Dembla]] joined him on stage and addressed questions of the media, pertaining to of Men’s-Only [[Fashion week|Fashion Week]], India Luxury Style Week. ==Future Plans== With [[Akshay Kumar]] as the face of the fashion week, India Luxury Style Week 2015 aims at propelling the Fashion Industry to greater heights by giving opportunities to new and existing international as well as national designers. Each day of the exciting fashion week will be characterized with fashion shows, events and parties never seen before.
