Benson''' (born April 8, 1990), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Blac Youngsta''', is an American [[hip hop music|hip hop]] [[recording artist]]. He is signed to [[Yo Gotti]]'s [[Yo Gotti#Collective Music Group|CMG]] record label and [[Epic Records]].{{cite news|url=http://www.hotnewhiphop.com/yo-gotti-signs-blac-youngsta-to-cmg-new-video.35322.html|title=Yo Gotti Signs Blac Youngsta To CMG|first=Rose|last=Lilah|publisher=[[Hotnewhiphop]]|date=16 September 2015|accessdate=4 August 2016}} Youngsta and another Memphis hip hop recording artist, [[Young Dolph]] got into a feud in early 2016,{{cite news|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2016/03/blac-youngsta-young-dolph-diss/|title=Blac Youngsta Takes the Gloves Off on Young Dolph Diss “Shake Sum”|publisher=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL Mag]]|date=17 May 2016|accessdate=4 August 2016}} which was later dropped that year. ==Career== Youngsta first became known regionally after his 2014 hit single, ''Heavy'', from his third mixtape, ''Fast Bricks 3''.{{cite news|url=http://heavy.com/news/2016/01/blac-youngsta-sam-benson-rapper-handcuffed-stopped-by-police-outside-atlanta-bank-200000-200k/|title=Blac Youngsta: 5 Fast Facts You Need to Know|first=Stephanie|last=Dube Dwilson|publisher=[[Heavy.com]]|date=9 January 2016|accessdate=4 August 2016}} He later caught the attention of [[Yo Gotti]], another hip hop recording artist from his area, from appearing in one of Gotti’s videos, which landed a meeting with his label and latter meeting with Gotti himself, and gave Youngsta an opportunity to tour with him for two months.
