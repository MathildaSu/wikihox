17 Total |concert_tour_name = PSA Tour | artist = [[Fifth Harmony]] |number_of_legs = 2 }} The '''PSA Tour''' is the third major headlining concert tour by American girl group [[Fifth Harmony]], it is in support of their third studio album, ''[[Fifth Harmony (album)|Fifth Harmony]]''. The tour is scheduled to begin on September 29, 2017, in [[Santiago]], [[Chile]], at the [[Movistar Arena]]. ==Background== The first leg of tour dates, that will take place in [[Latin America]], were officially announced on August 9, 2017, 16 days before the release of ''[[Fifth Harmony (album)|Fifth Harmony]]''. https://twitter.com/FifthHarmony/status/895301761145217026. On August 13th, the group announced a batch of Australian dates.http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7898375/fifth-harmony-2017-australia-tour.
