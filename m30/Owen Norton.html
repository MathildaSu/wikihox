xml:space="preserve" bytes="8455"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Owen Norton|timestamp=20160613084950|year=2016|month=June|day=13|substed=yes|help=off}} {{Multiple issues| {{notability|Music|date=June 2016}} {{BLP self-published|date=June 2016}} }} {{Infobox musical artist |name= Owen Norton |image= Owen Norton Portrait.jpg |caption= |alias= |background = non_performing_personnel |birth_date= {{birth date and age|df=yes|1999|09|18}} |origin= [[Leeds]], England |genre= [[Electronic dance music|EDM]], [[future house]], [[progressive house]], [[future bass]] |website= {{URL|www.owennorton.com}} }} '''Owen Norton''' (born 18 September 1999), is a British producer and musician. He is best known for the tracks “Infinity”, with an aggregated play count of over 300,000,{{Cite web|title = Infinity - Single by Owen Norton & Chasner on Spotify|url = https://open.spotify.com/track/2XhkTL5tUJzsxNyLC17GcD|website = Spotify}}{{Cite web|title = Infinity - Single by Owen Norton & Chasner on YouTube|url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ckyNMDC-ZSQ|website = YouTube}}{{Cite web|title = Infinity - Single by Owen Norton & Chasner on Soundcloud|url = https://soundcloud.com/owennortonmusic/infinity|website = Soundcloud}} and his remix of [[Flo Rida]]’s hit “Hello Friday”, which amassed over 400,000 digital plays on YouTube.{{Cite web|title = Hello Friday (Owen Norton Remix) | url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zgrfK7OxfHc | website = YouTube}} He also has a weekly playlist on [[Spotify]], named '''“The Owen Norton Selection”'''.{{Cite web|title = The Owen Norton Selection|url = https://open.spotify.com/user/owennorton/playlist/6lteOFfDd6cvcw9dd0Mwak|website = Spotify}} == Career == === 2013 - 2015: early career and first releases === In 2013, Norton started to produce songs with [[Image-Line]]’s [[FL Studio]], and published tracks independently on his Soundcloud page. During the summer of 2013, his track “Stranger” was released on [[Beatport]] and [[Spotify]], via Cue Digital Records{{Cite web|title = Stranger (Single) |url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Zsp6yglvjc |website = YouTube}} After this, Norton followed up with several bootlegs including [[Calvin Harris]]’s “Flashback” {{Cite web|title = Calvin Harris - Flashback (Owen Norton Remix) |url = http://www.yourmusicradar.com/calvin-harris-flashback-owen-norton-remix/|website = Your Music Radar}} and [[Ella Henderson]]’s “Ghost” {{Cite web|title = Ella Henderson - Ghost (Owen Norton Remix) | url = http://www.earbuddy.net/51332/ella-henderson-ghost-owen-norton-remix.html/|website = Earbuddy}} In October 2014 Norton released a second original, this time a collaboration with Dutch DJ Flowmotion called "Ready" {{Cite web|title = Ready (Single) | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y4pvzyXV_YU | website = YouTube}} and then subsequent single "Road To Nowhere" which was eventually uploaded to EDM.com's [[Soundcloud]] profile later that year.{{Cite web|title = Road To Nowhere (Single) |url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Zsp6yglvjc | website = YouTube}} In 2015 he released a bootleg of [[Snow Patrol]]'s "Chasing Cars".{{Cite web|title = Chasing Cars (Owen Norton & Sebastian Wibe Remix) |url = https://soundcloud.com/sebastian-wibe-bootlegs/chasing-cars | website = Soundcloud}} Dutch DJs [[Bassjackers]] closed their set at [[Tomorrowland]] with the bootleg,{{Cite web|title = Bassjackers Live at Tomorrowland 2015 (Tracklist) |url = http://www.1001tracklists.com/tracklist/82960_bassjackers-at-smash-the-house-stage-tomorrowland-belgium-2015-07-26.html | website = 1001tracklists}} and then [[Martin Garrix]] played it during some smaller club shows towards the end of the year. Later in 2015 [[BBC Radio Leeds]] played his single "Wildfire"{{Cite web|title = Wildfire (Single) | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0CDXDtL-V-Y | website = YouTube}} on their [[BBC Introducing]] show. Norton’s [[Soundcloud]] profile was terminated on the 14th of August, 2015 {{Cite web|title = Owen Tweeting about his Soundcloud’s termination | url = https://twitter.com/OwenNorton_/status/632192693347741697 | website = Twitter}} and his discography disappeared from music streaming services, including [[Spotify]], shortly afterwards. === 2016=== On February 19, Norton’s first release on his new [[Soundcloud]] profile and previously cleared [[Spotify]] tracklist was "Infinity”, a collaboration with personal friend Chasner.{{Cite web|title = Infinity - Single by Owen Norton & Chasner on iTunes|url = https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/infinity-single/id1084122774|website = iTunes}} Garrix played it in Mexico at [[Electric Daisy Carnival]] {{Cite web|title = Martin Garrix playing 'Infinity' at EDC Mexico 2016 |url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=frPlps1RhHU | website = YouTube}} Shortly afterwards, Norton released "Liberté" {{Cite web|title = Liberté - Single by Owen Norton on iTunes|url = https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/liberte-single/id1099284223|website = iTunes}} - a track significantly different to his previous releases in both sound and style. Later that month, Liberté was broadcast on [[Capital (radio network)]] and [[Radio 538]] on Garrix's radio show.{{Cite web|title = The Martin Garrix Show 85|url = http://www.1001tracklists.com/tracklist/104794_martin-garrix-the-martin-garrix-show-085-2016-04-22.html#tr_306603 | website = 1001tracklists}} After being approached by [[Atlantic Records]], Norton went on to produce one of four official remixes of [[Flo Rida]]’s [[Hello Friday]] which was released on May 6, 2016.{{Cite web|title = Hello Friday (remixes) on iTunes|url = https://itunes.apple.com/au/album/hello-friday-feat.-jason-derulo/id1109067667|website = iTunes}} The remix also premiered on EDM Youtube Channel "House Nation" {{Cite web|title = Hello Friday (Owen Norton Remix) | url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zgrfK7OxfHc | website = YouTube}} and has over 400,000 views as of June 2016. Since release, the remix gathered radio support from [[Tiësto]],{{Cite web|url=http://www.1001tracklists.com/tracklist/108690_tiesto-olly-james-club-life-480-2016-06-10.html|title=Tiësto & Olly James - Club Life 480 2016-06-10 Tracklist / Playlist · 1001 Tracklists|last=1001Tracklists|website=www.1001tracklists.com|access-date=2016-06-12}} [[Martin Garrix]]{{Cite web|url=http://www.1001tracklists.com/tracklist/106926_martin-garrix-the-martin-garrix-show-089-2016-05-20.html#tr_309162|title=Martin Garrix - The Martin Garrix Show 089 2016-05-20 Tracklist / Playlist · 1001 Tracklists|last=1001Tracklists|website=www.1001tracklists.com|access-date=2016-06-12}} & [[Bassjackers]],{{Cite web|url=http://www.1001tracklists.com/tracklist/107427_bassjackers-jackindabass-radio-094-2016-05-27.html#tr_309162|title=Bassjackers - JackinDaBass Radio 094 2016-05-27 Tracklist / Playlist · 1001 Tracklists|last=1001Tracklists|website=www.1001tracklists.com|access-date=2016-06-12}} all of which were broadcast on Dutch [[Radio 538]]. == Discography == === Singles === {| class="wikitable" !
