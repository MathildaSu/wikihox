The other missions in India are the Australian High Commission in [[New Delhi]] and the Australian Consulate General in [[Mumbai]]. The current Consul General of the [[Chennai]] consulate is Sean Kelly, incumbent since January 2014. He was preceded by David Holly. ==History== The Australian Government has had a presence in Chennai since 1995. The decision of opening a Consulate General in South India was announced in March 2006, when the then Australian prime minister, John Howard, visited the [[Indian Institute of Technology Madras]].{{cite news | last = | first = | coauthors = | title = Australian consulate in Chennai soon | newspaper = The Hindu | location = Chennai | pages = | language = | publisher = The Hindu | date = 9 March 2006 | url = http://www.hindu.com/2006/03/09/stories/2006030910750100.htm | accessdate = 19 Jul 2012}} On 26 February 2007, the mission was upgraded to Consulate General when the Australian Minister for Trade, Warren Truss, opened a new office for the Australian Consulate General in the city.{{cite news | last = | first = | coauthors = | title = India important for Australia's prosperity | newspaper = The Hindu | location = Chennai | pages = | language = | publisher = The Hindu | date = 27 February 2007 | url = http://www.hindu.com/2007/02/27/stories/2007022706770500.htm | accessdate = 19 Jul 2012}} Austrade's Trade Commissioner to Chennai, Aminur Rahman, became the first Consul General of the expanded mission. A new chancery was planned to be established for an expected opening in 2011.{{cite news | last = Suryanarayana | first = P. S. | coauthors = | title = More powers for Australian consuls in Chennai, Mumbai | newspaper = The Hindu | location = Chennai | pages = | language = | publisher = The Hindu | date = 7 July 2010 | url = http://www.thehindu.com/news/international/article503118.ece | accessdate = 20 Jul 2012}} The Consulate General is located at Express Avenue Estate, Whites Road, [[Royapettah]]. ===List of consul generals=== {{Expand section|date=July 2012}} Upon expansion as a Consulate General in January 2007, the mission has been headed by consul generals.
