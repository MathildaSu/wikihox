xml:space="preserve" bytes="1694"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Geoffrey Wong|timestamp=20160801181808|year=2016|month=August|day=1|substed=yes|help=off}} '''Geoffrey Wong''' (born May 1993, [[Melbourne]], [[Australia]]) is a British businessman and [[hedge fund]] manager, specialising in the [[Global Macro]] markets.{{cite web|title=Company info|url=https://webb-site.com/dbpub/officers.asp?p=2478613|website=Company Information}}. Griffin]]'s [[Citadel LLC]], [[Israel Englander]]'s Millennium Management LLC, Ortus Capital Management and Graham Capital Management. {{DEFAULTSORT:Wong, Geoffrey}} [[Category:1993 births]] [[Category:Living people]] [[Category:British hedge fund managers]] [[Category:British investors]] [[Category:Hong Kong financial businesspeople]]
