She has one sister ''Nidhi'', who is an actress.{{cite web|url=http://www.mangalam.com/women/interview/150779?page=0,0 |title=രസതന്ത്രം |publisher=mangalam.com |date=2014-02-19 |accessdate=2014-04-01|language=Malayalam}} Rasna acted in tele films as well. Director Saji Surendran invited Rasna for his upcoming serial "Ammakkay". It was Razna's first serial and she did well even though her role was not a prominent one. Luck was with her, seeing her performance in the serial "Ammakkay", she was called to act in the Asianet mega serial "Parijatham". The serial "Parijatham" was able to bring in lot of fame and popularity to the teenage actress. She did double roles in the serial as Aruna and Seema, the twin sisters.
