He is best known as one half of the [[United Kingdom|British]] [[duet (music)|duo]], [[Lighthouse Family]].{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/lighthouse-family-p165384/biography|title=Biography: Lighthouse Family|last=Bush|first=John|publisher=[[Allmusic]]|accessdate=19 February 2011}} He attended [[Newcastle University]], from which he graduated with a double first class [[Bachelor's degree|Honours degree]] in [[French language|French]] and [[German language|German]]. Currently, Tucker is playing [[piano]] with his new [[musical ensemble|band]], The Orange Lights.
