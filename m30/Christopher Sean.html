He won the title of Mr. Asia USA in 2007. He has been cast in [[NBC]]'s long running soap opera drama series, ''[[Days of Our Lives]]''. He will debut as Paul on November 7, 2014.{{cite web|last=Lewis|first=Errol|url=http://www.soapoperanetwork.com/2014/10/christopher-sean-joins-days-cast|title=Christopher Sean Joins ‘DAYS’ Cast|publisher=soapoperanetwork.com||date=October 31, 2014|accessdate=November 4, 2014}} He resides in [[Los Angeles]] and is the Co-founder of Between The Lines Productions. == Early life and pageants == Sean was born in [[Oak Harbor, Washington]] to a military father. He grew up being trained in Boxing, tae kwon do, wrestling, boxing, jou-jitsu and mixed martial arts. He graduated from [[Oxnard College]] in 2005, where he studied psychology. He also attended acting classes at Michael John studio for a year in Van Nuys, [[California]]. He is half-Japanese. He won the title of Filipino male pageant, Ginoong Philipinas-USA that was based in [[San Francisco]] in 2006. He went onto win another beauty pageant title when he was named Mr. Asia USA 2007 that was held in [[Beverly Hills, California]] on April 7, 2007. {{cite web|last=Carnay|first=Oliver|url=http://hollywoodflip.com/news.php?subaction=showfull&id=1176503425&archive=&start_from=&ucat=1&| publisher=hollywoodflip.com|title=Chris Friel wins first star-studded Mr. Asia USA 2007 pageant|accessdate=April 13, 2007|date=November 4, 2014}}{{cite web|last=Carnay|first=Oliver|url=http://www.hollywoodflip.com/article.php?subaction=showfull&id=1164823712&..| publisher=hollywoodflip.com|title=2007 Ginoong Pilipinas-USA proclaimed; Mark Figueroa from Chicago, Illinois wins title|accessdate=November 29, 2006|date=November 4, 2014}} == Career == Sean appeared on the web series, ''[[The Lizzie Bennet Diaries]]'' from 2012 to 2013, as Bing Lee.{{cite web|last=|first=|url=http://www.pemberleydigital.com/the-lizzie-bennet-diaries/cast-lbd/christopher-sean/| publisher=pemberleydigital.com|title=Christopher Sean|accessdate=|date=November 4, 2014}} He also guest starred in the episodes of ''[[Hollywood Heights (TV series)|Hollywood Heights]]'', ''[[The Mindy Project]]'' and ''[[Hawaii Five-0]]''.
