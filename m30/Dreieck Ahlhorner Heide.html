It connects the [[Bundesautobahn 29|A29]] coming from [[Wilhelmshaven]] to the [[Bundesautobahn 1|A1]] [[Heiligenhafen]]-[[Saarbrücken]]. {{Internetquelle|hrsg=Autobahnkreuze & Autobahndreiecke in Deutschland|url=http://www.autobahnkreuze-online.de/junction.php?road=A29&exit=21|titel=AD Ahlhorner Heide|datum=2011|zugriff=2013-03-03}} == Geography == The interchange lays south the district Ahlhorn, in the village [[Großenkneten]], in the [[Landkreis Oldenburg]] and in the municipal area of the village [[Emstek]] in the [[Landkreis Cloppenburg]], southwest of the interchang. East of the interrchange lays the village of [[Visbek]] in the [[Landkreis Vechta]]. So the interchange lays in the triangle of three Lankreisen. The interchange lays inside the triangle [[Oldenburg]] and [[Bremen]] north, and [[Osnabrück]] south of it. The interchange lays near the national reserve [[Naturpark Wildeshauser Geest]]. == History == The Dreieck Ahlhorner Heide was opened in 1984 when the A29 was linked to the A1, that in 1966 already opened as ''A11''. == Interchange layout == Near the interchange the A29 has a 2x2 configuration as well as the A1 to Bremen vice-versa, instead the A1 to Osnabrück vice-versa have got a 2x3 configuration. All connecting road are having a single lane. == Traffic near the interchange == Approximately 60.000 vehicles pass the interchange on a daily basis. {| class="wikitable" ! From !! to !!
