He gained major recognition by releasing the mixtape ''[[Luv is Rage]]'' in 2015. Uzi signed a record deal with Generation Now and [[Atlantic Records]]. == Career == ===2015–present: Career beginnings and rise to fame=== Lil Uzi Vert got his name from a friend who said he rapped "like a little uzi". Uzi's rapping skills were first noticed my DJ Diamond Kuts, [[DJ Drama]] and [[Don Cannon]]. He stood out on DJ Carnage's single "WDYW" in 2015. That same year, Uzi signed a record deal with DJ Drama and Don Cannon's company Generation Now and [[Atlantic Records]].{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2016/01/next-wave-meet-lil-uzi-vert-the-next-phenom-in-rap|title=Next Wave: Meet Lil Uzi Vert, the Next Phenom in Rap|last=Diep|first=Eric|date=2016-01-20|website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|accessdate=2016-05-05}} Uzi then released his third mixtape ''[[Luv is Rage]]'' was released on December 18, 2015, featuring productions from [[Don Cannon]], [[FKi_(production_team)|FKi]], [[Sonny Digital]], [[808 Mafia|TM88]], and more, with [[Wiz Khalifa]] and [[Young Thug]] the only guest features on the album.
