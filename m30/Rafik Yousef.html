xml:space="preserve" bytes="3875"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Rafik Yousef|timestamp=20160211021835|year=2016|month=February|day=11|substed=yes}} '''Rafik Mohamad Yousef''' was an [[Islamist]] terrorist who was shot and killed when he attacked a German police officer with a knife in Berlin on September 17, 2015.{{cite news|last1=Huggler|first1=Justin|title=Islamic terrorist shot dead after Berlin attack on policewoman|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/europe/germany/11872727/Islamic-terrorist-shot-dead-after-Berlin-attack-on-policewoman.html|accessdate=17 September 2015|publisher=Telegraph|date=17 September 2015}}{{cite news|title=Iraqi man shot dead in Berlin after stabbing policewoman|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/sep/17/iraqi-man-shot-dead-in-berlin-after-stabbing-policewoman|accessdate=17 September 2015|agency=Agence France|publisher=The Guardian|date=17 September 2015}}http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/europe/germany/11872727/Islamic-terrorist-shot-dead-after-Berlin-attack-on-policewoman.html Yousef was 41 years old and a citizen of Iraq. ==Attack== The attack took place in the [[Spandau]] district of Berlin. Police were called by citizens who reported a “madman with a knife” who was threatening passersby.{{cite news|last1=Ford|first1=Dana|title=Police kill Iraqi man in Berlin after knife attack on street|url=http://www.cnn.com/2015/09/17/europe/berlin-knife-attack/|accessdate=8 February 2016|publisher=CNN|date=17 September 2015}} As the first police officer arrived at the scene emerged from her vehicle, Rafik Y attacked, stabbing her in the neck just above her protective vest. Her partner immediately drew his gun and shot the knife-wielding man four times. ==Perpetrator== In 2004, Refik Y. and two other Iraqis, Ata R. (34), and Mazen H., were convicted of plotting to assassinate Iraq's then-current Prime Minister, [[Ayad Allawi]] while he visited Germany, and were sentenced to prison. The three were arrested separately on 3 December 2004. Refik Y served an eight year jail sentence, and was freed in 2013 but required to wear an electronic leg tag.{{cite news|title=Germany police shoot Berlin Islamist after knife attack|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-34284044|accessdate=8 February 2016|publisher=BBC|date=17 September 2015}} He removed the tag before attacking the police officer. All three would-be assassins were members of an [[Ansar al-Islam]], a radical Islamist group linked to [[al-Qaeda]].{{cite news|title=Ansar al-Islam in Germany: Iraqis Convicted of Allawi Assasination Plot|url=http://www.spiegel.de/international/world/ansar-al-islam-in-germany-iraqis-convicted-of-allawi-assasination-plot-a-565930.html|accessdate=8 February 2016|publisher=Der Spiegel|date=15 July 2008}} ==References== {{Reflist}} {{DEFAULTSORT:Y, Rafik}} [[Category:2015 deaths]] [[Category:Iraqi Islamists]] [[Category:Prisoners and detainees of Germany]] [[Category:Year of birth missing]] [[Category:Stabbing attacks in 2015]] [[Category:2015 crimes in Germany]] [[Category:Islamic terrorism in Germany]] [[Category:Terrorist incidents in 2015]] [[Category:21st century in Berlin]] [[Category:Terror attacks committed by paroled Islamic Terrorists]] [[Category:Category:Failed terrorist attempts]]
