[[Android (operating system)|Android]] | released = '''July 2, 2015''' (Steam) | genre = [[Casual game|Casual]] | modes = [[Single-player video game|Single-player]] }} '''''NotGTAV''''' is a 2015 [[Casual game|casual]] [[video game]] developed and published by British game developer group, NotGames LLP, who are donating all profits from the title to local charity [[Peer Productions]]. It is currently the lead game in NotGames series. The game was released on July 2, 2015, for [[Microsoft Windows]] and [[OS X]] (available via [[Steam (software)|Steam]]) with the price $2.99.{{cite web|url=http://store.steampowered.com/app/369580/|title=NotGTAV on Steam|publisher=Valve Corporation|accessdate=July 10, 2015}} The game was also released on [[Android (operating system)|Android]] via [[Google Play]] and [[iOS]] via [[App Store (iOS)|App Store]].{{cite web|url=https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.NotGames.NotGTAV|title=NotGTAV on Play Store|publisher=Google|accessdate=July 10, 2015}}{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/notgtav/id903354679?mt=8|title=NotGTAV on AppStore|publisher=Apple|accessdate=July 10, 2015}} ==Gameplay== NotGames LLP describes ''NotGTAV'' as a 'ruthless Snake-like parody' of [[Rockstar Games]]' hit, [[Grand Theft Auto V]], but set in various locations in [[England]]. The [[snake game]] style is used as reductionist satire of the original GTA title, whilst the characters and voice acting satirise the locations and characters in Britain. ===Plot and mission=== In-game, the player is given missions to complete, like the [[Grand Theft Auto (series)|Grand Theft Auto series]] with repeated mechanics of hitting targets and completing heists. ===Graphics=== [[File:NotGTAV Graphics.jpg|thumbnail|right|The graphics of ''NotGTAV'', using 2D and hand drawn graphics]] While Grand Theft Auto V uses [[3D computer graphics|3D graphics]], ''NotGTAV'' uses [[2D computer graphics|2D graphics]].,{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9YgaQOyNVBw|title=NotGTAV Trailer|publisher=YouTube|accessdate=July 10, 2015}} and is played with a top-down view.{{cite web|url=http://gamesfiends.com/2014/04/01/egx-rezzed-interview-with-jason-orbaum-notgtav/|title=GameFiends - NotGTAV|publisher=GameFiends|accessdate=July 10, 2015}} NotGames LLP also use hand-drawn graphics throughout the game.{{cite web|url=http://www.notgames.co.uk/not-gtav/|title=NotGTAV Homepage|publisher=NotGames LLP|accessdate=July 10, 2015}} ===Sound=== ''NotGTAV'' uses "homemade sounds", with the entire soundtrack recorded [[a cappella]] in-house. ==Reception== ===Critical reception=== ''NotGTAV'' received acclaim from critics. [[Rock, Paper, Shotgun]] said "the game has a really fun look".,{{cite web|url=http://www.rockpapershotgun.com/2014/04/04/not-gtav-is-not-gtav-but-is-trying-to-save-a-charity/|title=NotGTAV Review|publisher=Rock, Paper, Shotgun|accessdate=July 10, 2015}} whilst FragRadio called the game “absolutely brilliant”.{{cite web|url=http://fragradio.com/forums/topic/3405-egx-rezzed-2014-not-gta-v-interview/|title=NotGTAV Review on FragRadio|publisher=FragRadio|accessdate=July 10, 2015}} ==Trailer== The game trailer was uploaded to [[YouTube]] on October 28, 2014. In the trailer, viewers can see some of the gameplay and NotGames highlight some of the differences between this game and the original GTA series.{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9YgaQOyNVBw|title=NotGTAV Trailer|publisher=YouTube|accessdate=July 10, 2015}} ==Copyright issues== After NotGames released the game to Steam, the game disappeared from Steam after a few days since the day that Not Games released it. [[Valve Corporation|Valve]] apparently removed the game from Steam after receiving a [[DMCA takedown|DMCA copyright claim]] from Rockstar Games. The game was re-published to Steam within 24 hours, after it was revealed that the DMCA notice was being treated as a false complainant.{{cite web|url=http://steamed.kotaku.com/parody-game-notgtav-vanishes-from-steam-after-strange-c-1716839010|title=NotGTAV is removed from Steam due to copyright claim|publisher=Kotaku|accessdate=July 10, 2015}} ==References== {{Reflist|30em}} [[Category:2015 video games]] [[Category:Casual video games]]
