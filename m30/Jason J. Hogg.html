[[Colby College]] | contributions = | main interests = | spouse = | website = }} '''Jason J. Hogg''' (born August 8, 1971) is CEO of Aon Cyber Solutions. Mr. Hogg previously served as a Senior Adviser and CEO Partner for Tritium Partners, a private equity firm focused on buyouts of growth companies.
