xml:space="preserve" bytes="3089"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Ashley Zais|timestamp=20151209042321|year=2015|month=December|day=9|substed=yes|help=off}} {{Infobox pageant titleholder | name=Ashley Zais | title=[[Miss South Carolina USA]] 2007 | nationalcompetition=[[Miss USA 2007]] (Top 15) | birth_place= [[Fort Lewis, Washington]] | birth_name= Ashley Derham Zais | birth_date= {{Birth date and age|1987|03|12}} | height={{height|ft=5|in=10}} | eye_color= Blue | hair_color= Brown }}'''Ashley Derham Zais''' (born 1987) in [[Fort Lewis, Washington]]){{source?|date=December 2015}} is an American [[beauty pageant]] titleholder from [[Newberry, South Carolina]]. She competed in the [[Miss USA]] pageant in 2007 and the [[Miss South Carolina Teen USA]] pageants from 2002–2003, making the semi-finals of the 2003 event and placing first runner-up at Miss South Carolina Teen USA 2004.{{source?|date=December 2015}} In 2005 she competed in the [[Miss South Carolina USA]] pageant for the first time and in 2006 won the 2007 title. Zais represented South Carolina in the nationally televised [[Miss USA 2007]] pageant on March 23, 2007 and placed in the top 15.{{source?|date=December 2015}} Zais graduated from [[Wofford College]].{{cite pressrelease|title=Wofford senior Ashley Zais crowned Miss South Carolina USA 2007 |publisher=Wofford College|date=2006-11-22|accessdate=2006-11-26}} She was a recipient of the Palmetto Fellowship for academic excellence. Her professional modelling career has taken her to [[Tokyo, Japan]]. In 2008, Zais served as the Summer Intern coordinator for the [[John McCain presidential campaign, 2008|John McCain presidential campaign]].{{cite news|title=McCain camp hires beauty queen|url=http://www.politico.com/blogs/jonathanmartin/0408/McCain_camp_hires_beauty_queen.html|publisher=Politico|date=2008-04-21}} After the election she took a job with the [[Decisive Analytics Corporation]] in Arlington, Virginia.{{source?|date=December 2015}} Ashley married former John McCain presidential campaign staffer Michael Culhane Harper, an attorney at [[Jones Day]], on April 17, 2010.
