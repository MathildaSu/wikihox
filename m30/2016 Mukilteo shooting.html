xml:space="preserve" bytes="4269"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=2016 Mukilteo shooting|timestamp=20160801023849|year=2016|month=August|day=1|substed=yes|help=off}} {{recentism|date=August 2016}} {{Infobox civilian attack | title = 2016 Mukilteo shooting | image = | alt = | caption = | location = [[Mukilteo]], [[Seattle]], [[Washington]], [[United States]] | coordinates = | date = {{start date|2016|07|30}} | time = After 12:00am | timezone = [[Pacific Daylight Time|PDT]] | type = [[Mass shooting]] | weapons = [[AR-15]] | fatalities = 3 | injuries = 1 | perp = Allen Christopher Ivanov (suspected) | motive = }} On 30 July 2016, a shooting occurred during a house part held by students of the [[University of Washington]] and [[Kamiak High School]] in the affluent district of [[Mukilteo]], a suburb of [[Seattle]], [[Washington]]. Three people were killed and one person was injured. A man was arrested as he was suspected of committing the shootings.https://www.washingtonpost.com/national/3-dead-1-hurt-in-shooting-near-seattle-suspect-in-custody/2016/07/31/998ef712-56ee-11e6-b652-315ae5d4d4dd_story.html == Attack == The incident took place on an early Saturday morning. Around 20 university and high school students were attending a house party in an upscale district of Seattle. The gunman opened fire not long after he arrived at the party just after midnight local time. The gunmen approached a fireplace in the backyards and shot two students. He then went on the roof of the building and began firing down onto a group of people. Some survivors hid and contacted their relatives during the shooting. Three people were killed and one person was injured.http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/dead-shooting-small-washington-town-article-1.2732039 The suspect then fled the house. He was arrested on an interstate by state troopers over 100 miles away from the place where the incident took place in [[Lewis County]]. The arrest followed the shooting.https://www.washingtonpost.com/national/3-dead-1-hurt-in-shooting-near-seattle-suspect-in-custody/2016/07/31/998ef712-56ee-11e6-b652-315ae5d4d4dd_story.html == Aftermath == === Perpetrator === The suspected perpetrator was identified by police as 19-year-old Allen Christopher Ivanov, a software engineer who posted troubling messages on social media before the shooting. He bought an [[AR-15]] rifle one week before the shooting.http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/dead-shooting-small-washington-town-article-1.2732039 === Victims === The victims were identified as:http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-3716919/Terrified-student-hid-closet-Mukilteo-shooter-killed-ex-girlfriend-two-friend-party.html *Anna Bui, a University of Washington student and the suspect's ex-girlfriend, who broke up with him one week before the shooting took place. She was killed. *Jordan Ebner, a student who attended [[Everett Community College]]. He was killed. *Jake Long, who was also killed. *Will Kramer, an 18-year-old man who was seriously injured. === Reactions === Mayor [[Jennifer Gregerson]] reacted to the incident, saying that the community was “shaken to its core”. 200 people attended a vigil at Kamiak High School.
