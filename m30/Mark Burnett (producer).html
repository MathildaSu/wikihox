Whilst at college he developed his love for film which later lead him to [[Leeds Metropolitan University]] where he achieved an [[Master of Arts|MA]] in film production. From an early age he had a love for science, physics in particular. With this passion he gained his PhD from [[Cambridge University]] in astrophysics. == Career == Though officially starting his career after graduating, whilst at university he worked on the set of BBC television shows such as [[Doctor Who]] and [[Merlin]], shadowing their producers. Doing this gave him on the job experience that he put forward when he began his first independent film production, ''Sleeper'', which began after he graduated.
