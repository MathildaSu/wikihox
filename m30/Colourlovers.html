Monsef IV]] |launch date = December 2004 |current status = Active |revenue = |slogan = }} '''Colourlovers''' (styled '''COLOURlovers''') is a [[social network service]], [[blog]], and international creative community that focuses on [[color]] inspiration and color trends for both personal and professional creative projects. ==Overview== The colors, palettes, patterns and shapes on Colourlovers are created by a community of members using tools on the website. As of December 2011, over one million of its members have created more than 4.6 million colors, 1.9 million palettes, two million patterns and 122,000 pattern templates.{{cite web|url=http://www.colourlovers.com/|accessdate=2011-12-22|title=COLOURlovers.com home page}} There are about one million visitors each month.[http://www.reuters.com/article/pressRelease/idUS206428+11-Apr-2008+PRN20080411] COLOURlovers.com Press Release Colourlovers was founded in December 2004 by [[Darius A. Monsef IV]],{{cite news | url=http://www.oregonlive.com/living/oregonian/index.ssf?/base/living/120891031199880.xml&coll=7 | title=ColourLovers.com brings fame to Lake Oswego man | work=[[The Oregonian]] | date=April 24, 2008 | accessdate=January 15, 2012 | author=Woodward, Steve}} and is based in [[Portland, Oregon|Portland]], [[Oregon]]. In April 2011, Colourlovers raised $1 million in [[Angel investor|angel funding]].{{cite web|url=http://techcrunch.com/2011/05/03/colourlovers-raises-1-million-to-make-everyone-an-artist/|accessdate=2011-09-11|title=COLOURlovers Raises $1 Million To Make Everyone An Artist|date=3 May 2011}} A book was published in 2011 containing about three thousand choice patterns from the website.{{cite book | url = http://www.amazon.com/Color-Inspirations-Innovative-Colourlovers-Com-Community/dp/1600619452 | title = Color Inspirations: More than 3,000 Innovative Palettes from the Colourlovers.Com Community | author = Darius A. Monsef IV | isbn = 978-1-60061-945-8 | date = July 12, 2011 | location = Portland, Oregon | accessdate = 2012-01-28 }} == Color of the year == Each year since 2010, in the members of Colourlovers have voted in February of each year for their favorite colour created by themselves during the previous year, which is designated as the "Colour Lovers Colour of the Year". For example, in January 2010, all the colours created in the previous year between February 2009 and January 2010 were voted on to determine which one was the favorite colour of 2010.
