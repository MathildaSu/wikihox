Connors represented [[Iowa]] in the [[Miss USA 2010]] pageant held in [[Las Vegas Valley|Las Vegas]], [[Nevada]] on May 16, 2010 but did not place.{{cite news|title=Miss Iowa's sexy photo: It's pageant marketing|publisher=Quad-City Times|date=2010-05-11|first=Kay|last=Luna}} ===Miguel Batista incident=== [[File:Katherine Connors ceremonial pitch 11.jpg|thumb|left|Connors after throwing the ceremonial pitch]]Connors gained national attention in July 2010 after a comment from [[Washington Nationals]] [[pitcher]] [[Miguel Batista]] led to her throwing a ceremonial pitch at a Nationals game. In a previous game Batista was announced as the replacement started for [[Stephen Strasburg]] who was injured. The crowd booed Batista as he walked onto the field, which lead to him commenting after the game, "Imagine if you go to see Miss Universe, then you end up having to watch Miss Iowa. You might get those kind of boos".{{cite news|title=Katherine Connors: Can Miguel Batista walk in a swimsuit|publisher=Washington Post|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2010/07/28/AR2010072805933.html|date=2010-07-29}} When Connors replied in a statement, "I know I can throw a pitch or two!
