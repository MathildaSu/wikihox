xml:space="preserve" bytes="6313"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Linda Pinizzotto|timestamp=20141118235725|year=2014|month=November|day=18|substed=yes}} {{infobox person | image = Pinizzotto_Photo.jpg | image_size = 230px |name=Linda Pinizzotto |birth_place=[[Toronto]], [[Ontario]] |occupation=[[Radio Personality|Philanthropist]] |employer=''[[COA Ontario]]'' }} '''Linda Pinizzotto''' is a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[Entrepreneur|Radio Personality]], Founder and President of the [[COA Ontario]], which is the Condo Owners Association in [[Ontario]], a non profit Association registered in March 2010. Pinizzotto appeared on major television news broadcasting channels and the [[CBC]] documentary, The Condo Game "{{cite web|title=The Condo Game|url=http://www.cbc.ca/doczone/episodes/the-condo-game}} outlined the [[condominium]] issues on new condo construction. She appeared on CBC Metro Morning to provide information on condominium accidents. "{{cite web|title=Who Pays|url=http://www.cbc.ca/metromorning/episodes/2013/02/05/who-pays/}} Pinizzotto advocates for better rules regarding security and safety. She reports on drug labs found in high rise condominiums on CBC News "{{cite web|title=2 more drug last found in Scarborough highrise|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/2-more-drug-labs-found-in-scarborough-highrise-1.1154201}} and was on the Fire Marshal Technical Advisory Committee for recommendations relating to enhanced safety of all Ontarians from the harmful effects of unsafe levels of carbon monoxide. "{{cite web|title=Fire Marshal Carbon Monoxide Safety for Ontarians|url=http://www.mcscs.jus.gov.on.ca/english/FireMarshal/Legislation/TechnicalGuidelinesandReports/OFM_COAlarms.html#P33_1069}} Pinizzotto spoke before Members of Provincial Government, addressed professional and business groups and works to modify the Condominium Act. She has appeared on several news columns advocating better consumer protection and licensing of Property Management firms and Managers. ",{{cite web|title=Building a Better condominium act|url=http://news.ontario.ca/mcs/en/2012/06/building-a-better-condominium-act.html}}".,{{cite web|title=Expert to province Tread carefully with Condominium Act revisions|url=http://www.mississauga.com/news-story/4241196-expert-to-province-tread-carefully-with-condominium-act-revision/}} ",{{cite web|title=Ontario moves to license condo managers|url=http://www.remonline.com/ontario-moves-to-license-condo-managers/#comments}} The object of her organization [[COA Ontario]] is to raise public awareness of the lack of consumer protection and to promote better and more accountable legislation with the condominium act. News articles on new legislation and the importance of [[COA Ontario]]. Her strong [[advocacy]] has been reported in ''[[The Mississauga News]]'', part of the southwest Division of [[Metroland Media Group]] and [[Metro Morning]], [[The National (CBC)]], [[CP24]], and [[City (TV network)]] on behalf of [[COA Ontario]] and [[Condominium]] owners rights. She met with [[Queen's Park]] [[Toronto]] to meet with [[MPPs]] to introduce [[reform]] on the [[Condominium]] [[Act]] in [[Ontario]]. The outdated legislation required revision for stricter regulations to protect condo owners and a strong united voice was needed. [[COA Ontario]] supported a Private member's Bill 72 for changes in the condominium act.
