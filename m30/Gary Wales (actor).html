He is an upcoming actor, director, and producer, best known for his work in the [[television series]], ''[[2Late]]'', {{cite web |url=http://www.2latemovie.com/ |title=2014 Official Website for 2late |accessdate=2014-06-30 |publisher=2Late}} and in short films like ''[[Zombie Formal Queen]]'', along with films like ''[[The Girl in the photograph]]'', ''[[An Actor prepares (film)|Blood on the Carpet]]'', and ''[[Line of Duty 2]]''. ==Early life== Wales was born on 29 of September 1995, in [[Falkirk]], Scotland. He lived in [[Armagh]], Northern Ireland when he turned 13, he attended City of Armagh High School. It was here that he first developed his passion for Performing Arts. He then went on to the Southern Regional College to study Performing Arts Level 2 & Level 3 in Armagh. In 2013, he started his own production company G.A Productions, based in Armagh.{{cite web |url=http://garywales.mixform.com/news |title=2014 Official Website |accessdate=2014-06-30 |publisher=Gary Wales}} ==Career== Wales began his acting career in 2008 in the musical ''[[Oliver!]]''. In addition to acting, Wales is also a director and producer.
