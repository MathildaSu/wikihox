His parents moved to Kabul because of his mothers was working for the Afghanistan government. then later his family moved to Iran because of war and they lived there until he turn 11 but again his family moved to Denmark by the help of The United Nations (UN) organization. ===Career=== [[File:Arian Romal Holi.jpg|thumb|Arian Romal and Kiran Janjani at Zoom Holi Party Mumbai 2015.]] Arian started his career as a background dancer for many known artists in Denmark in 2006 after he became the Danish champion in Hip Hop and Breakdance. In the end of year 2010 he released his debut single ''I'm A Star'' in a cooperation with music composer and producer [[Ahmad Darwich]] and then another track '' Shake it '' in 2011 with [[Kim Bodnia]] appearance as guest in the video, It was a commercial hit in Denmark and become most popular. He started to do his own production in 2011 and started his own music company ''Famous Crown Entertainment'' in 2012 based in three major cities Copenhagen, Dubai and Mumbai. In 2013 He released his first [[Farsi]] song ''In Dastan'' with an interview on the leading channel ''Tolo TV'' Afghanistan, The Official video for the song was one of the most viewed video on Dari TV channels. In 2014 Arian moved to [[Mumbai]] [[India]] for his first Cinema Movie project.
