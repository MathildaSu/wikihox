His accomplishments include gold 6 consecutive times from 2007 to 2012, silver before that in 2006 in the [[International Olympiad in Informatics|IOI]][http://ioi.eduardische.com/people/804 IOI Hall of Fame - Gennady Korotkevich] and world champion (2013) in the [[ACM ICPC]][[ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest]] World Finals as part of the team of [[Saint Petersburg State University of Information Technologies, Mechanics and Optics]][[SPbSU ITMO]] as well as numerous national and International contests. He is currently the highest rated at [[Codeforces]][http://www.codeforces.com/ratings Codeforces - Ratings] and the second-highest rated Algorithm competitor at [[TopCoder]][http://community.topcoder.com/tc?module=AlgoRank TopCoder Top Ranked Algorithm Competitors] as of November 14th, 2013. == Early life == His parents are programmers. He was inspired by parents at a very early age. He made his mark on the rest of the world when he first qualified for [[International Olympiad in Informatics|IOI]] at an age of 11, which itself is a world record by a big margin. One of his famous remarks, made in an interview after [[International Olympiad in Informatics|IOI]] 2009, was "I am no Genius. I am simply good at it."[http://www.ioi2009.org/downloads/br8-3str-en.pdf Interview from IOI 2009] In the [[International Olympiad in Informatics|IOI]], he has won 6 gold medals as of the 2012 competition, with the top score overall in every contest from 2009 to 2011. He's topped all Belarusian teams in the Western Quarterfinal for [[NEERC]] 2009. == Career achievements == * Facebook Hacker Cup 2014 winner. * World Champion at [[ACM-ICPC World Finals]][[ACM ICPC]] 2013 . *In 2013 he won Yandex.Algorithm.[http://algorithm.contest.yandex.ru/contest/311/standings/?lang=en Yandex.Algorithm Final Standings] *In 2011 and 2013 his team won the [[Internet Problem Solving Contest]]. *In the [[International Olympiad in Informatics]] he won absolute first place in 2009, 2010 and 2011; a gold medal in 2007 (20th), 2008 (7th) and 2012 (2nd); a silver medal in 2006 (26th). Currently he holds records for quantity of gold medals (six) and absolute first places (three). *He won the Cormen Medal in 2010, 2011 and 2012 in [[Codeforces]] for being the best Codeforces participant in 2010, 2011 and 2012.[http://codeforces.ru/blog/entry/1101?locale=en Cormen Medal Laureates 2010][http://codeforces.ru/blog/entry/3503?locale=en Cormen Medal Laureates 2011][http://codeforces.ru/blog/entry/7613?locale=en Cormen Medal Laureates 2012] *He won Google Code Jam 2012 Round 1B, achieving a perfect score in just 54 minutes, 41 seconds from the start of the contest.[https://code.google.com/codejam/contest/1836486/scoreboard?c=1836486# Google Code Jam 2012 Round 1B] == Online Coding Profiles == *[[TopCoder]] -
