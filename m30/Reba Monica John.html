She made her debut film through [[Vineeth Sreenivasan]] directed ''[[Jacobinte Swargarajyam]]'' in 2016.{{cite web|url=http://www.filmibeat.com/malayalam/news/2016/nivin-pauly-heroine-reba-monica-john-jacobinte-swargarajyam-217275.html|title=Meet Nivin Pauly's New Heroine, Reba Monica John|work=www.filmibeat.com}} Reba was credited as the 2nd Runner-up of the popular reality series ''Midukki'' which earlier premiered on [[Mazhavil Manorama]].{{cite web|url=http://www.manoramanews.com/news/entertainment/reba-interview.html|title=An interview with Reba Monica John|work=www.manoramanews.com}} == Personal life and career == Reba Monica John was born in [[Kochi]]. She is currently pursuing her Masters in Chemistry at [[Christ University]], [[Bangalore]].{{cite web|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/malayalam/movies/news/Nivin-pauly-makes-everyone-smile/articleshow/51343899.cms|title=Nivin pauly makes everyone smile|work=timesofindia.indiatimes.com}} Before essaying the lead role in the film, she was too seen in few advertisement in which Dhatri Hair Oil was one of her notable works.
