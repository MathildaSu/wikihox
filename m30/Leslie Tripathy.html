She was dragged into the controversial Biranchi murder case.{{cite web|url=http://www.hindu.com/2008/05/02/stories/2008050258300300.htm|title=Branch Case:Official summons Leslie Tripathy}} She is a student of English Literature. Leslie is the daughter of Prof.Dr.Sailendra Narayan Tripathy, Poet, and Professor of English in Bhubaneswar, India. Leslie has topped her Bachelors in English and pursued Masters in English. She has been awarded and felicitated for her contributions to Indian and Oriya Culture by Their Excellencies The Governors of Orissa and Tamil Nadu respectively. She has also been awarded the State Rajiv Gandhi Samaan for her contribution as an Actress. She has acted in a Hollywood Crossover English Film ''A Woman From Georgia'' and also acted in 2 Indian movies and 240 music videos. Young Leslie Tripathy is the Editor of Poesie India International, and Brand Ambassador of The Eternity, another Poetry Journal, of which she is the Asst.Editor.
