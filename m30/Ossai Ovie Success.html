Arthur Okowa Ifeanyi{{Cite journal|date=2016-07-15|title=Arthur Okowa Ifeanyi|url=https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Arthur_Okowa_Ifeanyi&oldid=729952583|journal=Wikipedia|language=en}} on special duties, as well as a blogger and an entrepreneur == Early Life and Education == Ossai Ovie Success was born on February 2, 1992 to Ossai Macaulay and Ubroda Marian in Ogume, Ndokwa West Local Government area{{Cite journal|date=2016-09-12|title=Ndokwa West|url=https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Ndokwa_West&oldid=739104753|journal=Wikipedia|language=en}} of Delta State.{{Cite journal|date=2016-09-22|title=Delta State|url=https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Delta_State&oldid=740603227|journal=Wikipedia|language=en}} He attended Emuseri Primary School Jesse Town (1994–2000) and Idjerhe grammar School, Ugo Secondary School (2000–2006) where he graduated at the age of 16. At age 18 he went on to attend the College of Education Warri where he studied Computer and Economics and graduated in 2012 with a national certificate in education (NCE) == CAREER == While at the College of Education, Warri, Ossai Ovie Success became Students Union Government 2011/2012 session Member, Secretary to Social and Cultural Committee at the College of Education Warri . Then later National Association of Nigeria Students NANS/JCC Electoral Committee Member. On July 6, 2015, Gov Ifeanyi Okowa appointed Mr Ossai Ovie Success as his Political Aide on Special Duties at the age of 23 making him the youngest political appointee in Delta State{{Cite web|url=https://www.naij.com/1010264-amazing-read-how-24-year-old-man-gets-political-appointment-in-delta-state.html|title=Meet the youngest political appointee in Nigeria at age 24 ▷ NAIJ.COM|last=Oyibode|first=Austin|website=www.naij.com|access-date=2016-10-18}} and Nigeria{{Cite journal|date=2016-10-14|title=Nigeria|url=https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Nigeria&oldid=744285457|journal=Wikipedia|language=en}} What raised a few eyebrows concerning appointment was his age . But for those close to the Delta State government, the appointment of Ossai Ovie a Success was a merited one and did not come as a surprise because he was an integral part of Governor Ifeanyi Okowa's Media campaign team. == BLOGGER == After graduation, Ossai Ovie Success started blogging in 2013. He became an active blogger 2015 with a custom domain [http://www.ossaioviesuccess.com/ www.ossaioviesuccess.com] after making name on Facebook .
