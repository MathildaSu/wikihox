xml:space="preserve" bytes="8905">{{Multiple issues| {{Dead end|date=May 2015}} {{Orphan|date=May 2015}} }} {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Banchbo Sharad Samman|timestamp=20150524172956|year=2015|month=May|day=24|substed=yes}} {{New unreviewed article|date=May 2015}} '''Banchbo Sharad Samman''' is a unique award given to best Durga Pujo committee in Kolkataen.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kolkata during Durga pujaen.wikipedia.org/.../Durga_Puja festival,one of the important Indian festival & consider as largest carnival of Bengali residing in India specially in the state of West Bengal.en.wikipedia.org/.../West_Beng Though it is mainly Hinduen.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hindu festival but in Bengal,it is incorporated as integral part of culture of West Bengalen.wikipedia.org/.../Culture_of_.. & all section of people enjoy this festival as Sarbojanin (universal).This unique award is adjucated by Marginalized children of Kolkata & Sunderban & also by the Senior citizen.Unique conception of judging award by Marginalized children was started in the year 2002. Since inception it added new dimension in Sharodatav of Kolkata.From 2014 another category was introduced along with existing categories - "Prabasher chokhe desher sera"(best pujo adjucated by NRI's of UK). == Award category == Total five categories of awards are given to pujo committees. * '''Shera Pujo (Best Pujo) by Marginalized children''' (from Street chilfren of Kolkata,children from Sundarban,children from orphanage,children of Sex workers) (1st,2nd,3rd); * '''Shera Pujo by Seniors''' (from underprivileged of Kolkata & eminent from different field)(1st,2nd,3rd); * '''Prabasher chokhe desher sera-best pujo adjucated by NRI's of UK''' in association with London Sharad Utsav{{cite web|url=http://www.londonsu.org|title=London Sharad Utsav: Cultural Events and Durga Puja in London|work=londonsu.org|accessdate=26 May 2015}} (1st,2nd,3rd); * '''Sera Samajsebi Pujo''' for best social excellence; * '''Sera Pujo Sangathak (Best Managed Pujo)''' by management expert from Calcutta Management Association.{{cite web|url=http://www.cmakolkata.com|title=Calcutta Management Association|work=cmakolkata.com|accessdate=26 May 2015}} At the year of inception 2002 only one category of award (Shera Pujo by Marginalized children) were given to pujo committees but later other categories were introduced - * Shera Pujo by Seniors - 2008; * Prabasher chokhe desher sera - 2014 * Sera Samajsebi Pujo - 2003; * Sera Pujo Sangathak - 2014. == List of winners == * DUMDUM PARK TARUN SANGHA, * DUMDUM PARK BHARAT CHAKRA, * DUMDUMPARK 4 NO TANK YUBAK BRINDA, * DUMDUMPARK SARBOJANIN,
