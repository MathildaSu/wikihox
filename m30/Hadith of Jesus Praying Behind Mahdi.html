xml:space="preserve" bytes="6993"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Hadith of Jesus Praying Behind Mahdi|timestamp=20150326073320|year=2015|month=March|day=26|substed=yes|help=off}} {{Shia Islam |state= collapsed}} '''Hadith of Jesus Praying Behind Mahdi''' ({{lang-fa|حدیث نماز خواندن عیسی به امامت مهدی}}) refers to a collection of narrations related about the prophecy that "[[Jesus]]" will follow [[Mahdi]]'s lead in ''[[salat]]'' ("prayer") after he descends. Mahdi is the twelfth Imam of the [[Shia muslims]] and salat is the Islamic practice of worship of [[God]] which muslims perform five times a day. The prophecy is narrated in numerous hadith collections. A total of 29 Hadiths relate the return of Jesus, and his prayer with Mahdi's lead.{{cite book |last= [[Adnan Oktar|Harun Yahya]] |DUPLICATE_date= |title= The Prophet Jesus (as) and Hazrat Mahdi (as)
