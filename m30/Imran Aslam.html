xml:space="preserve" bytes="2229"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Imran Aslam|timestamp=20160327112528|year=2016|month=March|day=27|substed=yes|help=off}} {{For|the Pakistani actor of same name|Imran Aslam (actor)}} {{BLP sources|date=February 2009}} '''Imran Aslam''' is a [[journalist]] and media personality from [[Pakistan]]. Before he turned to journalism, he worked for [[Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan|Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan]] of the [[United Arab Emirates|UAE]]. He began his journalistic career as the editor of ''[[The Star (Pakistan)|The Star]]'' in the 1980s. In the 1990s he was editor of ''[[The News International, Pakistan|The News]]'', a Karachi English daily.
