xml:space="preserve" bytes="4868"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Kalyan Varma|timestamp=20140317055530|year=2014|month=March|day=17|substed=yes}} {{Infobox person |name = Kalyan Varma |image = Kalyan_Varma.jpg |image_size = 190px |caption = At BR Hills, Karnataka, India in 2006 |birth_date = {{birth date and age|1980|01|13}} |residence = [[Bangalore]], [[India]] |occupation = Wildlife Photographer & Filmmaker |website = [http://kalyanvarma.net KalyanVarma.net] |box_width = 200px }} '''Kalyan Varma''' is an award-winning[http://cities.expressindia.com/fullstory.php?newsid=160754 Jungle Book] [[Wildlife photography|wildlife photographer]] and filmmaker[http://archive.deccanherald.com/Content/Feb152009/finearts20090214118518.asp The final frontier] from [[Bangalore]], [[India]]. He currently freelances with the [[BBC Natural History Unit|BBC Natural History]] and [[National Geographic Channel|National Geographic Channel (India)]], and also works with grassroots NGO's to highlight environmental issues in India. An active evangelist of [[Free Software]] and [[Creative Commons]], he is an expert on Application security and cryptography. Kalyan is also ==Early career== He completed his degree in Mechanical Engineering from the [[People's Education Society Institute of Technology|PES Institute of Technology]]. While in college he trained at ''Exocore Consulting'', and then joined [[Yahoo]], where he worked as an application security engineer. He took care of the security needs of Yahoo Asia. He won the Yahoo super star award in 2003, given only to the best few employees in the company. In 2004, he left Yahoo to pursue his interest in wildlife and photography and discovered a love for nature and [[wildlife conservation|conservation]]. ==Current work== Kalyan works on projects for the [[BBC Natural History Unit]], [[National Geographic Channel]] and Icon Films as their Natural History cameraman and photographer. He has been involved with many landmark [[Natural World (TV series)|BBC Natural world]] programs. He is also a BBC Earth Explorer.[http://www.bbc.co.uk/expeditions/chasingthemonsoon Chasing the Monsoon] He worked on the 2011 documentary series, 'The Secrets of Wild India' for National Geographic. He is actively involved with many wildlife [[NGO]]s and conservation bodies in India, such as the [[Nature Conservation Foundation]] and VGKK. He is a founding member of [[India Nature Watch]]. He has been featured in the best seller book 'Connect the dots' as one of the top non-MBA entrepreneurs in India.[http://www.flipkart.com/connect-dots-rashmi-bansal-book-8190453025 Book : Connect the Dots ] ==Free and open source software== Kalyan is active in the [[linux]] and [[open source]] community in India, and applies his knowledge of [[FOSS]] to bring technology benefits to wildlife conservation projects. In 2008, he was invited to deliver the closing keynote at [[FOSS.IN]] in 2008.[http://foss.in/news/kalyan-varma-to-give-closing-keynote-at-fossin2008.html Closing Keynote at FOSS.IN/2008] He regularly shares his knowledge of photography through lectures and workshops on digital photography and [[wildlife photography]]. ==Awards== * Kalyan was one of the ''[[Wildlife_Photographer_of_the_Year|BBC Wildlife photographer of the year]] winners in 2013[http://www.nhm.ac.uk/visit-us/wpy/gallery/2013/images/botanical-realms/4758/cocoon-of-life.html Cocoon of life] National History Museum Retrieved 14 January 2014 * He won the ''[[Sanctuary Asia|Sanctuary]] Wildlife Photographer of the Year'' award in 2005.[http://www.thehindubusinessline.com/2005/12/11/stories/2005121102211600.htm Of tigers and tribals] * His film ''Secrets of Wild India'' won the 'Best TV series' at the International Wildlife Film Festival in Missoula. ==References== {{reflist}} ==External links== * http://kalyanvarma.net/ * http://www.bbc.co.uk/expeditions/stories/published/by/kalyanvarma * http://www.kolkatabirds.com/photo/kalyanprofile.htm {{Persondata | NAME = Varma, Kalyan | ALTERNATIVE NAMES = | SHORT DESCRIPTION = Indian photographer | DATE OF BIRTH = 1980-01-13 | PLACE OF BIRTH = | DATE OF DEATH = | PLACE OF DEATH = }} {{DEFAULTSORT:Varma, Kalyan}} [[Category:Nature photographers]] [[Category:People from Bangalore]] [[Category:BBC people]] [[Category:Indian photographers]] [[Category:1980 births]] [[Category:Living people]] [[Category:Indian people]] [[Category:Indian Hindus]] [[Category:Nature photography]]
