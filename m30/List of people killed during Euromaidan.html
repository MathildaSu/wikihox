({{lang-uk|«Плине кача по Тисині…»}}). ==Description== The first deaths occurred on [[Ukrainian unification day|Unity Day]], 22 January 2014, during [[2014 Hrushevskoho Street riots|clashes with the police]] on [[Hrushevskoho Street]] in [[Kiev]], where 3 Euromaidan activists were shot dead. On the same day, a dead body of another Euromaidan member was found on the outskirts of the city; he was [[kidnapping|kidnapped]] a day before together with [[Ihor Lutsenko]], who was released. These were the first victims to die in demonstrations in Ukraine since gaining national independence in 1991. The deaths caused [[Euromaidan#Protests across Ukraine|widespread protests across the country]]. 5 more deaths in connection with Euromaidan occurred in the period between January 25 and February 13. The [[2014 Ukrainian revolution|second active phase]] started February 18. After a brief truce on February 19, the clashes renewed February 20. According to the newspaper Ukrainska Pravda, the special force ([[Berkut (Ukraine)|Berkut]]) and [[Internal Troops of Ukraine|Interior Troops]] snipers[http://www.pravda.com.ua/news/2014/02/20/7015163/ Video of snipers shooting people] [[Ukrainska Pravda]] {{Uk icon}} shot at people on Maydan and/or snipers located in nearby buildings, with special forces firing with AK-74 assault rifles.{{cite web|url=http://www.radiosvoboda.org/media/video/25270710.html|title=Силовики стріляють бойовими набоями із снайперських гвинтівок та автоматів Калашникова|publisher=radiosvoboda.org|accessdate=2014-04-03}} {{Uk icon}} However, this claim is disputed by Ex-Ukraine security chief Aleksandr Yakimenko saying that snipers who killed people during the Euromaidan operated from a building controlled by the opposition on Maidan square.{{cite web|url=http://www.pravda.com.ua/rus/news/2014/04/5/7021481/|title=Экс-глава СБУ через российские СМИ обвинил оппозицию в убийствах на Майдане|publisher=[[Ukrainska Pravda]]|accessdate=2014-04-08}} {{Ru icon}}{{cite web|url=http://vz.ru/news/2014/3/12/676797.html|title=Экс-глава СБУ рассказал подробности о стрельбе снайперов в Киеве|publisher=[[Vzglyad (newspaper)|vz.ru]]|accessdate=2014-04-08}} {{Ru icon}} The final death toll from these clashes in late February was 103 protesters and 13 police.http://wyborcza.pl/1,75477,15300590,Po_nocnym_ataku_na_Majdan__Sa_zabici_i_wielu_rannych_.html[http://www.unian.ua/politics/887221-sogodni-v-kievi-vbito-ponad-60-protestuvalnikiv-svoboda.html More than 70 were killed in Kyiv] [[UNIAN]] {{Uk icon}}[http://www.pravda.com.ua/news/2014/02/20/7015249/ Maidan medical service: 70 to 100 poople killed on Thuersday] [[Ukrainska Pravda]] {{Uk icon}} At least 17 people died from previously received wounds and injuries since then. ==Identified victims== {| class="wikitable sortable" |- !
