Directorial debut film: Thora Jee Le Chief Executive at 1NFLUENCE ]] '''Rafay Akbar Rashdi'''{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/rafay.rashdi|title=Rafay Rashdi|last=Rashdi|first=Rafay|date=|website=https://www.facebook.com/rafay.rashdi|publisher=|access-date=}} (born 16 January 1982) is a Pakistani [[filmmaker]], [[film director]], [[screenwriter]], [[Film producer|producer]] and [[music producer]]. He is the son of acclaimed TV and Radio personality Mahtab Akbar Rashdi. Rashdi is about to make his debut as a [[Film director|director]] and [[Film producer|producer]] in the upcoming Pakistani feature film ''[[Thora Jee Le]]''. == Life and career == Rafay Rashdi was born in [[Hyderabad, Pakistan]]. His father Akbar Rashdi is a retired [[bureaucrat]] and mother Mahtab Akbar Rashdi is a renowned radio and TV personality. Rashdi has done his bachelors in Business Technology Management from [[Ryerson University]], [[Toronto, Canada]] and Masters from [[City, University of London]], in Business System Analysis and Design. He has previously directed short films and web series.
