Sports|publisher=sports.yahoo.com|accessdate=2014-04-05}} ==Playing career== A teammate of fellow Rockie [[Troy Tulowitzki]] at [[Long Beach State]],{{cite web|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20130612&content_id=50414812¬ebook_id=50415050&vkey=notebook_col&c_id=col|title=Tulo credits success to good approach, staying even | Rockies.com: News|publisher=mlb.mlb.com|accessdate=2014-04-05}} Burgess never played for a [[Major League Baseball|Major]] or [[Minor League Baseball|Minor League]] team. He instead had a brief career in [[independent baseball league|independent]] ball. In 2008, he appeared in all 19 games for the [[San Diego Surf Dawgs|San Diego Surf Dogs]] of the [[Arizona Winter League]]. Primarily a [[catcher (baseball)|catcher]], he also made appearances at [[first baseman|first base]] and in the [[outfield]]. He posted a .333 [[batting average]] in 67 [[plate appearances]] with 4 [[home runs]] and 14 [[runs batted in|RBIs]].{{cite web|url=http://www.baseball-reference.com/minors/team.cgi?id=8cc59851|title=2008 San Diego Surf Dogs Statistics -- Minor Leagues - Baseball-Reference.com|publisher=baseball-reference.com|accessdate=2014-04-05}} Also in 2008, he played in 53 games for the [[Texarkana Gunslingers]] of the now-defunct [[Continental Baseball League]]. While he played a few games at catcher, he was primarily a [[left fielder]] with a few additional games played at [[third baseman|third base]].
