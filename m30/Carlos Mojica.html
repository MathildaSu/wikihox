[[Lalo Rodriguez]] |website = [http://www.carlosmojicamusic.com/ Official website] }} '''Carlos Mojica''' (born January 19, 1975) is an American singer-songwriter and musical arranger. ==Early life== Carlos Mojica was born in [[Paterson, New Jersey]] to Puerto Rican parents. By age 15, Mojica and his family had moved to [[Orlando, Florida]] and he began singing in numerous local salsa bands.http://www.carlosmojicamusic.com/biohttp://diarionoticiasweb.com/carlos-mojica-el-unico-criminal-en-el-mundo-que-nos-pone-a-gozar-videos/ ==Career== Mojica began his career in 1990 by collaborating with local bands in Orlando, Florida, and sharing the stage with various artists including [[Ismael Miranda]], [[Tito Puente Jr.]], [[Brenda K. Starr]], and [[Lalo Rodriguez]] among others.[https://www.reverbnation.com/sonidocriminal/press Carlos Mojica & Sonido Criminal / Press ] In 2007, Mojica went solo, forming his band "Sonido Criminal" and touring extensively throughout the U.S. and Latin America. In 2010, Mojica launched his own record label and released his first album ''[[Crime Payz]]''. Mojica later released his second album ''[[Competencia Es Ninguna]]'' in 2015, which features a prelude track with the voice of [[Frankie Ruiz]] . ==Personal life== Mojica currently resides in [[Windermere, Florida]], with his wife Delilah Mojica, and their son Tino. In 1992, Mojica was arrested and sentenced to four years in prison.
