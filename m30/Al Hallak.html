His [[Science fiction|Sci-Fi]] [[Thriller film|thriller]] ''The Paradigm'' received The Award of Excellence at Los Angeles Movie Awards.{{cite web|title=MFA Filmmaking Grad to Judge Washington West Film Festival|url=https://www.nyfa.edu/film-school-blog/mfa-filmmaking-grad-to-judge-washington-west-film-festival/|website=[[New York Film Academy|NYFA]]|publisher=Frank Pasquine|date=13 September 2016}} He has been a faculty member at the [[New York Film Academy]] and International Academy of Film & Television, Los Angeles. In 2013, he received a recognition certificate from [[Burbank]] mayor Emily Ganel-Luddy and was commended by [[Los Angeles County Board of Supervisors]]. Al Hallak was appointed judge at the 2016 Washington West Film Festival held in [[Washington D.C.]]{{cite web|title=Award Winning Filmmaker Al Hallak to Judge Washington West Film Festival|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/award-winning-filmmaker-al-hallak-to-judge-washington_us_57e1508ce4b053b1ccf2a5e0?timestamp=1474385490282|website=[[The Huffington Post]]|publisher=Marcin Migdal|date=20 September 2016}} He was recognized filmmaker as the October 2016 Filmmaker of the Month by [[The District of Columbia]] (OCTFME).{{cite web|title=OCTFME Announces October 2016 Filmmaker of the Month Al Hallak|url=https://film.dc.gov/release/octfme-announces-october-2016-filmmaker-month-al-hallak|website=[[Government of the District of Columbia|DC.gov]]|publisher=Angie M.
