xml:space="preserve" bytes="5332"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Joost van den Broek|timestamp=20140211203754|year=2014|month=February|day=11|substed=yes|help=off}} {{dablinks|date=February 2014}} {{BLP sources|date=March 2010}} [[Image:Masters of Rock 2007 - Joost van den Broek - 01.jpg|200px|thumb|Joost van den Broek performing at the Masters of Rock 2007 Festival]] '''Joost van den Broek''' (born 13 July 1982) is a Dutch [[Keyboard instrument|keyboardist]], [[Piano|pianist]], [[record producer]], [[mixer]], [[composer]] and [[arranger]]. As a musician he is most known as the former keyboardist of [[symphonic metal]] band [[After Forever]] and his work with [[Star One]], but nowadays he's a full-time producer for bands like [[Epica]], [[ReVamp]] and [[Stream of Passion]] based at the Sandlane Recording Facilities in the Netherlands. Besides this he teaches keys, arrangement and education at the Conservatory of Amsterdam and has composed & arranged for some main (public) events in the Netherlands. ==Discography as producer/mixer==
