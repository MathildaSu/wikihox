xml:space="preserve" bytes="1487"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Abandoned vehicle|timestamp=20161203005901|year=2016|month=December|day=3|substed=yes}} [[File:Abandoned car on bridleway - geograph.org.uk - 453353.jpg|thumb]] '''Abandoned vehicles''' are [[decrepit car|decrepit]] cars or cars that have become useless in other ways, which are [[abandonment (legal)|abandoned]] and [[illegal dumping|illegally dumped]] in the environment. Abandoned vehicles will be tagged with an official notice when found or reported.https://www.gov.uk/report-abandoned-vehicle Criteria for "abandonment" may differ, and a minimum duration of abandonment on the order of a few days to weeks is required.http://www.wikihow.com/Get-a-Title-to-an-Abandoned-Vehicle http://www.nolo.com/legal-encyclopedia/dealing-with-abandoned-vehicles-your-neighborhood.html When reporting such a vehicle, the required data will usually comprise the exact location, the make, colour and type, and - if available and readable - possibly the [[VIN number]] and the [[licence plate]]. Costs for removal will as a rule be taken by public councils. ==References== {{reflist}} {{stub}} [[Category:Environmental crime]]
