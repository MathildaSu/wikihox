He took about $1 million while a member of the [[MIT Blackjack Team]] and used this money to create a team to build 3D CAD software {{cite news |url=https://3dprint.com/44267/xbom-tool-for-solidworks/ |title=Hawk Ridge Systems and ATR Soft Create xBOM Tool for SOLIDWORKS }} {{cite news |url=http://www.xconomy.com/boston/2008/03/27/of-card-counting-startups-and-the-real-story-of-the-mit-blackjack-team/ |title=Of Card-Counting, Startups, and the Real Story of the MIT Blackjack Team }}. Jon founded SolidWorks in 1993, his goal was to build easy-to-use 3D CAD software for Windows . In 1997, SolidWorks sold to [[Dassault Systemes]] for $318 million, and Hirschtick stayed on board for the next 14 years. Under his leadership, SolidWorks grew to a $100 million revenue company.
