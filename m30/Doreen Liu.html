[[File:Aston Webb Hall, Birmingham University.jpg|thumb|right|150px|[[Birmingham University]]]] Doreen Liu graduated from [[Birmingham University]] with a BSocSc degree in [[Economics]], [[Politics]] and [[Sociology]] in 1970. Liu is Managing Director of World Scientific Publishing which she co-founded in Singapore in 1981. She is Treasurer of the International Women's Forum. She has served as Chair of the Publishing Association of Singapore. She has been Chair of the Publishing Committee of the Singapore-British Business Council. She has also served as chair of the Printing and Publishing Advisory Committee. Liu received the Entrepreneurship Excellence Award in 1990, organised by the Entrepreneurship Development Centre. She received the Entrepreneur of the Year Award for Women in Asia in 2010, organised by RBS Coutts and the [[Financial Times]].
