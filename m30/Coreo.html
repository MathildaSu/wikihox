[http://www.mycoreobahrain.com/ www.mycoreobahrain.com] |footnotes = |intl = }} '''Coreo''' is a real estate company operating in Qatar and Bahrain.{{cite web|url=http://www.theedge.me/qatars-online-real-estate-portals/|title=Qatar's online real estate portals|publisher=Theedge.me |date=February 24, 2013|accessdate=September 29, 2014|archivedate=September 17, 2014|archiveurl=//web.archive.org/web/20140917224951/http://www.theedge.me/qatars-online-real-estate-portals/|deadurl=no}} The site lists properties for sale and rent and has an active [[blog]] section. The company is headquartered in [[Doha]], [[Qatar]] and operates all over [[Qatar]] and [[Bahrain]]. ==History== Founded in Doha, Qatar in 2008, Coreo gradually expanded its operations into nearby Bahrain providing real estate services for clients interested in selling, renting or buying in Qatar and Bahrain's real estate market. As a real estate brokerage, Coreo has been consulting and assisting its clients with marketing real estate portfolios for sale or lease, conducting property evaluations, and providing relocation and property management services. ==Notable achievements== Coreo has worked with many notable clients including UDC, [[Qatar Foundation]], W Doha Hotel, Commercial Bank, QIB and Al Wa’ab City.
