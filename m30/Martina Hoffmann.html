Martina Hoffmann was born in Germany and she spent most of her childhood in Cameroon, West Africa.{{cite web|last=Transpersonal|first=Spirit|title=Visionary Artist: Martina Hoffmann (interview)|url=https://transpersonalspirit.wordpress.com/2013/02/13/visionary-artist-martina-hoffmann/|publisher=1 February 13th, 2013}} She studied art at the Johann Wolfgang Goethe University in Frankfurt, Germany.{{cite web|last=Tradition|first=WiseWomen|title=The Visionary Art of Martina Hoffman (interview)|url=http://www.wisewomantradition.com/wisewomanweb/2010/10/martina-hoffmann.html|publisher=3 October 10th, 2010}} ==Quotes== In a 2013 interview for [[Transpersonal Spirt]] Hoffmann: “My work is an attempt to show spirit as the universal force which unifies us beyond the confines of cultural and religious differences. By accepting the interdependency of all life and our universal interconnectedness we have a chance to heal and transform the planet’s general state of woundedness. In using art as a tool for transformation, we have the opportunity to create a reality as beautiful, healthy and strong as our imagination permits.”{{cite web|last=Hoffmann|first=Martina|title=Visionary Artist About: Martina Hoffmann (biography)|url=http://www.martinahoffmann.com/about|publisher=2 February 13th, 2013}} ==See also== *[[Robert Venosa]] *[[Visionary art]] ==References== {{Reflist|colwidth=30em}} ==External links== *{{cite web|url=http://www.venosa.com/martina/biography_photos.html|publisher=venosa.com|title=Martina's Hoffmann|accessdate=2017-03-08}} *{{cite web|url=https://transpersonalspirit.wordpress.com/2013/02/13/visionary-artist-martina-hoffmann/|publisher=transpersonalspirit.wordpress.com|title=Visionary Artist: Martina Hoffmann | transpersonalspirit|accessdate=2017-03-08}} *{{cite web|url=http://www.wisewomantradition.com/wisewomanweb/2010/10/martina-hoffmann.html|publisher=wisewomantradition.com|title=Wise Woman Weblog: The Visionary Art of Martina Hoffman|accessdate=2017-03-08}} *{{cite web|url=http://www.martinahoffmann.com/about|publisher=martinahoffmann.com|title=Martina Hoffmann Fine Art Gallery and Boutique|accessdate=2017-03-13}} {{DEFAULTSORT:Hoffmann, Martina}} [[Category:1957 births]] [[Category:German women artists]] [[Category:Living people]]
