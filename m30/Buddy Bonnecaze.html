xml:space="preserve" bytes="2731"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Buddy Bonnecaze|timestamp=20150701005313|year=2015|month=July|day=1|substed=yes}} {{multiple issues| {{cleanup|date=December 2009}} {{Notability|Biographies|date=December 2009}} }} {{Infobox poker player |name=Buddy Bonnecaze |wsop bracelet count=2 |wsop money finishes=6 |wsop main event best finish rank=35th |wsop main event best finish year=1986 }} '''Buddy Bonnecaze''' is an American poker player, winner of two [[World Series of Poker bracelet]]s. Bonnecaze began cashing in poker tournaments in the mid-1980s and made his first cash in the World Series of Poker in 1986, finishing in 35th place in the Main Event, winning $7,500. He won his first WSOP bracelet in 1992 in the $1,500 Pot Limit Hold'em event.
