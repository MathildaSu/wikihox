xml:space="preserve" bytes="1970"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Cornish Cyder Farm|timestamp=20150715170413|year=2015|month=July|day=15|substed=yes|help=off}} {{multiple issues| {{notability|Companies|date=June 2012}} {{Update|inaccurate=yes|date=June 2012}} {{unreferenced|date=June 2012}} }} '''Healey's Cornish Cyder Farm''' is a small independent family run business near [[Truro]] in [[Cornwall]]. It brews and sells its own [[cider]], [[brandy]], country fruit wines and [[apple juice]] that it produces on site, this range includes Cornish Rattler cyder which is distributed by [[St Austell Brewery]] in addition to this they now produce Pear Rattler made by adding pear juice to the original Cornish Rattler. Healey's Cornish Cyder Farm also produces traditional scrumpy cyder available in medium dry and medium sweet varieties, reserve and classic cyder [[ABV]] 7.4% which is made in second hand oak whiskey barrels. The farm also produces 27 different varieties of jams, marmalades, sauces, chutneys, a pickle and a mustard. Healey's also make England's Oldest [[Whiskey]], which recently won [[Jim Murray's]] 'European Whiskey of the year'{{cn|date=June 2013}} The farm is also a [[tourist attraction]], with some 400,000 visitors per year.{{cn|date=June 2013}} ==See also== * [[List of farms in Cornwall]] ==References== {{reflist}} [[Category:Beverage companies of the United Kingdom]] [[Category:Cider]] [[Category:English ciders]] [[Category:Companies based in Cornwall]] [[Category:Cornish cuisine]] [[Category:Brandies]] [[Category:Apple products]] [[Category:Visitor attractions in Cornwall]] [[Category:Farms in Cornwall]] {{drink-company-stub}} {{beer-stub}} {{Cornwall-geo-stub}}
