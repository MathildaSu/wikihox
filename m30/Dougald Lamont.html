xml:space="preserve" bytes="2657"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Dougald Lamont|timestamp=20170912175816|year=2017|month=September|day=12|substed=yes|help=off}} '''Dougald Lamont''' (born April 23, 1969) is the owner of Jet Pack Media, a [[Winnipeg]] based communications and digital media company, and lecturer of Government Relations at the University of Winnipeg. A former Policy Advisor and Communications Officer for [[Robert-Falcon Ouellette]],{{cite web|url=http://www.nationalnewswatch.com/2017/06/05/dougald-lamont-makes-another-bid-for-leadership-of-the-manitoba-liberals/|title=Dougald Lamont makes another bid for leadership of the Manitoba Liberals – National Newswatch|website=www.nationalnewswatch.com|accessdate=12 September 2017}} he is also a contributor to iPolitics{{cite web|url=https://ipolitics.ca/author/dougald-lamont/|title=Why public sector work pays|publisher=|accessdate=12 September 2017}} and the CBC{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/manitoba/canadian-taxpayer-federation-opinion-lamont-1.3802441|title=Canadian Taxpayers Federation has 5 members — why should we care what they think?|publisher=|accessdate=12 September 2017}} and is regularly involved with Althea Guiboche, the “Bannock Lady”, and Got Bannock.{{cite web|url=https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/take-got-bannock-to-the-next-level#/|title=Take Got Bannock? to the Next Level!|website=Indiegogo|accessdate=12 September 2017}} Lamont ran in 2013 for leader of the [[Manitoba Liberal Party]] and finished second to [[Rana Bokhari]]. On June 5 2017, Lamont announced he was seeking the [[Manitoba Liberal Party leadership election, 2017|leadership]] of the Manitoba Liberal Party after the resignation of Rana Bokhari.{{cite web|url=http://globalnews.ca/news/3503886/dougald-lamont-enters-name-as-second-candidate-for-helm-of-manitoba-liberals/|title=Dougald Lamont running for helm of Manitoba Liberals|publisher=|accessdate=12 September 2017}} == References == {{reflist}} {{DEFAULTSORT:Lamont, Dougald}} [[Category:1969 births]] [[Category:Living people]] [[Category:Manitoba Liberal Party politicians]] [[Category:University of Winnipeg faculty]]
