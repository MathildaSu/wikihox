Founder and owner Brad Kriser began in 2006 with a mission to create the "whole foods of pet stores".{{cite web|url=http://www.chicagobusiness.com/article/20120324/ISSUE01/303249981/its-a-dog-eats-apples-world|title=Subscription Center|publisher=|accessdate=27 February 2015}} Bloomberg Business profiled Brad in 2013.{{cite web|url=http://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2013-04-17/fido-goes-organic-as-private-equity-eyes-pricey-pet-food|title=Fido Goes Organic as Private Equity Eyes Pricey Pet Food|author=Lauren Coleman-Lochner|work=Bloomberg.com|accessdate=27 February 2015}} Kriser's now operates 26 locations in California,{{cite web|url=http://finance.yahoo.com/news/natural-pet-retailer-krisers-open-150500958.html|title=All-Natural Pet Retailer Kriser's To Open Sixth Location in Laguna Niguel|date=21 April 2014|work=Yahoo Finance|accessdate=27 February 2015}} Texas,{{cite web|url=http://urbanpawsmagazine.com/archives/2525|title=Nationally Acclaimed All-Natural Pet Retailer to Open Second Houston Location|publisher=|accessdate=27 February 2015}} Colorado {{cite web|url=http://www.denverpost.com/ci_20324500/natural-pet-food-retailer-krisers-open-three-stores|title=Natural pet food retailer Kriser's to open three stores in Colorado|publisher=|accessdate=27 February 2015}} and Illinois with the 27th store set to open in Houston, TX in Spring 2015. In 2014 Kriser's Natural Pet was named Pet Product News Retailer of the Year {{cite web|url=http://www.petproductnews.com/retailer-of-the-year/2014-2015/its-a-family-affair.aspx|title=It’s A Family Affair|publisher=|accessdate=27 February 2015}} and was named to Inc. 5000's list of power rankings.{{cite web|url=http://www.inc.com/profile/krisers|title=Kriser's - Santa Monica, CA|work=Inc.com|accessdate=27 February 2015}} ==Product Lines== ===Natural Pet Food=== Kriser’s advocates use of natural pet food brands like [[The Honest Kitchen]], [[Nature's Variety|Nature’s Variety]], [[Orijen]], Fromm, Primal, Stella & Chewy, Earthborn and Wellness. Natural pet food products contain no artificial preservatives, fillers, chemicals or byproducts. Kriser’s looks for products that feature high quality whole food ingredients to “help pets lead their healthiest and happiest lives”.{{cite web|url=http://krisers.com|title=Natural Pet Store - Natural Pet Food, Grooming & Supplies - Kriser's|publisher=|accessdate=27 February 2015}} ===Pet Supplies & Toys=== Kriser’s Pet supplies include leashes, bowls, collars, and beds from brands like [[Kong (dog toy)|Kong]], Jax & Bones, Mammoth, and Aussie Naturals. Kriser’s toys provide both mental and physical stimulation; products include tugs, wands, catnip, plush toys, indoor use and outdoor use.{{cite web|url=http://www.krisers.com/our-products/toys-and-more/|title=Pet Toys & Accessories l Pet Supply Store - Kriser's|publisher=|accessdate=27 February 2015}} ==Pet Grooming== Kriser’s offers pet grooming services at each location. Services include blueberry facials, teeth brushing, nail trims, and special breed cuts. Kriser's Motto {{cite web|url=http://www.krisers.com/grooming/|title=Pet Grooming - Kriser's|publisher=|accessdate=27 February 2015}} states “Our groomers treat your pets like their own. Because for a few hours, they are”. ==Pet Events== Kriser's hosts events to help educate and engage pet parents with a natural lifestyle. From pet adoptions to breed specific gatherings to pet birthdays and training Kriser's focus is on happy healthy pets.{{cite web|url=http://www.krisers.com/events/|title=Pet Events - Local Adoptions, Meetups & More - Kriser's|publisher=|accessdate=27 February 2015}} ==References== {{reflist}} {{uncategorized|date=February 2015}}
