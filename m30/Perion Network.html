The company’s headquarters are in Holon, Israel, with offices in [[San Francisco|San Francisco, CA]]; [[New York City, NY]]; [[Redmond, Washington|Redmond, WA]], and [[Paris, France]]. ==History== Perion was formerly IncrediMail, an email company founded in 1999 by cousins Yaron and Ofer Adler. Opens First Office in the United States; New York Location Strategic Milestone in Meeting Revenue and Growth Objectives|location=[[Tel Aviv]]; [[New York]]|publisher=[[AllBusiness.com]]}} and on March 25, 2009, the company's board of directors approved a buyback of up to $1 million worth of shares.{{wayback|url=http://finance.yahoo.com/news/IncrediMail-Board-Approves-46-bw-14738204.html|title=IncrediMail Board Approves $4.6 Million Dividend and Continuation of Share Buyback Program|date=20090330075149}} In August 2011, the company acquired [[Smilebox]] for $32 million.{{cite news|last=Tsipori|first=Tali|title=IncrediMail to change name to Perion Interactive|url=http://www.globes.co.il/serveen/globes/docview.asp?did=1000683465&fid=1725|accessdate=18 September 2011|newspaper=[[Globes]]|date=18 September 2011}}{{cite web|url= http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20110801005875/en/IncrediMail-Acquire-Smilebox#.VMZgdiusWSo|title=IncrediMail to Acquire Smilebox Inc.}} In September 2011, the company changed its name to Perion, the Hebrew word for productivity. In November 2012, Perion purchased SweetPacks (aka SweetIM) for up to $41 million.{{cite web|url=http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20121107006926/en/Perion-Announces-Accretive-Acquisition-SweetPacks |title=Perion Announces Accretive Acquisition of SweetPacks |publisher=businesswire.com |date= |accessdate=2013-09-23}} In January 2014, the company acquired Conduit’s ClientConnect business in an all-stock transaction valued $660 million.{{cite web|url= http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20140102005313/en/Perion-Completes-Acquisition-Conduit%E2%80%99s-ClientConnect-Creating-Leading#.VMZhhiusWSo|title=Perion Completes Acquisition of Conduit’s ClientConnect Creating a Leading Provider of Digital Solutions for Publishers}} In June 2014, Perion acquired Grow Mobile for $42 million in order to expand mobile promotions and Perion joined the TA-75 Index on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange. In February 2015, Perion acquired MakeMeReach - a rapidly growing, Paris-based company and one of Europe's leading social media advertising platforms. MakeMeReach's AdsOptim technology enables mobile app developers to efficiently and effectively scale their advertising campaigns on social media, with a specific focus on optimizing mobile ad campaigns. MakeMeReach is a Facebook Preferred Marketing Developer (PMD) and Twitter Marketing Platform Partner (MPP). The acquisition of MakeMeReach has enabled GrowMobile by Perion to expand its position as the industry’s most complete and comprehensive automated mobile marketing platform.{{cite web|url=http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20150211005348/en/Perion-Acquires-MakeMeReach-Adding-Social-Media-Mobile#.VRLpdI6sWSp|title=Perion Acquires MakeMeReach, Adding Social Media to Its Mobile Marketing Platform}} Business Wire, retrieved February 11 2015 ==Products for Developers and Advertisers== ===Perion Codefuel=== [[Perion Codefuel]] aims to optimize app installs, analyze user data and maximize ad revenue for app developers. Perion claims{{cite web|url= http://in.reuters.com/finance/stocks/PERI.TA/key-developments/article/3025748|title=Perion Network Ltd's Perion Codefuel partners with CYREN to enhance browsing safety for its app users}} Codefuel serves more than 260,000 developers, publishers, and advertisers worldwide. Codefuel includes: * InstallFuel - a customizable installer that claims to accelerate downloads * MobileFuel - integrated search feeds for mobile app developers * SearchFuel - search tools to monetize apps, extensions and websites * DisplayFuel - an advertising network.{{cite web|url= http://www.timesofisrael.com/perion-extends-microsoft-deal-ups-mobile-business/|title=Perion extends Microsoft deal, ups mobile business}} ===Growmobile=== GrowMobile by Perion helps mobile marketers acquire and engage users. The user acquisition platform provides a centralized, programmatic way to buy media and optimize campaigns across dozens of traffic sources, including ad networks, exchanges, Facebook and Twitter. The user engagement platform provides CRM managers a way to manage entire campaign lifecycles from one platform and offers pre-made campaign templates used to increase the value of app users. GrowMobile was acquired by Perion in June 2014. GrowMobile was founded in San Francisco by Co-Founders, Brendan Lyall, A.J. Yeah and Minglei Xu. ==Products for End Users== The company’s mobile and desktop apps are IncrediMail, a free email program, and [[Smilebox]], a desktop and mobile app for customizing and animating photos. ==Corporate Social Responsibility== The company donates 2% of its net profits and permits all employees to spend 40 work hours per year volunteering in the community.
