[[Think-tank]] |founded =2002 |fields = |homepage =[http://www.civitatis.eu/ civitatis.eu] }} '''Civitatis International''' is a private foundation that promotes peace, dialogue and cooperation between nations and civilizations. Civitatis International convenes policy seminars for the Ambassadors to the Court of St. James's and advises stakeholders around the world.
