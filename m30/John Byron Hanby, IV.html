His family moved to [[Austin]], [[Texas]] in 1999, where he continues to live today. He graduated from the University of Texas at Austin in 2017, where he received the honor of top film graduate for the Spring 2017 semester. He founded producing company Fractal Visuals in 2012 and has been nominated for many awards when featured in SXSW http://schedule.sxsw.com/2014/events/event_FS15863, Austin Music Video Festivalhttp://www.austinmusicvideofestival.com/new-page-3 and other festivals for his music video work. https://www.dailytexanonline.com/person/john-hanby He is most well known for directing music videos for [[Silversun Pickups]], [[The Crazy World of Arthur Brown]], and Noveller. In recent years, his production company has gained a large following from artists and filmmakers around the world. Hanby always works closely with his best friend and Director of Photography Michael Castoro.https://www.instagram.com/fractalvisuals/ [[Red Digital Cinema Camera Company]] has featured a 6 of Hanby's music videos on their "Shot on RED" feature page including "Glacial Wave," You're Too Late Satan," and others. http://www.red.com/shot-on-red/music-videos He has also been featured by [[BlackMagic Design]] for his use of their color grading products. http://hollywoodindustry.digitalmedianet.com/article/Fractal-Visuals-3702057 Hanby's work is typically focused around love and explores environments in the abstract and experimental, favoring ethereal settings and mood.
