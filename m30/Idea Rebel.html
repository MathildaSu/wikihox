They develop web and mobile content and apps. The company's headquarters is in [[Gastown]], [[Vancouver]] with a second office in [[Toronto]], [[Ontario]]. ==About== ===History=== Idea Rebel was founded in 2008 in Vancouver by [[Jamie Garratt]] (current acting [[CEO]]). They have 40 employees worldwide. Idea Rebel clients include [[Yokohama]], [[Quiksilver]], [[BMW]], [[Bell Media]], and [[FOX]].List of clients from [http://www.idearebel.com/ Idea Rebel Official Website]. In July 2012, Idea Rebel opened an office in [[Toronto]] - shortly after, a third office in Santa Monica was opened.[http://www.marketingmag.ca/news/agency-news/idea-rebel-riding-the-wave-to-california-73827 Article in Marketing Mag] ===Paperlessness=== Idea Rebel is a completely paperless company, using technology and other digital solutions to uphold this objective. Pay stubs are sent via email, notes are taken on tablets, and whiteboards are used daily. Idea Rebel believes that using paper in the office would be contradictory to the values of the company.[http://www.theglobeandmail.com/technology/tech-news/how-one-company-went-completely-paperless/article12522284/] ==Works== ===BMW i3 Become Electric=== Idea Rebel and Tool of North America collaborated on an app called Become Electric that puts the user in the drivers seat of BMW's electric car. The app was developed using a video filmed with a 360º camera, creating an immersive 3D world in which the device is a user's window into a panoramic movie.
