He is the Recipient of [http://SAM%20GLOBAL%20YOUTH%20AWARD%202014 SAM GLOBAL YOUTH AWARD 2014] and the Recipient of [http://www.gcgi.info/2013-paris-papers/429-youth-time-and-rhodes-youth-forum-introduction Best Suggested Project Award (Social and Quality Improvements in India]) at [http://Rhodes%20Youth%20Forum,Greece. Rhodes Youth Forum 2012, Greece.] He has been delegated as a Speaker at many international conferences including in USA, London, Greece, Pakistan, Nepal, Criotia, Nepal, Bangladesh Thailand, Spain, Paris, Sri Lanka, Ireland. He has Chaired many national and international conferences on renewable technology And environmental affairs. He is the youngest person in India to have 7 applied patents and more than 25 Research papers. He is working on research projects related to Energy system ,Renewable technology, Waste management, Social and Political Affairs, Biotechnology and Youth affairs. He is not only the Founder and Chairman of [http://www.iysert.org IYSERT] but he was the Youth Ambassador of India for the [http://www.youth-time.org International Youth time Movement], Checz Republic, Director in U -Governance India, Chair Role in [http://Asia%20–Pacific%20Model%20United%20nation Asia –Pacific Model United nation]Conferences, Bangkok Thailand and Project coordinator of Think Green Project Cairo (Egypt) for development of Bio-Gas plant in Cairo, Egypt project under [http://AIESSEC AIESSEC].{{cite news|last=BISWAS|first=RAKESH|title=A PRACTITIONER -INTERNATIONAL YOUTH SOCIETY OF ECOFRIENDLY AND RENEWABLE TECHNOLOGY , RAKESH BISWAS|url=http://media.wix.com/ugd/56e1a9_4c66ce8f427d46d0b5e251d8bdf8e9d2.pdf|accessdate=20 Feb 2014|newspaper=COMMON GOODS FORUM MAGZINE}} == Early life == Biswas was born in MH Jamnagar, [http://Gujart Gujart], India to an Army Background family on 1 January 1991, He belong from a village in [[Kalna]] , [[West bengal]] . He completed his basic education from '''[http://Gen.%20B.C%20Joshi%20Army%20Public%20School,%20Pithoragarh. Gen. B.C Joshi Army Public School, Pithoragarh.]''' For further studies he enrolled in to '''[[Graphic Era University]], Dehradun''' for the course [http://B-Tech%20Biotechnology B-Tech Biotechnology]. His exposure to talent began with winning the Bourn vita Quiz Contest in the year 2003and thereafter he grabbed every opportunity that came his way by participating in various science events organized at the state as well as National level. After almost a year, he had already developed a Rain water harvesting system in national children science congress], which gave an eminent exposure to his talent. But it was just the beginning, as his major achievements took lead with his work of '''magnetic engine''' (a first perceptual machine in India) and soon applied patent for this invention was filed atIPR India. He got media coverage and recognition when he presented the design model of '''Hydro Air Car''' (a car that runs in air, water and land). It also gave him the attention of people regarding his innovative nature and attracted many awards regarding that project.{{cite web|last=BISWAS|first=RAKESH|title=JOURNEY OF RAKESH BISWAS A CHID PREDILOGY TO AN ENTREPRENEUR|url=http://rakeshbiswas.in/articles-on-mr-biswas-|publisher=GYAN DARWAR MAGAZINE, INDIA}} == Work == [http://www.iysert.org International Youth Society for Eco friendly and Renewable Technology (IYSERT)] India is a venture led by Mr. Rakesh Biswas, a child prodigy and an able entrepreneur. He is presently pursuing his dual degree course in B.tech Biotech+MBA from [http://www.geu.ac.in Graphic Era University, Dehradun]. During his 3rd year of his B.tech, he thought of an idea of forming an organisation along with more students of his university, which would work independent of the college’s administration, and work for the welfare of the society and the environment. He gave a name to this idea, IYSERT. Today, IYSERT is not only an idea, but it’s an Internationally recognized organisation, working in 26 colleges of India, and 7 countries across the globe. In a period of merely 1 and a half years, [http://www.iysert.org IYSERT] has organized 10 remarkable events, and looking forward to spread its branches to the rest of the world.{{cite
