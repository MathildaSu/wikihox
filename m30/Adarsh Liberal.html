xml:space="preserve" bytes="3822"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Adarsh Liberal|timestamp=20150412070932|year=2015|month=April|day=12|substed=yes}} '''Adarsh Liberal''' (''translation: Ideal Liberal'') are a series of posters that started appearing on micro-blogging site [[Twitter]] from an anonymous handle of the same name. The posters were targeted at the Indian liberals.{{cite web| title = Indian liberals mocked - and mocking - online | url = http://www.bbc.com/news/blogs-trending-31833682|publisher= [[BBC]] | date = 11 March 2015 | first = Samiha| last = Nettikkara}} As a counter to Adarsh Liberal poster, the Indian Liberal fraternity started circulating Adarsh Bhakt(''translation: Ideal Devotee'') posters.{{cite web| title = Viral Memes: A War Between 'Adarsh Liberal' and 'Adarsh Bhakt' on Twitter | url = http://www.ndtv.com/offbeat/viral-memes-a-war-between-adarsh-liberal-and-adarsh-bhakt-on-twitter-745783|publisher= [[NDTV]] | date = 11 March 2015 | first = Adrija| last = Bose}} Adarsh Liberal posters are spin-off of the Adarsh Balak posters which originally demonstrated traits of an Ideal Boy, which were popular in India in 1980s and 90s. Indian news site ''Deccan Herald'' describes the Adarsh Liberal posters as propagated by 'saffron brigade that has hit the social networking site with poster'.{{cite web| title = 'Adarsh Liberal' hits again with a family poster | url = http://www.deccanherald.com/content/465927/adarsh-liberal-hits-again-family.html|publisher= [[Deccan Herald]] | date = 16 March 2015 }} Indian news websites have described the online exchange of posters between Indian liberals and [[Hindutva]] groups as war{{cite web| title = 'Twitter war breaks out between Adarsh bhakts and liberals' | url = http://www.deccanherald.com/content/464877/twitter-war-breaks-adarsh-bhakts.html|publisher= [[Deccan Herald]] | date = 11 March 2015 }} ==Characteristics of Adarsh Liberal== As depicted in the posters, an Adarsh Liberal is someone who:{{cite web| title = Adarsh Liberal vs Adarsh Bhakt: Hashtag war on Twitter | url = http://www.hindustantimes.com/india-news/adarshliberal-or-adarshbhakt-adarsh-liberal-cartoon-ignites-hashtag-war-on-twitter/article1-1325203.aspx|publisher= [[Hindustan Times]] | date = 11 March 2015 }} * Hits on colleague and proceeds to lift * Shifts goalposts if losing argument * Plays with pet [[Anonymity|anonymous]] [[Internet troll|trolls]] who agree * Supports [[PETA]], but eats chicken and [[Cattle slaughter in India|beef]] * Practices playing the victim card * Attacks [[Hindu gods]] to become [[Secularism in India|secular]] * Never lets facts bite him * Goes on expensive holidays to discuss & understand poverty * Supports [[Naxals]] * Misguides blind Twitter followers * Brushes up his lies early in the morning * Takes bath after meeting [[Hindi]] speaking people ==See also== * [[Liberalism in India]] * [[Secularism in India]] ==References== {{reflist}} ==External links== * [https://twitter.com/AdarshLiberal Adarsh Liberal Twitter Account] [[Category:Internet memes]] [[Category:Internet trolling]] [[Category:Internet in India]] [[Category:Twitter controversies]]
