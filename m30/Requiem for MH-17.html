The poem, reacting to the crash in Ukraine of the [[Malaysia Airlines Flight 17]] on July 17, 2014, was read[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_321nOB1X0I Reading of "Requiem for MH-17" on Echo of Moscow] on [[Echo of Moscow]] radio channel by actress [[Liya Akhedzhakova]], published in the news media, [http://fakty.ua/185135-andrej-orlov-rekviem-po-mh-17 "Andrei Orlov. Requiem for MH-17" (on "Facts" in Russian)] translated into English,[http://www.sumizdat.org/Requiem_for_MH-17.html English translation of "Requiem for MH-17" at Sumizdat website] and broadly distributed over the Internet, generating sharply divided response.{{citation needed|date=July 2014}} == References == {{Reflist}} [[Category:Malaysia Airlines Flight 17]] [[Category:Russian poems]] [[Category:2014 poems]] [[Category:2014 in Russia]] [[Category:Echo of Moscow]]
