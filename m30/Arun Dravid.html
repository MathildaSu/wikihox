He is also a technocrat with a high profile professional career. ==Early life and background== Arun's early training was under the tutelage of late Ustad Abdul Majid Khan Sahab, a direct disciple of late [[Ustad Alladiya Khan]], and life-time [[Sarangi]] accompanist to late [[Kesarbai Kerkar]]. Arun then had several years of advanced musical training with [[Kishori Amonkar]], with occasional guidance from her mother and Guru, late [[Mogubai Kurdikar]]. Arun Dravid is an alumnus of [[IIT Bombay]] and a Gold Medalist and a Ph.D. chemical engineer from [[MIT]] (USA) by academic training.http://www.arundravid.com/biography.htm He has had an engineering career with numerous awards and distinctions{{Citation needed|date=January 2009}}. ==Career== Arun Dravid has cultivated a balanced version of the “Bhava-dominant” style of Smt. [[Kishori Amonkar]] and the structural beauty that is the characteristic of the puritanical [[Jaipur-Atrauli Gharana]] gayaki. He is now recognized as an outstanding exponent of Jaipur-Atrauli gayaki in India and abroad. In her recent biography titled "Ganasaraswati" (page 61), Smt. [[Kishori Amonkar]] has specifically noted that Arunji is her only disciple who has preserved and continues to sing the pure [[Jaipur-Atrauli Gharana]] gayaki tradition. Arun also sings with equal charm light-classical varieties such as [[Thumris]] and [[Natya-Sangeet]].
