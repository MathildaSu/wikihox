xml:space="preserve" bytes="1303"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Revosiete|timestamp=20140108082347|year=2014|month=January|day=8|substed=yes|help=off}} '''Revosiete''' is a Spanish Christian Rock Band from [[Melrose, MN]]. Revosiete has been playing for about 8 years. The members are German Morelos, Victor Morelos, Jose De Jesus Morelos (Chuche) and Jose Raya. At first Revosiete was known as Luz en la Oscuridad. The band originally started with Chuche, German, and Jose, Victor than joined the band about a two years later. German is known to play the bass guitar, Victor plays the drums, Chuche is the Lead singer/ rhythm guitar and Jose is known to play the lead guitar.
