It is notable for being a partial replica of the town of [[Supetar]], [[Croatia]].Adriatica McKinney [http://www.adriaticamckinney.com/ official home page][http://www.slobodnadalmacija.hr/Mozaik/tabid/80/articleType/ArticleView/articleId/53692/Default.aspx "Milijun dolara za vilu u američkom Supetru"], article on Adriatica McKinney in [[Slobodna Dalmacija]] {{hr icon}} According to news reports, the [[real estate developer|developer]] [[Jeff Blackard]] is taking pains to lend Adriatica an air of authenticity, from purchasing [[church bell]]s from Croatia, to [[Croatian language]] signs on [[waste disposal]] areas.
