He is the son of former [[Detroit Red Wings]] and [[Philadelphia Flyers]] defenceman [[Frank Bathe]]. Bathe had brief spells in the [[American Hockey League]] over his five year professional career, playing nine games with the [[Milwaukee Admirals]] and one game with the [[Utah Grizzlies]] without registering a single point. He spent the majority of his career in the [[ECHL]] with the [[Toledo Storm]], [[Idaho Steelheads]] and the [[Stockton Thunder]] where he suffered injury that ultimately ended his playing career.
