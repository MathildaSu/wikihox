She is best known for appearing on the [[Lifetime (TV network)|Lifetime]] reality TV show ''[[Dance Moms]]''. She is also a National Title{{clarify|date=March 2014}} winner, with 10 crowns. ==Background== Ziegler was born on September 30, 2002 in [[Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania]] to Melissa Gisoni and Kurt Ziegler.{{cite web|url=http://okmagazine.com/get-scoop/ok-exclusive-dance-moms-star-melissa-insists-im-nice-mom/|title=OK! Exclusive: ‘Dance Moms’ Star Melissa Insists, “I’m The Nice Mom!” | OK! Magazine|publisher=okmagazine.com|accessdate=2014-02-14}}{{cite web|url=http://famous101.com/famous-abby-lee-miller-dancers|title=Famous Abby Lee Miller Dancers | Famous Things, People and Events|publisher=famous101.com|accessdate=2014-02-14}} Ziegler's parents divorced in 2011, citing that dance took time away from family time. Ziegler's mother later remarried Greg Gisoni in mid-2013. Ziegler has a younger sister named [[Mackenzie Ziegler]] (born June 4, 2004).{{cite web|url=http://www.twistmagazine.com/posts/twist-exclusive-twist-chats-with-maddie-mackenzie-ziegler-9254|title=TWIST Exclusive: TWIST Chats with Maddie & Mackenzie Ziegler! | Twist|publisher=twistmagazine.com|accessdate=2014-02-14}} Her family currently resides in [[Murrysville, Pennsylvania]].{{cite web|url=http://www.pittsburghmagazine.com/Pittsburgh-Magazine/November-2013/Inside-the-Dance-Moms-Empire/|title=Inside the ‘Dance Moms’ Empire - Pittsburgh Magazine - November 2013 - Pittsburgh, PA|publisher=pittsburghmagazine.com|accessdate=2014-02-14}} ==Professional Work== Ziegler began dancing at the age of 2 at Reign Dance Productions of [[Pittsburgh]] and at age 5-6 she left to go to the abby lee dance company {{cite web|url=http://www.abbyleedancecompany.com/rdp/standing%20ovation%20stats.htm|title=rdp/standing%20ovation%20stats|publisher=abbyleedancecompany.com|accessdate=2014-02-14}} Ziegler's younger sister, Mackenzie, later followed in her footsteps and join RDP and ALDC as well. In 2011, Ziegler was a contestant on the [[CBS]] dance competition show, "[[Live to Dance]]", however the show was cancelled before her episode aired.{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1625330/fullcredits?ref_=tt_cl_sm#cast|title=Live to Dance (TV Series 2011– ) - Full Cast & Crew - IMDb|publisher=imdb.com|accessdate=2014-02-14}}{{cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9V-d2S1jM3U|title=YouTube - ✰ Maddie Ziegler "Live to Dance" clip ✰|publisher=youtube.com|accessdate=2014-02-14}}{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-1357349/Paula-Abdul-devastated-post-Idol-Live-To-Dance-cancelled.html|title=Paula Abdul 'devastated' as post-Idol show Live To Dance is cancelled | Mail Online|publisher=dailymail.co.uk|accessdate=2014-02-14}} Later that year, Ziegler's mother earned a featured role on [[Lifetime (TV network)|Lifetime]]'s ''[[Dance Moms]]'', a program that documents the day-to-day lives of the young dancers on the Abby Lee Dance Company Elite Competition Team. On the program, Ziegler appears alongside her mother and Mackenzie, also a dancer. {{cite web|url=http://www.mylifetime.com/shows/dance-moms/about|title=About Dance Moms - Official Site - myLifetime.com|publisher=mylifetime.com|accessdate=2014-02-14}} In 2013, Ziegler made an appearance on the sister program, ''[[Abby's Ultimate Dance Competition]]''. In 2012, Ziegler landed her first professional acting job playing the role of 'Young Deb' on the [[Lifetime (TV network)|Lifetime]] series ''[[Drop Dead Diva]]''.{{cite web|url=http://www.shockya.com/news/2012/08/12/dance-moms-abby-lee-miller-and-maddie-ziegler-guest-star-on-lifetimes-drop-dead-divas/|title=Dance Moms' Abby Lee Miller and Maddie Ziegler Guest Star on Lifetime's Drop Dead Divas : Shockya.com|publisher=shockya.com|accessdate=2014-02-14}} Deb, a lawyer, would represent her mother, Bobbie, a dance school director, as she is being sued by the family of a young dancer. The case would remind Deb of her former dance career with flashbacks of a young Deb dancing, portrayed by Ziegler.
