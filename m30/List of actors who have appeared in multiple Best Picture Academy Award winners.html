The [[Academy Award for Best Picture]] is one of the [[Academy Awards|Academy Awards of Merit]] presented annually by the [[Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]] (AMPAS) to artists working in the motion picture industry. As of [[2012 in film|2012]], 85 films have won the Academy Award for Best Picture. Each of the actors in this list has appeared in two or more such films. This list contains a total of 131 actors and collectively represents their 309 appearances in 82 Best Picture-winning films.
