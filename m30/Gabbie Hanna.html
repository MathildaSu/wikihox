If you've been here, welcome; if you're new here | silver_year = 2015 | gold_year = 2016 | diamond_year = | stats_update = February 26, 2017 | silver_button = Yes | gold_button = Yes | diamond_button = }} '''Gabrielle Jeanette Hanna''' (born February 7, 1991),{{cite web|last1=Hanna|first1=Gabbie|title=#AskGabbie — The Gabbie Show Q&A|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=29NdeFtsaC4|website=YouTube|accessdate=30 December 2016|date=10 September 2014}} better known as '''The Gabbie Show''', is an American [[vlogger]] and web-based comedian.{{cite news |last1= Doyle |first1= Alicia |date= June 19, 2015 |title= 'Gabbie Show' star comes to Ventura to spread anti-smoking message |url= http://archive.vcstar.com/news/local/ventura/gabbie-show-star-comes-to-ventura-to-spread-anti-smoking-message-ep-1148880982-341183721.html |newspaper= Ventura County Star |edition= Electronic |location= Camarillo, CA |publisher= The VC Star |access-date= December 4, 2016 }} She is best known for her [[YouTube]] channel, where she makes comedic vlogs. ==Life== Hanna was born and raised in [[New Castle, Pennsylvania]]. She has six siblings and comes from parents of [[Lebanese people|Lebanese]], [[French people|French]], and [[Poles|Polish]] descent. She graduated from the [[University of Pittsburgh]] with a degree in [[psychology]] and a minor in [[economics]]. ==Career== Hanna began her career on the [[Social networking service|social networking]] platform [[Vine (service)|Vine]] after graduating from college whilst working as a waitress. She claims her [[pseudonym]] "The Gabbie Show", obtained at a young age, was a nickname given when she was being "obnoxious". She later began to film herself in front of a mirror now portrayed as "The Gabbie Show". Shortly after moving to [[Los Angeles]], fellow Vine personality Jessie Smiles taught her how to use the service. She also claims to have started using Vine after comedian [[Bo Burnham]] shared one of his Vines on his [[Twitter]] account.{{cite web|last1=Francesca|first1=Lauren|title=Comedians Who have Inspired Vine Stars|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ttQGGuQlHiA|website=YouTube|accessdate=30 December 2016|date=23 October 2014}} Starting on Vine, she collaborated with many of its popular creators within the network such as David Dobrik, Alex James, and Jessie Smiles. Shortly after her rise to fame on Vine, she began a YouTube channel titled "The Gabbie Show", contributing of anecdotes about her personal life and the events that happen within it ("story times"). In one of Gabbie's story time videos she spoke about briefly dating a television actor whom she didn't name, Gabbie's fans rumoured the actor to be [[Desmond Harrington]] and in a later video Gabbie confirmed this. She has collaborated with some of the more renown YouTube personalities in the past, such as [[Lilly Singh]], [[Liza Koshy]], David Dobrik, [[Joey Graceffa]], [[D-trix|D-Trix]], [[Ricky Dillon]], [[Grace Helbig]], [[Jason Nash]], [[Shane Dawson]] and [[Colleen Ballinger]] (whom also portrays herself as a fictitious character named [[Miranda Sings]]). In 2015, as an advocate for youth smoking prevention, Hanna participated in a 100-stop tour for [[Truth (anti-tobacco campaign)|Truth]], a national campaign aimed at curbing youth smoking in the United States.
