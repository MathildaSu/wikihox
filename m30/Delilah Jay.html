xml:space="preserve" bytes="2642"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Delilah Jay|timestamp=20151228090437|year=2015|month=December|day=28|substed=yes}} '''Delilah Jay''' (Born in Berlin, 1962), is a British-German novelist, singer-songwriter and politician, based in London. She has released several suspense thriller romantic literature books, as well as a song titled ''It's A Boy'' with [[Nimrod Kamer]], recorded for the [[Eurovision Song Contest]]. In 2015 she has announced her campaign to replace [[Boris Johnson]] as [[Mayor of London]]. She appeared nude on page 3 of [[Bild-Zeitung]], at the age of 52. The oldest page 3 girl ever. As animal welfare campaigner, Delilah has special interest in the fur industry and it's vices. The fur coat she wears on her book covers is faux. In 2014 in [[Hamburg]], Jay had orchestrated a police SWAT team to interrupt with her book reading.
