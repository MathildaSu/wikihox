How to Plan|url= http://www.nytimes.com/2013/03/17/travel/booking-hotels-in-asia-the-getaway.html?_r=2&/|publisher= The New York Times |date= 13 March 2013 |accessdate= 31 March 2014 }}{{cite news |title=Hotel booking websites: The Face Off|url= http://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/leisure/2014/02/25/hotel-booking-websites-the-face-off/|publisher= Free Malaysia Today |date= 25 February 2014 |accessdate= 31 March 2014 }} and it offers service in 38 languages. Its inventory includes various types of accommodations, from [[hotels]] and [[Bed and breakfast|b&bs]] to [[Seaside resort|beach resorts]], rest houses and homestays.{{cite news |title=Off the beaten path with Agoda|url= http://gumballmag.com/culture/features/off-the-beaten-path-with-agoda/|publisher= GUMBALLMAG |date= 10 March 2014 |accessdate= 31 March 2014 }} As of 2015, the company has over 2,000 employees worldwide, with offices in [[Bangkok]], [[Tokyo]], [[Sydney]], [[Kuala Lumpur]], [[Hong Kong]], [[Jakarta]], [[Budapest]], [[London]] and [[New York]]. Agoda Company Pte Ltd is part of [[Priceline.com|The Priceline Group]]. ==History== Agoda.com was originally founded in [[Thailand]] in the late 1990s. In 2007, [[Priceline.com|The Priceline Group]] acquired Agoda Company Pte Ltd.{{cite news |title= Priceline acquires Asian online hotel reservation service co Agoda|url= http://www.reuters.com/article/2007/11/08/idUSIN20071108160105PCLN20071108|publisher= Reuters |date= 8 November 2007 |accessdate= 31 March 2014 }} The company launched its [[Mobile application software|mobile app]] in 2011. Supported on [[Android (operating system)|Android]], [[iOS]] and [[Windows Phone]] devices, the app offers full booking functionality in multiple languages.{{cite news |title=Agoda.com launches Android App for easy mobile hotel booking|url= http://www.breakingtravelnews.com/news/article/agoda.com-launches-android-app-for-easy-mobile-hotel-booking/|publisher= Breakingtravelnews.com |date= 10 September 2011 |accessdate= 31 March 2014 }} ==Product== Agoda.com is known for collecting upfront payment for room nights booked on the company’s website or mobile app, an approach known as the Merchant Model in the online travel industry.{{cite news |title=Priceline: Why the online agency model still works for us especially in Asia-Pacific|url= http://www.tnooz.com/article/priceline-why-the-online-agency-model-still-works-for-us-especially-in-asia-pacific/|publisher= Tnooz |date= June 2012 |accessdate= 31 March 2014 }} The company lists over 700,000 properties worldwide on its website.
