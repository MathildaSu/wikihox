xml:space="preserve" bytes="3676"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Renzo Tomellini|timestamp=20151014114949|year=2015|month=October|day=14|substed=yes|help=off}} {{notability|date=October 2015}}'''Renzo Tomellini''', born in 1960, is a chemist by education and currently Head of a Unit at the European Commission.{{cite web|title=European Comission|url=http://europa.eu/whoiswho/public/index.cfm?fuseaction=idea.hierarchy&nodeID=1384568&lang=en}} ==Career== His career has been into materials science and engineering, first in steel making and then in nanotechnology, whose development he pioneered in Europe via and integrated and systemic approach, also creating and managing a dedicated group gathering concerned European Commission services. He is energetic on a transformative agenda for a sustainable and responsible development, also at international level. In 2020, he co-organised the Euro-American workshop "Nanotechnology: Revolutionary Opportunities and Societal Implications", editing the proceedings with [[Mihail Roco]] of the US National Science Foundation.{{cite web|title=NSF|url=http://www.nsf.gov/}} ==Publications, Movies and other Media== His intellectual production includes many articles,{{cite web|title=Google books|url=https://books.google.be/books?id=Rh8IGR4rOA4C&pg=PR28&lpg=PR28&dq=renzo+tomellini+book&source=bl&ots=_daZ_d0X2a&sig=7A0dOAs8V3NC_WsaQccl3NoSFRQ&hl=fr&sa=X&ved=0CFEQ6AEwBmoVChMIvPvU4K2hxwIVQZ0aCh39mARc#v=onepage&q=renzo%20tomellini%20book&f=false}} authoring or editing 15 books,{{cite web|title=Bibliography|url=http://www.amazon.ca/European-Steelmaking-Developments-Perspectives-Reheating/dp/9282745724|website=Amazon}} realising 4 standards on analysis and measurements, launching 2 newsletters and 4 web-pages, and conceiving or contributing to 5 movies{{cite web|title=Nanoinlife|url=http://ec.europa.eu/research/industrial_technologies/nanoinlife_en.html}} on science and research issues. Very popular has been his book{{cite web|title=Movies|url=http://cat.inist.fr/?aModele=afficheN&cpsidt=14732196}} to mark the end of the European Coal and Steel Treaty, The Secret Materials Box, an educational toy box devoted to stimulate interest for science in the younger public,{{cite web|title=Industrial Technologies|url=http://ec.europa.eu/research/industrial_technologies/promotional-material_en.html}} as well as the movies Nano: The Next Dimension and Nanotechnology (available in 20 languages), which received various prizes and recognition internationally.{{cite web|title=Industrial technologies|url=http://ec.europa.eu/research/industrial_technologies/dvds-videos-films_en.html}} Very numerous are his public interventions such as interviews{{cite web|title=European comission|url=http://www.euractiv.com/science/renzo-tomellini-head-nanotechnology-unit-european-commission/article-117415}} and speeches at international{{cite web|title=Nanotecnologia|url=http://www.ghente.org/publicacoes/nanotecnologia_sociedade_meio_ambiente_II.pdf}} or national conferences. ==Academic career== He teaches knowledge management and currently is lecturer in "Management of Enterprises in the European Union" at the University of Bergamo,{{cite web|title=University of Bergamo|url=http://www.unibg.it/}} Faculty of Economics. ==References== {{reflist}}
