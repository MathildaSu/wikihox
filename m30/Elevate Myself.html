(2014) {{Audiosample | Upper caption = Audio sample | Name = "Elevate Myself" | Audio file = Elevate Myself.ogg }} }} "'''Elevate Myself'''" is a song by American [[indie rock]] band [[Grandaddy]]. It was written by [[Jason Lytle]]. The single was released in May 2006, and the song appeared on Grandaddy's album ''[[Just Like the Fambly Cat]]''. It received positive critical reviews. ==Background== "Elevate Myself" was written by vocalist Jason Lytle.[http://allmusic.com/song/elevate-myself-t8779046 "Elevate Myself"]. allmusic.com. Retrieved May 18, 2011. In an interview with ''[[The Sun (United Kingdom)|The Sun]]'', Lytle said: "This song is a homage to why I began doing all this. I discovered making songs and recording them gave me a sort of high. And to this day, despite all of the pressures, I still love it."[http://www.thesun.co.uk/sol/homepage/showbiz/sftw/article47996.ece "It's the Grandaddy Finale"]. thesun.co.uk, May 12, 2006. Retrieved May 18, 2011. ==Release== The single for "Elevate Myself" was released in May 2006, with the b-side "Winners". The song was also included on Grandaddy's final studio album, ''Just Like the Fambly Cat'', which was released during the same month.Strong, Martin Charles.
