xml:space="preserve" bytes="3755"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Prof. Vinzelts|timestamp=20160412054756|year=2016|month=April|day=12|substed=yes}} {{Orphan|date=March 2016}} John Kojo Vinzelts (born December 25, 1986), popularly known as '''Prof. Vinzelts''' is a [[Ghanaian]] Peace Activist, a Human Rights Advocate, a Climate Environmentalist, also Known for Peace Music and Youth advocacy. Currently the Country Director and Pioneer of [[United Nations]] Youth Ghana. He is a multi-talented and multi-Instrumentalist. http://ghana.youth-un.org/prof-vinzelts.html /1 [[Prof. Vinzelts]] is the [[founder]] of SIRCIET (Scientists Innovations Research Centre for Inventions, Engineering and Technology) based in [[Accra, Ghana]]. http://sirciet.org/2 Prof. Vinzelts is known for Peace and Youth Advocacy through his Music Ambassadorial Projects, with both Youth Activism and Advocacy. He is an Online Peaceful Character development Lecturer and crusader at the [[Kofi Annan]] Youth University. http://buzz.newsjs.com/list/prof-vinzelts-and-kalybos-begin-peace-tour-new-york-summit/3 As a Peace through [[Music]] [[Ambassador]], his activities encompass issues such as [[Peace]], [[Human Rights]], [[Women]] and [[Children]], [[education]], [[health]], [[Climate]] and the Youth. http:// http://genun.unausa.org/un/4 Prof. Vinzelts began his [[Peace]] [[Crusade]] through online music, spreading much of his peace messages on [[Facebook]] and [[twitter]], uploading his peace videos and Clips on [[YouTube]], Vimeo and other [[Social media]] as a faster medium of spreading his Peace and [[Human Rights]] messages. He has a tremendous impacts in promoting peace stability among the young people and the Adults who were so touched by the lyrics of his Peace Songs, and has made a lot of Peace musical compositions that convey heart- touching messages of freedom, Unity, Care, Oneness and Harmony. https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCe1jnHRt8CKEaKmbdJzPD8g/5 Prof. Vinzelts' first Vision was to promote Peace through Music, Education, and Entertainment. He is the Founder of the Special Envoys Choir. Among Vinzelts dreams is to continue being the passionate advocate for multi-sector problem-solving, [[Africa]] [[leadership]] on [[global]] issues and the inclusion of Youth at all levels and in all sectors. Spending a large part of his Part Time Music career on Peace Advocacy and [[Human Rights]] [[Activism]] as well as African Youth affairs.
