He is an adjunct scholar of the [[Ludwig von Mises Institute]], USA, and Academic Director of the Instituto Ludwig von Mises Brasil. He maintains the blog Money, Markets and the Business Cycle. Antony Mueller was born and grew up in Germany and holds a doctorate in economics from the University of Erlangen-Nuremberg.
