As per the demand of the story, the filmmaker, casted Aima and her twin sister Aina as female leads. Aima, proficient in both Western and classical dance, happened to dance with dancer-actress [[Mythili Roy]], who suggested her name to the makers of ''[[Jacobinte Swargarajyam]]'' directed by [[Vineeth Sreenivasan]]. They were looking for a girl who looked sixteen and was based in Dubai. She was selected after attending an audition.
