The party describe themselves in their [[manifesto]] as [[Techno-progressivism|techno-progressive]].[http://oneloveparty.eu/manifesto One Love Party Manifesto] The party general secretary is Pax Brown (born Giacomo Brunelli).{{Cite web|url=http://www.huckmagazine.com/perspectives/reportage-2/ankit-love-revolutionary-royal-london-mayoral-candidate-youve-never-heard/|title=The unknown revolutionary royal who wants to be London's next Mayor|last=Andrei|first=Thomas|date=May 5, 2016|website=Huck Magazine|language=en-US|access-date=2016-05-05}} The party was formed from a hostel in Hackney in October 2015, when Ankit Love was homeless.{{Cite web|url=http://www.hackneygazette.co.uk/news/mayoral_candidate_drew_up_manifesto_while_homeless_and_living_in_hackney_hostel_1_4521460|title=Mayoral candidate drew up manifesto while homeless and living in Hackney hostel|last=Bartholomew|first=Emma|website=Hackney Gazette|access-date=2016-05-05}} ==Policies== The party wishes to put in place new legislation to combat [[air pollution]] (which they refer to as Chemical Air Wastes of Mass Destruction) and reduce industrial emissions to zero.[http://ankitlove.com/london2016 Ankit Love Productions - London 2016] They wish to rapidly adopt clean sources of energy such as solar, electric and hydrogen power technologies. They also plan to introduce [[flat-pack]] skyscrapers that can be erected in 19 days - building one million new homes by 2020, and to [[Drug liberalization|legalise]] [[Cannabis (drug)|cannabis]]. Their policies for London include indefinitely delaying the construction of the [[Garden Bridge]], and instead building 6 new bridges in East London based on a proposal by British architect [[Terry Farrell (architect)|Terry Farrell]].{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/OneLovePartyUK/status/723902782575656960|title=One Love Party on Twitter|website=Twitter|access-date=2016-05-05}} They also want
