Netwealth was founded in 2015 and is regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority.https://register.fca.org.uk/ShPo_FirmDetailsPage?id=001b000000qr6bwAAA ==Overview== Netwealth is aimed at individuals with £50,000 or more to invest over the medium to long term.http://citywire.co.uk/wealth-manager/news/city-grandees-back-ex-goldman-partners-online-wealth-firm/a912815Netwealth offers investing through ISA, JISA, SIPP and General accounts. Clients can invest into goal-based portfolios that can be customised based on their attitude to risk. Clients create, monitor and review their portfolios online. Netwealth also has an advisory arm for those who wish to discuss their financial affairs with a financial advisor.http://citywire.co.uk/wealth-manager/news/profile-the-boutique-the-blue-chips-are-backing/a915513 Netwealth portfolios are predominantly made up of investments in Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs), open-ended investment companies (OEICs) and Unit Trusts, and direct holdings in Gilts (UK Government bonds). Netwealth is fully independent and is therefore not tied to any specific provider. The company has raised just over £6.5m of investment, understood to be one of the largest angel rounds in the UK in the last five years.http://www.investmentweek.co.uk/investment-week/news-analysis/2459011/bonham-carter-backs-launch-of-online-discretionary-manager-netwealth ==History== Netwealth was founded in 2015 by former Goldman Sachs partner Charlotte Ransom and Thomas Salter a former managing director at JPMorgan.http://www.portfolio-adviser.com/news/1029353/bonham-carter-backed-netwealth-pins-hopes-technological-edge Netwealth is now based on Hyde Park Corner in London with registered offices in Reading.https://beta.companieshouse.gov.uk/company/09493628 ==Key Personnel== Charlotte Ransom (Founder and CEO), Thomas Salter (Founder and COO), [[Gerard Lyons]] (Chief Economic Strategist), Iain Barnes (Head of Portfolio Management) and Rachel Willox (Head of Operations).http://citywire.co.uk/new-model-adviser/news/boris-johnsons-economic-adviser-joins-robo-advice-firm/a912991 ==References== {{reflist}} * * * *
