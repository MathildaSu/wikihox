Also known for his writing, rap music, and deck-design, Chapin has made five [[Magic: The Gathering Pro Tour|Pro Tour]] top eights spanning three decades, and four [[Grand Prix (Magic: The Gathering)|Grand Prix]] top eights. In 2012 Chapin was voted into the [[Magic: The Gathering Hall of Fame]]. His induction took place during Pro Tour Return to Ravnica in October 2012. == Books == Chapin wrote two books about Magic: The Gathering titled ''Next Level Magic'' and ''Next Level Deckbuilding''.
