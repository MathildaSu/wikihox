She is the youngest politician in the United States and she is commonly known in Greece for her views on the Coalition of the Radical Left ([[Syriza]]) published on the [[Young Communist League, USA]],{{cite web|url=http://www.yclusa.org/coalition-radical-left-syriza/|title=COALITION OF THE RADICAL LEFT (SYRIZA)|first=Emily|last=Dykes|publisher=''[http://www.yclusa.org/ Young Communist League, USA]''|date=2015-01-30|accessdate=2015-05-29}} She is also commonly known in the [[Kingdom of Hawaii]] for strongly opposing the United States' [http://www.globalresearch.ca/hawaii-120-years-of-us-occupation-militarism-and-americas-pacific-century/5318834 illegal occupation of Hawaii] published on [[5th World news]],{{cite web|url=http://fifthworldnews.blogspot.com/2015_04_01_archive.html|title=Native Americans across the United States have been helping the Hawaiians in regards to the illegal occupation by the United States|first=Dawnstar |last=INC|publisher=''[http://fifthworldnews.blogspot.com/ 5th World news]''|date=2015-04-25|accessdate=2015-05-29}} In her article published on [[Facebook]],{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/notes/emily-nashoba-dykes/hitler-was-a-christian-the-holocaust-was-brought-on-by-christian-fundamentalism/1016413468376480|title=Hitler was a Christian. The Holocaust was brought on by Christian fundamentalism!|first=Emily|last=Dykes|publisher=''[[[https://www.facebook.com/RevolutionaryEmily Emily Nashoba Dykes' Facebook Page]]]''|date=2015-05-21|accessdate=2015-05-29}} Emily laid out her analysis of [[Adolf Hitler]] and warned "history is right now being mutilated by the large number of Christians who lie to have us accept that the Holocaust was not a Christian deed." In another article she published on [[Facebook]],{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/notes/emily-nashoba-dykes/why-should-we-choose-communism-and-socialism-over-capitalism/1016403238377503|title=Why should we choose communism and socialism over capitalism? |first=Emily|last=Dykes|publisher=''[[[https://www.facebook.com/RevolutionaryEmily Emily Nashoba Dykes' Facebook Page]]]''|date=2015-05-21|accessdate=2015-05-29}} Emily laid out her plans for the future of the United States, she has stated plans from guaranteed work for everybody, Social Security for all, and to a public bank in every state. She says "communism is our vision for America's future. It is a dream we are winning more individuals to in light of the fact that it is the consistent - truly an incredible - swap for private enterprise. What's more, on the grounds that it is the following unavoidable venture up the step of human development." Emily has also wrote an article about the Soviet Union she published on [[Facebook]],{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/notes/emily-nashoba-dykes/seven-myths-about-the-soviet-union/1019298278087999|title=Seven myths about the Soviet Union |first=Emily|last=Dykes|publisher=''[[[https://www.facebook.com/RevolutionaryEmily Emily Nashoba Dykes' Facebook Page]]]''|date=2015-05-21|accessdate=2015-05-29}} Emily rebukes "absolute stander" myths against the Soviet Union.
