[[Interactive entertainment]] |area_served = Global | homepage = [http://www.37.com// www.37.com] }} '''37Games''' ({{zh|c=37游戏}} officially Sanqi Interactive Entertainment {{zh|c=三七互娱}}) is a browser and mobile game developer and publisher. Its gaming services cover mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, Vietnam, South Korea, Thailand, and other Southeast Asia countries such as the Philippines, Malaysia, and Singapore.
