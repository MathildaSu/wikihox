In 2014 his Motion photography ([[Graphics Interchange Format]] GIFs - short films) was shortlisted by the [[Saatchi Gallery]] and [[Google+]] for The Motion Photography Prize.[http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2567931/Spectacular-moving-images-compete-win-inaugural-Motion-Photography-Prize-honoring-gifs.html Motion Photography Prize], Saatchi Gallery. Panel of judges: film director [[Baz Luhrmann]], artists [[Shezad Dawood]], [[Tracey Emin]], [[Cindy Sherman]] and [[Saatchi Gallery]] CEO Nigel Hurst.[http://www.complex.com/art-design/2014/02/gifs-saatchi-gallery-google-motion-photography-prize "Mesmerizing GIFs From Saatchi Gallery and Google+'s Motion Photography Prize"], Complex.com, 22 February 2014.
