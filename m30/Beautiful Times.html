(2014) }}}} '''Beautiful Times''' is a song by American [[electronica]] project [[Owl City]] feautirng American violinist [[Lindsey Stirling]]. Is the first [[single (music)|single]] from Owl City's fourth [[extended play]], ''[[Ultraviolet (EP)|Ultraviolet]]''. The song was written by [[Adam Young]]. ==Background and release== From March 31 to April 2, 2014, Young revealed the album artwork for a single, "Beautiful Times", through his [[Instagram]] account.{{cite web|url=http://instagram.com/p/mQjkvbgWRv/ |title=Piece five of the April single album artwork on Instagram |publisher=Instagram |accessdate=April 3, 2014}}{{cite web|url=http://instagram.com/p/mQJU3IgWQE/ |title=Piece four of the April single album artwork on Instagram |publisher=Instagram |accessdate=April 3, 2014}} Later, Young published a letter in Owl City's official website talking about [[Ocean Eyes]] fifth anniversary and his intends to release a steady "series of EPs" in 2014 rather than one larger recording.{{cite web|url=http://www.freqazoid.com/electronic/2014/4/8/owl-city-announces-new-eps-for-2014|title=Owl City 2014 EPs|accessdate=April 8, 2014}} On June 5, 2014, Young announced that the first extended play would be titled ''[[Ultraviolet (EP)|Ultraviolet]]''.
