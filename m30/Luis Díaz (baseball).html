xml:space="preserve" bytes="1173"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Luis Díaz (baseball)|timestamp=20170417122530|year=2017|month=April|day=17|substed=yes|help=off}} '''Luis Felipe Diaz Medina''' (1971 - February 27, 2014) was a baseball pitcher who played for the [[Metropolitanos]] in the [[Cuban National Series]] for 16 seasons, from 1990-1991 to 2005-2006. He was 86-103 with a 4.93 ERA in 278 games (231 starts). He also had 50 complete games, 15 shutouts, 5 saves, 720 strikeouts, 655 walks and 1,444 innings pitched. He later coached for the [[Industriales]]. He was born in [[Havana]], [[Cuba]].
