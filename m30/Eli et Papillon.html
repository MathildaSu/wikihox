xml:space="preserve" bytes="1512"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Eli et Papillon|timestamp=20170218012345|year=2017|month=February|day=18|substed=yes|help=off}} {{Orphan|date=March 2016}} '''Eli et Papillon''' are a Canadian [[Indie pop music|indie pop]] band based in [[Montreal]], [[Quebec]]. The band consists of members Élise Larouche and Marc Papillon-Ferland. Their music is in French and tends to be [[Pop music|pop]] with [[Folk music|folk]] elements.
