xml:space="preserve" bytes="6003"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=LawnStarter|timestamp=20160319172505|year=2016|month=March|day=19|substed=yes|help=off}} {{refimprove|date=March 2016}} '''LawnStarter''' is an Austin-based [[Startup company|startup]] that allows homeowners to find, book and pay for [[lawn care]] from local pros. LawnStarter currently has locations and operates across several major US cities, including Austin, Houston, Dallas, Fort Worth, San Antonio, Jacksonville, Orlando, Washington DC and Charlotte. The company's online and mobile applications enable the booking and paying for of lawn care, with access to services and payments on-the-go. == Investment == In January 2015, LawnStarter raised an initial [[Seed money|seed round]] of $1 million from [[Gary Vaynerchuk]] (via Vayner RSE), Rob Taylor, Cotter Cunningham, Rony Kahan, and others.{{Cite web|url=http://venturebeat.com/2015/01/14/techstars-grad-lawnstarter-bags-1m-from-gary-vaynerchuk-others/|title=Techstars grad LawnStarter bags $1M from Gary Vaynerchuk, others|website=VentureBeat|access-date=2016-03-17}} In June of 2015, the company received $6 million in [[Series A round|Series A]] funding from Binary Capital. {{Cite web|url=http://social.techcrunch.com/2015/06/02/lawnstarter-series-a/|title=LawnStarter Raises $6M To Become Your “Lawn Care Concierge”|last=Ha|first=Anthony|website=TechCrunch|access-date=2016-03-17}} To date, the company has received over $7 million in funding. == Applications == LawnStarter offers two online apps, one for consumers and one for lawn care professionals. Both apps can be accessed through a browser from nearly any internet connected device or via their respective [[iOS]] and [[Android (operating system)|Android]] mobile app. === Consumer applications === The consumer app allows homeowners to fully manage their account and services from an iOS{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/lawnstarter/id968630246?mt=8|title=LawnStarter on the App Store|website=App Store|access-date=2016-03-18}} or Android{{Cite web|url=https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lawnstarter.lawnstarter&hl=en|title=LawnStarter - Android Apps on Google Play|website=play.google.com|access-date=2016-03-18}} device. === Provider applications === The provider app allows lawn care professionals to accept and manage their jobs from their iOS{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/lawnstarter-for-providers/id963343083?mt=8|title=LawnStarter for Providers on the App Store|website=App Store|access-date=2016-03-18}} or Android{{Cite web|url=https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lawnstarter.pros&hl=en|title=LawnStarter for Providers - Android Apps on Google Play|website=play.google.com|access-date=2016-03-18}} device. The app has automatic billing which allows pros to focus on handling their jobs without worrying about waiting around for payment from customers. == Quality of life report == In 2015, LawnStarter produced a quality of life report that ranked states and cities by different ranking factors including Material Well Being (GDP Per Capita), Health (Life Expectancy in Years), Job Security (Unemployment Rate), Family Life (Divorce Rate), Climate Geography (Location [Assumed Weather]), and Gender Equality (Male to Female Income Ratio).{{Cite web|url=https://www.lawnstarter.com/quality-of-life|title=LawnStarter's 2015 Quality of Life Report for States|website=www.lawnstarter.com|access-date=2016-03-18}} == In the media == In January 2016, Built In Austin ranked LawnStarter as one of the top 50 startups to watch in Austin.{{Cite web|url=http://www.builtinaustin.com/2016/01/25/50-austin-startups-watch-2016|title=50 Austin startups to watch in 2016|website=Built In Austin|access-date=2016-03-18}} ==References== {{reflist|30em}} ==Further reading== * {{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/caroline-pugh/lawncare-made-easy-lawnst_b_7577478.html|title=Lawncare Made Easy, Lawnstarter Raises $6 Million Series A|date=15 June 2015|work=The Huffington Post}} * {{cite web|url=http://techcrunch.com/2015/06/02/lawnstarter-series-a/|title=LawnStarter Raises $6M To Become Your “Lawn Care Concierge”|date=2 June 2015|publisher=AOL|work=TechCrunch}} * {{cite web|url=http://www.bizjournals.com/austin/blog/techflash/2015/06/austin-online-landscapingmarketplace-startup.html|title=Austin online marketplace operator LawnStarter raises funding -
