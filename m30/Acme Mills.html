Founded in 1917, it is part of the Acme Group of companies. Activities of the company include cutting, sewing, dyeing, and applying textile finishing to fabrics. ==History== Acme Mills was founded in 1917 as a mill to manufacture [[Batting (material)|cotton batting]] for use in the [[automotive industry]] for seat cushions.
