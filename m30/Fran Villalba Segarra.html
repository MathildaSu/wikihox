Marketing Manager of HostMania | website = {{URL|https://www.fran.global}} }} '''Francisco Leopoldo''' "'''Fran'''" '''Villalba Segarra''' (born April 28, 1997) is a [[Spanish]] [[entrepreneur]]. At the age of 13, Fran started learning [[programming]]. It was then when he started with his first online projects. At the age of 18 he joined Hostinger International. He now works there as a Business Development Assistant. In addition, he recently joined HostMania, a Hostinger brand, where he works as the Marketing Manager{{cite web|title=ComputerHoy.com - HostMania |url=http://computerhoy.com/noticias/internet/branded--hostmania-forma-mas-facil-crear-almacenar-tu-pagina-web-45830|accessdate=May 2, 2016}}. As Fran stated in multiple interviews{{cite web|title=Levante TV - HostMania|url=http://videotecahtml5.es/levantetv/media/valencia-en-directe-hostmania-30-de-marzo|accessdate=May 2, 2016}}, he does those things he likes most. He is also enrolled in a BSc in International Business Administration in [[Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University]]. Fran also plans to cooperate in the research of fighting aging and immortality.
