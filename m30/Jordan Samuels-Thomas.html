He was selected by the [[Atlanta Thrashers]] in the 7th round (203rd overall) of the [[2009 NHL Entry Draft]]. ==Playing career== Samuels-Thomas played junior hockey with the [[Waterloo Black Hawks]] of the [[United States Hockey League]] (USHL) before atteneding [[Bowling Green State University]] to play the [[2009–10 NCAA Division I men's ice hockey season|2009–10]] and [[2010–11 NCAA Division I men's ice hockey season|2010–11]] seasons with the [[Bowling Green Falcons men's ice hockey|Bowling Green Falcons]].
