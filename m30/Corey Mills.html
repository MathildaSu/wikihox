[[Record Producer]] | years_active = 2014-present | module = {{Infobox musical artist|embed=yes | background = solo_singer | genre = {{flat list| *[[Christian Hip Hop|Christian Hip Hop/Rap]] *[[Gospel]] }} | instrument = | label = [[Independent record label]] | website = {{URL|seemrecords.com}} }} }} '''Corey Mills''' (Born 26 February 1989) is an independent Christian [[Gospel]] singer, songwriter and [[Record Producer|record producer]]. He is best known as a Christian [[Christian hip hop|Rapper]].{{cite web|title=Corey Mills|url=http://www.reverbnation.com/coreymills|website=reverbnation.com|accessdate=13 February 2016}} Brought up in [[Helena, Arkansas]], he moved to [[Memphis Tennessee]] to pursue his music career. Motivated by his Love for ''[[Jesus|Jesus Christ]]'', he started trying different things with Christian Rap and in 2009 released his first unofficial album- ''inhale the word of God''. He owns and manages inhaletheword.com (2010 – present).{{cite web|title=Contact us|url=http://inhaletheword.com/invitations.php|website=Inhaletheword.com|publisher=Corey Mills|accessdate=12 February 2016}} Mills has released one full album, one EP and four singles.{{cite web|title=Corey Mills|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/corey-mills/id648607890|website=itunes.apple.com|publisher=itunes|accessdate=12 February 2016}}{{cite web|title=Albums Corey Mills Dicography|url=http://muzoic.net/artist/corey-mills|website=muzoic.net|accessdate=12 February 2016}} He is the founder and President of See-M Records{{cite web|title=About See-M Records|url=http://seemrecords.com/|website=Seemrecords.com|accessdate=12 February 2016}} an entertainment agency and record name formed in Gulf Shores, Alabama in July 2015. ==Early life== Mills was born by ''Carla Mills'' and ''Carlos Williams''. The family lived in [[Helena, Arkansas]] for about twelve years. At age eight, Mills started composing [[poetry]] and developed his ability as he grew. Mills was consistently [[nominated]] for an award as the best poet of his age in [[United states|America]]. {{cite web|title=Biography|url=http://seemrecords.com/Corey-Mills.php|website=seemrecords.com|accessdate=13 February 2016}} {{citation needed|date=February 2016}}. Amid his [[adolescent]] years, he was offered the opportunity to further develop his writing skills in [[England]], with the prospect of receiving a publishing contract. In any case, he turned down the offer. Mills currently attends [[Liberty University]] in [[Lynchburg, Virginia]]. ==Musical career== Mills started trying different things with rap when he moved to [[Tunica, Mississippi]], after 2004. His associates influenced him to give music a shot after perusing some of his work, and he did. At age eighteen, Mills moved to [[Memphis, Tennessee]].{{cite web|title=Corey Mills from West Helena|url=http://www.artisttrove.com/artist/283794971778643/Corey+Mills|website=artisttrove.com|accessdate=13 February 2016}} ===2009 to Present=== In 2009, Corey Mills began with a project he knew little about - Christian Rap. He worked together with his friend Trentiss Wade, {{citation needed|date=February 2016}} who produced [[instrumental|instrumentals]] for him. One month after finishing the project, Mills released his first unofficial album -'’Inhale the Word of God'’. As time passed, his music spread through many radio stations in the region. Individuals started to connect to his music, and requested for more. During this period, he received a number of offers to go on tour and additionally be part of a label. These offers were rejected by him. He later cited the reason as not fully understanding his gift at the time.{{cite web|title=Biography|url=http://seemrecords.com/Corey-Mills.php|website=seemrecords.com|accessdate=13 February 2016}} Mills later released his first mix-tape 'The Beginning Mix-tape volume 1’. This was an unofficial release. After releasing the mix-tape, Corey Mills started another project ‘The Blood of Jesus Cleanses’. Although he made a couple of songs for the album recorded by Nothing-But-Love Records in Tunica, Mississippi, the project was not completed.{{cite web|title=Biography|url=http://www.cdbaby.com/Artist/CoreyMills|website=cdbaby.com|accessdate=13 February 2016}} In 2012, Mills moved to [[Orange Beach, Alabama]] where he started composing [[contemporary]] and [[Traditional]]/Soul Gospel. In 2013, he moved to [[Pensacola, Florida]]. In [[Florida]], Mills began putting pen to paper and produced two additional singles ‘'Proposal'’- released on March 24, 2014 - and '’You Will Know’'- released on April 8, 2014. He also released an Extended Play album titled 'Proposal' (November 20, 2014); the instrumental was produced by Platinum Seller Beats. Mills recorded Proposal EP with a mobile phone and software application. His objective; he felt inspired to encourage others with similar gifts and talent to bless the Lord and others with their fruit.{{cite web|title=Biography|url=http://seemrecords.com/Corey-Mills.php|website=seemrecords.com|accessdate=13 February 2016}} In 2015 Mills moved to [[Gulf Shores, Alabama]]. He released a full album in September 2015 ''(Redemption)''.{{cite web|title=Redemption by Corey Mills|url=https://www.reverbnation.com/coreymills/album/106014-redemption|website=reverbnation.com|accessdate=13 February 2016}} Singles released by Mills in 2015 are; ''He is a shedding light'' and ''Bounce'' {{cite web|title=Bounce|url=https://www.microsoft.com/en-au/store/music/album/corey-mills/bounce-single/8d6kgwzztdw3|website=microsoft.com|accessdate=13 February 2016}} Mills is said to be working on a second full album expected to be released in 2016.{{cite web|title=Biography|url=http://seemrecords.com/Corey-Mills.php|website=seemrecords.com|accessdate=13 February 2016}} ==Tour== In 2015, Corey Mills performed as part of the ''Get money stop Hatin'' tour that made stops in Pensacola and Birmingham.
