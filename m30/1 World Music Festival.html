xml:space="preserve" bytes="5064"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=1 World Music Festival|timestamp=20140704083744|year=2014|month=July|day=4|substed=yes|help=off}} {{ infobox music festival | name = 1 World Music Festival | logo = [[File:1 World Music Festival Logo.jpg|150px]] | logo_caption = Official logo | location = [[Marina Barrage]], [[Singapore]] (main location) | founded = 2013, [[Singapore]] | founders = Retfar Entertainment | dates = September 20th & 21st, 2013 | genre = [[Hip Hop music|Hip Hop]], [[Electronic dance music|EDM]], [[House music|House]], [[Electro house|Electro House]], [[Progressive house|Progressive House]], [[Techno]], [[Trance music|Trance]], [[Dubstep]], [[Drum and bass|Drum and Bass]] | website = [http://1worldmusicfestival.com Official website] }} The '''1 World Music Festival'''{{cite web |url=http://1worldmusicfestival.com |title=1 World Music Festival |publisher=Retfar Entertainment Pte Ltd |accessdate=15 April 2013|author=Retfar Singapore |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6Hg2iwf9T|archivedate=2013-06-26|deadurl=no}} was a music event slated to coincide with the [[Formula One]] race weekend, 20th and 21 September 2013. The event was to be held at the [[Marina Barrage]] in [[Singapore]]. The [[music festival]] showcases international celebrity talent as well as local Singaporean and regional acts. According to Her World Plus,{{cite web |url=http://www.herworldplus.com/lifestyle/stuff-do/iggy-azalea-headline-1-world-music-festival |title=Iggy Azalea to headline 1 World Music Festival |publisher=Her World Plus |accessdate=13 June 2013|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6Hg2q6M9d|archivedate=2013-06-26|deadurl=no}} top Australian artiste [[Iggy Azalea]] is scheduled to headline the festival. The lineup also includes [[Snoop Dogg]], [[Moby]], [[Tinie Tempah]], [[Far East Movement]], [[Orbital (band)|Orbital]], [[Infected Mushroom]], Kim Kat, [[Miguel Migs]], Amadeus, [[The Cataracs]], Randy Seidman, [[Taboo (rapper)|Taboo]] (of [[Black Eyed Peas]]), [[Sub Focus]] + ID, [[Pendulum (band)|Pendulum]] + Verse and [[Cosmic Gate]], [[Cobra Starship]] and Malaysian songstress [[Yuna (singer)|Yuna]].{{cite web |url=http://www.mtvasia.com/news/1-world-music-festival-hosted-in-sg-featuring-epic-lineup/ |title=NEWS : 1 WORLD MUSIC FESTIVAL HOSTED IN SINGAPORE FEATURING EPIC LINEUP |publisher=MTV |accessdate=5 June 2013|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6Hg2pFyNq|archivedate=2013-06-26|deadurl=no}}{{cite web |url=http://msn.juiceonline.com/1-world-music-festival-welp-a-reason-to-cross-the-causeway/ |title=1 World Music Festival: Welp, A Reason to Cross the Causeway! |publisher=MSN |accessdate=20 June 2013|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6Hg2lrbIN|archivedate=2013-06-26|deadurl=no}}{{cite web |url=http://news.asiaone.com/News/Latest%2BNews/Showbiz/Story/A1Story20130619-430778.html |title=M'sian singer Yuna gave up legal career to tap musical talent |publisher=AsiaOne |accessdate=20 June 2013}} Local and regional acts include Inquizitive, Kevin Lester, Nicole Chen, Amy van de Beuken, Bangkok Invaders, Aldrin, Dodgy Yamamoto, Akshai Sarin, Shine5, Sezairi Sezali, Roxy June, DJ Benz, Stereo Vixens, Tenashar, Mera Jin, Jon White, MadebyTommy and Andrew T. With its tagline of “Turn Me On”, the 1 World Music Festival is intended as a late-night series of music performances. It is also envisioned as a platform to push the envelope on entertainment. It is intended to use state-of-the-art entertainment technology to back up its performers. The festival is organised by Retfar Entertainment Pte Ltd,{{cite web |url=http://retfar.sg |title=Retfar Entertainment Singapore |publisher=Retfar Entertainment Pte Ltd |accessdate=15 April 2013}} a sister company to Retfar Entertainment Pty Ltd of Australia. On 18 September 2013, it was announced that the event would be cancelled.[http://news.asiaone.com/news/showbiz/1-world-music-festival-cancelled 1 World Music Festival cancelled]. AsiaOne.
