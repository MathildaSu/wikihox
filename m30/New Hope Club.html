Their debut EP, ''Welcome to the Club'', was released on [[Steady Records]]/[[Hollywood Records]] on May 5, 2017. ==History== George Smith and Blake Richardson formed New Hope Club in October 2015.Nicola Craig, [http://celebmix.com/artist-101-new-hope-club/ "Artist 101: New Hope Club,"] Celebmix, February 2017. As a member of the boy band [[Stereo Kicks]],Lewis Corner, [http://www.digitalspy.com/music/news/a776609/the-vamps-have-signed-new-band-new-hope-club-to-their-record-label/ "The Vamps have signed new band New Hope Club to their record label,"] Digital Spy, December 8, 2015. Reece Bibby appeared on the eleventh season of ''[[The X Factor (UK series 11)|The X Factor]]'' in the UK in 2014, finishing fifth.Tania Willis, [http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2855237/Stereo-Kicks-sent-home-public-losing-Lauren-Platt-sing-X-Factor-results-show.html "Stereo Kicks are the latest act to be voted off X Factor… after losing out to Lauren Platt in sing-off,"] ''[[Daily Mail]]'', November 30, 2014. Stereo Kicks broke up in July 2015; in November 2015, Bibby joined New Hope Club.Kristine Hope Kowlaski, [http://www.twistmagazine.com/posts/reece-bibby-starts-a-new-band-after-stereo-kicks-breakup-77367 "Take a Listen: Reece Bibby Starts a New Band After Stereo Kicks' Breakup,"] ''[[Twist (magazine)|Twist]]'', November 13, 2015.Brie Hiramine, [http://www.j-14.com/posts/stereo-kicks-reece-bibby-starts-new-hope-club-band-77410 "'X Factor' Boy Band Member Starts Another Group After Leaving His Old Band,"] ''[[J-14 (magazine)|J-14]]'', November 13, 2015. Bibby, Smith and Richardson are all originally from [[Northern England]]."Meet Hollywood Record's British Band New Hope Club," ''[[Just Jared Jr.]]'', May 7, 2017.Shawna Hudson, [https://www.sweetyhigh.com/read/new-hope-club-mcm-050817 "Find Out All About British Boy Band New Hope Club,"] Sweety High, May 8, 2017. New Hope Club's first recording was a cover of "[[Wake Up (The Vamps song)|Wake Up]]" by [[The Vamps (British band)|The Vamps]] in October 2015.[http://essentiallypop.com/epop/2017/04/six-quick-questions-for-new-hope-club/ "Six Quick Questions For New Hope Club,"] essentiallypop.com, April 25, 2017. In December 2015, The Vamps signed the band to their label [[Steady Records]], a subsidiary of [[Virgin EMI Records]]. They were the second band signed to the label, after [[The Tide (band)|The Tide]]. New Hope Club's debut 4-song EP ''Welcome to the Club'' was released on [[Hollywood Records]] via [[Steady Records]] on May 5, 2017.[https://jammerzine.com/new-hope-club-announce-debut-ep/ "New Hope Club Announce Debut EP,"] Jammerzine, May 3, 2017. The video for "Perfume" was released in January 2017, and the video for "Fixed" was released with their EP. The band spent five weeks on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' Next Big Sound chart, peaking at number 10 on June 10, 2017.[http://www.billboard.com/artist/7786268/new-hope-club/chart New Hope Club Chart History,] ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]''. Accessed June 8, 2017. ==Band members== * Blake Richardson - Vocals, guitar * George Smith - Vocals, guitar * Reece Bibby - Vocals, bass, drums, guitar ==Touring== New Hope Club opened for [[The Vamps (British band)|The Vamps]] on their 2016 UK tour, and for their tour of the UK and Ireland in the spring of 2017.Hayley Smith, [http://www.unitedbypop.com/interviews/new-hope-club-uk-ireland-tour/ "Interview: New Hope Club Hit The Road With The Vamps For UK Arena Tour,"] United By Pop, April 27, 2017. They regularly performed "[[Oh Cecilia (Breaking My Heart)]]" with The Vamps during that tour. They also opened for [[Martina Stoessel|Tini Stoessel]] on her 2017 Got Me Started Tour in Germany.[http://www.juiceorcider.com/2017/03/17/new-hope-club-tour-in-germany-for-the-first-time/ "New Hope Club to Tour in Germany for the First Time,"] Juice or Cider, March 17, 2017. They have also opened for [[Sabrina Carpenter]] on her North American summer 2017 De-Tour,[http://www.juiceorcider.com/2017/04/27/new-hope-club-will-tour-america-with-sabrina-carpenter-this-summer/ "New Hope Club Will Tour America With Sabrina Carpenter This Summer,"] Juice or Cider, April 27, 2017. and will open yet again for The Vamps on their fall 2017 tour in Australia.[http://themusic.com.au/news/all/2017/06/02/the-vamps-are-bringing-their-huge-world-tour-to-aus-this-year/ "The Vamps Are Bringing Their Huge World Tour To Aus This Year,"] themusic.com.au, June 2, 2017.
