Williams''' is a [[comic book]] illustrator, [[conceptual art]]ist, painter and sculptor, living in [[Adelaide]], [[South Australia]]. Beginning his professional art career in 1994, David has worked for [[Marvel Comics]] and [[DC Comics]] on world renowned titles such as [[Wolverine (comic book)|Wolverine]], [[Catwoman]], [[Batman: Shadow of the Bat]], [[R.E.B.E.L.S.]], with [[Michal Dutkiewicz]],and on [[The Phantom]] with [[Glenn Lumsden]] and [[David de Vries]]. More recently, he collaborated with writer and musician, Baron Misuraca ([[Sheer Terror]]), on the gothic rock horror comic, In Flesh and Spirit,{{cite web|title=The making of In Flesh and Spirit|url=http://www.comicmonsters.com/section-article-225-the_making_of_in_flesh_and_spirit_-_by__baron_misuraca.html|publisher=Comic Monsters|accessdate=9 May 2014}} and on, The Wolfâ€™s Empire, in collaboration with [[Claudia Christian]] ([[Babylon 5]]). He is currently a concept artist and illustrator for the film and television production company, [[Angel Phoenix Media]], and works as a graphic designer and illustrator for the [[music education]] publisher, [[LearnToPlayMusic.com]]. David is one half of the creative team behind the pioneering and acclaimed, The Legend of Spacelord Mo Fo,{{cite web|title=Review: The Legend of SpaceLord Mo Fo|url=http://www.sub-cultured.com/review-the-legend-of-spacelord-mo-fo/|publisher=Sub-Cultured|accessdate=9 May 2014}} digital comic series; a serialized, full-color, comic, written by Pat McNamara and illustrated by David G.
