It offers live, instructor-led courses that cater mainly to working professionals who want to enhance their skills. Founded in mid-2011 and headquartered in [[Bangalore]], India, the company is privately owned and operated by Brain4ce Education Ltd. == History == Lovleen Bhatia, an [[IIT-BHU]] alumni exited his earlier venture to start Edureka in mid-2011. Lovleen partnered with Kapil Tyagi, an [[IIT Bombay]] alumni, to co-found this venture.{{cite web|title=ET - How Lovleen Bhatia turned Edureka into a Rs 30 crore venture in 4 years|url=http://articles.economictimes.indiatimes.com/2014-06-30/news/50974484_1_moocs-college-students-lovleen-bhatia|website=Economic times|publisher=Economic times}} Bhatia and Tyagi started off the company from a small office in Noida, with just 4 employees and an investment of Rs.1.5 crore.{{cite news|title=Bangalore mirror|url=http://www.bangaloremirror.com/columns/work/An-online-education-forum/articleshow/34650334.cms|accessdate=30 June 2015|agency=Bangalore Mirror}} The company launched its first pilot project to the final year students at ABES Engineering College in Ghaziabad, in mid-2011. A two-month training program was organized in partnership with a few Bangalore-based software companies and focused on mobile app development courses. Working professionals from the industry were its first instructors. Over the next eight months, Edureka added 30 new instructors from the industry and trained around 1,200 students.{{cite news|title=Economic times article|url=http://articles.economictimes.indiatimes.com/2014-06-30/news/50974484_1_moocs-college-students-lovleen-bhatia|accessdate=30 June 2015|agency=Bennet Coleman}} Within a year Edureka had shifted its complete education online, and in June 2012, Edureka had 15,000 students from across the globe registered for their online Android development course.{{Citation needed|date = November 2015}} Edureka got noticed when a 10-year-old created a mobile app in June 2013 using Edureka platform.{{cite news|title=Project developed by Priyansh Soni|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkOujTXKmsM}} Edureka shifted its base from Noida to Bengaluru in 2012 where they set up a small office in Indiranagar. Further in 2013, they expanded their office and relocated to Sigma Tech Park in Whitefield, Bengaluru.{{cite news|title=Bangalore mirror|url=http://www.bangaloremirror.com/columns/work/An-online-education-forum/articleshow/34650334.cms|accessdate=30 June 2015|agency=Bangalore Mirror}} == Awards == * In November 2014, Edureka was ranked number 1 in Deloitteâ€™s Technology Fast 50 India 2014{{cite web|title=Edureka - Deloitte's Technology Fast 50|url=http://www2.deloitte.com/in/en/pages/technology-media-and-telecommunications/articles/deloitte-tech-fast-50-2014.html|website=Deloitte|publisher=Deloitte|accessdate=8 June 2015}}{{cite news|title=TOI - Edureka ranked number 1 in Deloitte's Technology fast 50|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/tech/tech-news/Edureka-is-top-among-Deloitte's-Technology-Fast-50-India-2014-company/articleshow/45122748.cms|accessdate=8 June 2015|agency=Bennett, Coleman and Company Limited|issue=12th November 2014|publisher=Times of India}} * In June 2015, Business Today named Edureka as one of the Coolest Startups of 2015{{cite news|title=Business Today - Coolest Startups 2015|url=http://businesstoday.intoday.in/story/business-today-coolest-start-ups-2015-edureka-success-strategy/1/219947.html|accessdate=8 June 2015|issue=21st November 2015|publisher=Business Today}} == References == {{Reflist|30em}} [[Category:Companies based in Bangalore]] [[Category:Online companies]]
