His mother, Ines Simbe, is a [[subsistence farmer|subsistent farmer]], and his father, Guido Gudo was a train conductor. He has an older brother, Aboobakar Gudo and a younger sister, Delfina "Tracy" Gudo. He moved from Dondo, Sofala to Vanduzi - [[Manica Province|Manica]] in 1996 with his mother, leaving his bother with his father due to his parents’ separation.
