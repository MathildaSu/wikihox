That year the company had four offices, two in Canada and two in India, and employed 260 people.{{cite web |url=http://www.canadianunderwriter.ca/news/travel-insurance-is-the-best-way-to-handle-h1n1-related-trip-cancellations-airlines-say/1000346215/?&er=NA |title=Travel insurance is the best way to handle H1N1-related trip cancellations, airlines say |publisher=Canadian Underwriter |accessdate=July 25, 2014}} In 2010 FlightNetwork became the online travel search engine for MSN.com.{{cite web |url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-241494066.html |title=Canadian travel search results on MSN to be powered by FlightNetwork.com |publisher=M2 Best Books |date=November 8, 2010}} The company has an unlimited price drop protection, which refunds to the purchaser the difference between the initial purchase price of travel services and any lower price that may arise before the time of use. ==Studies== The company has also released studies regarding international travel.
