It received its insurance broking license from [[Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority]] (IRDA) in early 2014.{{cite web|last1=Das|first1=Pulomi|title=Varun Dua, CEO and co-founder of online insurance portal Coverfox says the finance startup space holds humongous potential|url=http://www.businessinsider.in/Varun-Dua-CEO-and-co-founder-of-online-insurance-portal-Coverfox-says-the-finance-startup-space-holds-humongous-potential/articleshow/50269160.cms|website=businessinsider.in|accessdate=2016-08-28}} == Background and Funding == Coverfox was founded by Devendra Rane and Varun Dua, in 2013.{{cite web|last1=Ganguly|first1=Paloma|title=A young startup is changing the rules of the game in India’s insurance market|url=https://www.techinasia.com/young-portal-changing-rules-game-indias-insurance-market|website=techinasia.com|accessdate=2016-08-26}} They had earlier founded the tech firm, Glitterbug Technologies Pvt. Ltd., in 2011, which used to build technology for insurance companies.{{cite web|last1=Sharma|first1=Samhida|title=NRN's Catamaran invests in fintech startup Coverfox|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/business/india-business/NRNs-Catamaran-invests-in-fintech-startup-Coverfox/articleshow/48965051.cms|website=timesofindia.indiatimes.com|accessdate=2016-08-27}} Until August 2016, Glitterbug Technologies Pvt. Ltd., which runs Coverfox, has received investments of around $14 million in venture capital from [[Accel Partners]] and SAIF Partners.{{cite web|last1=Sharma|first1=Samidha|title=Accel, SAIF invest in Coverfox|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/business/india-business/Accel-SAIF-invest-in-Coverfox/articleshow/47034094.cms|website=timesofindia.indiatines.com|accessdate=2016-08-27}} The company raised $2 million in series A round in 2014 and another $12 million in series B round in April 2015. The series B round also consisted of investments of an “undisclosed amount” from [[N. R. Narayana Murthy]]’s Catamaran Ventures.{{cite web|last1=Sharma|first1=Samidha|title=NRN's Catamaran invests in fintech startup Coverfox|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/business/india-business/NRNs-Catamaran-invests-in-fintech-startup-Coverfox/articleshow/48965051.cms|website=timesofindia.indiatimes.com|accessdate=2016-08-27}} == Accolades and Mentions == The company’s co-founder and CEO, Varun Dua, was featured in the Business Insider Year Roundup (2015) for ‘Five entrepreneurs making a mark for themselves in 2015’.{{cite web|last1=Das|first1=Poulomi|title=BI Year Roundup: Five entrepreneurs who made a mark for themselves in 2015|url=http://www.businessinsider.in/BI-Year-Roundup-Five-entrepreneurs-who-made-a-mark-for-themselves-in-2015/articleshow/50379525.cms|website=businessinsider.in|accessdate=2016-08-28}} Covefox was featured in the The Smart CEO Startup50 list for excellence in consumer tech space and keeping its platform digital ready for the millennial generation.{{cite web|last1=CEO|first1=TheSmart|title=The Smart CEO Startup50 Winners|url=http://startup50.in/winners/|accessdate=1 December 2016}} The company won the FinTech Rising Star Award at the IndiaFinTech Award 2016.{{cite web|last1=Awards|first1=India FinTech|title=India FinTech Awards 2016|url=http://indiafintech.com/|accessdate=1 December 2016}} ==Recent Activities== Coverfox launched their maiden brand campaign in September 2015. It was conceptualized by [[Bartle Bogle Hegarty|BBH India]].{{cite web|last1=Bapna|first1=Amit|title=Coverfox.com’s maiden brand campaign is for the new-age Indian|url=http://brandequity.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/advertising/coverfox-coms-maiden-brand-campaign-is-for-the-new-age-indian/49035791|website=brandequity.economintimes.indiatimes.com|accessdate=2016-08-29}} In January 2016, the company, launched a second set of TVCs with BBH India to highlight the need of car insurance.{{cite web|last1=Team|first1=Campiagn India|title=Coverfox uses surprising disclosures, conveys need and ease of car insurance|url=http://www.campaignindia.in/video/coverfox-uses-surprising-disclosures-conveys-need-and-ease-of-car-insurance/424131|website=campaignindia.in|accessdate=2016-08-28}} In July 2016, the company launched a digital campaign to get people to renew their insurance.
