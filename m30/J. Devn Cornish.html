Monson]] | start_date1 = {{start date|2011|04|02}}}} '''John Devn Cornish''' (born April 12, 1951) has been a [[general authority]] of [[The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints]] (LDS Church) since 2011. He is a member of the [[Second Quorum of the Seventy]] Cornish was born in [[Salt Lake City, Utah]], to George O. Cornish and Naomi Black. He received a [[bachelor of science]] and medical degrees from [[Johns Hopkins University]]. He spent his professional life as a medical doctor, specializing in newborn intensive care. Cornish served as a major in the [[United States Air Force]]. He married Elaine Simmons in the [[Manti Utah Temple]] in 1973. They are the parents of six children. Cornish was a full-time [[missionary (LDS Church)|missionary]] for the LDS Church in the [[Guatemala]]–[[El Salvador]] [[Mission (LDS Church)|Mission]]. He has served as an [[elder (Latter Day Saints)|elders]] [[quorum (Latter Day Saints)|quorum]] president, [[Ward (LDS Church)|ward]] [[Young Men (organization)|Young Men]] president, [[bishop (Latter Day Saints)|bishop]], [[high priest (Latter Day Saints)|high priests]] group leader, [[stake president]], [[mission president|president]] of the [[Dominican Republic]] [[Santiago, Dominican Republic|Santiago]] Mission (2003–06), and [[area seventy]]. On April 2, 2011, Cornish was [[common consent (Latter Day Saints)|sustained]] by church members as a member of the [[Second Quorum of the Seventy]]. Since August 2011, Cornish has been a member of the presidency of the church's Caribbean [[Area (LDS Church)|Area]].[http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/60833/Area-leadership-2--2011.html "Area Leadership 2011"], ''[[Church News]]'' ([[Deseret News]]), 29 April 2011.[http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/62311/Area-leadership-assignments-announced-for-2012.html "Area leadership assignments announced for 2012"], ''[[Church News]]'' ([[Deseret News]]), 5 May 2012.[http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/63595/Area-leadership-assignments-announced.html "Area leadership assignments announced"], ''[[Church News]]'' ([[Deseret News]]), 25 May 2013.
