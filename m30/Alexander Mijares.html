Mijares started his career in 2011. He creates works inspired by his hometown as well as his Cuban and Spanish roots. ==Works== {{Empty section|date=December 2015}} ==Recognition== Mijares became recognized among the media and entertainment industries after beginning his career. In 2013, [[Elite Daily]] recognized Mijares as a Top 10 Influential Artist.{{cite web|title=The 10 Most Influential Artists Of 2013|url=http://www.ocartwalk.com/the-10-most-influential-artists-of-2013/|publisher=Oc art walk|accessdate=6 December 2015}} The following year he collaborated with rapper Pitbull during the 2014 World Cup.{{cite web|title=Alex Mijares & Pitbull Team Up On A Painting Titled "We Are One" To Be Auctioned Off For Charity|url=http://the305.com/2014/07/09/alex-mijares-pitbull-team-up-on-a-painting-titled-we-are-one-to-be-auctioned-off-for-charity/|website=the305.com|accessdate=6 December 2015}}{{cite web|title=Own the Official Artwork for Pitbull's World Cup Anthem|url=http://oceandrive.com/own-art-by-alexander-mijares-inspired-by-pitbull-world-cup-song|website=oceandrive.com|accessdate=6 December 2015}}Since then he has created custom pieces for artists including Justin Bieber, Tori Kelly, and Pharrell Williams. Mijares also painted the iHeart Radio Station Studio. In addition to headlining events like Art Basel Miami Beach, Art Wynwood,{{cite web|title=Alexander Mijares – 2015 Official Poster Artist|url=http://www.wynwoodlife.com/trek6-2014-official-poster-artist/|publisher=wynwoodlife|accessdate=17 December 2015}} and Art Santa Fe, Mijares’ art has been displayed coast-to-coast including in New York City’s Times Square, Nobu’s former South Hampton outpost, and the Presidential Office at Florida International University. Mijares is known for his dedication to philanthropy and his avid charity work, especially with children. He was recently appointed by the Nicklaus Children’s Hospital to create a mural dedicated to the hospital’s adolescent patients.{{cite web|title=Alexander Mijares Unveils Art Donated to Nicklaus
