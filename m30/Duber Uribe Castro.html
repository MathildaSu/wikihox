Music producer | years_active = 2006 - present | known_for = | notable_works = | height = 168 cm | weight = 65 kg | website = {{URL|www.duberuribe.co}} }} '''Duber Uribe Castro''' (born September 28, 1986) is a [[Colombia]]n journalist[http://elperiodicodeportivo.com.co/noticias/deportes/f%C3%BAtbol/lo-ultimo-de-millonarios-altas-y-posibles-bajas-del-equipo-217017 Lo último de Millonarios, altas y posibles bajas del equipo azul] (in Spanish), music producer and[http://www.lomasmusical.es/2016/11/duber-uribe-el-talento-detras-de-la.html Duber Uribe el talento detras de la banda Salamandra] (in Spanish)founder of [[Notimillos.com]]. == Biography == Son of Juan Uribe Diaz and Mónica Castro Arias, Duber Arley Uribe Castro is a journalist[http://elevatordm.com/duber-uribe-la-cara-detras-de-notimillos/ Duber Uribe, la cara detrás de NotiMillos] (in Spanish) and musical producer[http://www.abc.com.py/espectaculos/musica/artistas-locales-nominados-a-premiacion-internacional-1293111.html Artistas locales nominados en premiación internacional] (in Spanish) born in Florencia (Caquetá) - Colombia, in 1986, he studied primary education in the city of Bogotá in a school called Liceo San Bernardo de 1992 to 1996, later his family moved to Florencia where he graduated as a Classical Bachelor in the San José Minor Seminary in 2004. The following year he returns to the Colombian capital to begin his university studies, from 2005 to 2006 he performs a Technician in Locution and Production of Audiovisual Media in the Higher School of Telecommunications, later in 2007 he began his studies of Social Communication and Journalism in [[Pontifical Xavierian University]], where he excelled by his abilities to write journalistic[https://www.vavel.com/lat/colombia/futbol-colombiano/millonarios/457363-cali-millonarios.html Millonarios pierde el clásico añejo por goleada] (in Spanish) texts and of reporting. During his last year of university he practiced as a reporter on the Caracol Gol [[Caracol TV]], there he followed the capital teams (Millonarios and Independiente Santa Fe). When graduating as a journalist,[https://notimillos.com/author/duribe/ Duber Uribe autor de NotiMillos] (in Spanish) he started work at [[BuzzFeed]] and was part of the team of Spanish-language writers for the website.
