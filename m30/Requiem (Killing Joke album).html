(2009) | Next album = }} '''''Requiem''''' is a dual-format DVD/CD live album release by [[Killing Joke]], documenting their performance of 8 August 2003 at the [[Lokerse Feesten]] in [[Lokeren]], [[Belgium]]. The album was released 22 September 2009.http://www.amazon.com/dp/B002EIJ9BW The liner notes in the release's booklet are adapted without credit from the main [[Wikipedia]] entry for Killing Joke. ==Track listing== ''All tracks written by Coleman/Walker/Glover/Ferguson, except where stated.'' #"Requiem" #"Total Invasion" (Coleman/Walker/Glover/Gill) #"Wardance" #"Blood On Your Hands" (Coleman/Walker/Glover/Gill) #"Change" #"Seeing Red" (Coleman/Walker/Glover/Gill) #"The Fall Of Because" #"The Death & Resurrection Show" (Coleman/Walker/Glover/Gill) #"The Wait" #"Whiteout" (Killing Joke) #"Frenzy" (Coleman/Walker/Raven/Ferguson) #"Pssyche" #"Pandemonium" (Killing Joke) The DVD and CD have the same track listing.
