xml:space="preserve" bytes="1486"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Lego Clutch Powers: Bad Hair Day|timestamp=20151022100510|year=2015|month=October|day=22|substed=yes|help=off}} {{Unreferenced|date=August 2014}} {{italic title}} '''''LEGO Clutch Powers: Bad Hair Day''''' is a computer animated short that was shown with ''[[Lego: The Adventures of Clutch Powers]]'' in worldwide theaters and LEGOland theaters. The short centers on Artie Fol who made an invention that is named the Portalteller. It was released in February 2010. ==Plot== The short starts with Artie Fol making the Portalteller and Peg Mooring sneaks up on him in accident. She asks him if he has seen her lucky hair which is blonde and he says no.
