xml:space="preserve" bytes="10936"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Australian Under-16 Individual Speedway Championship|timestamp=20160124061747|year=2016|month=January|day=24|substed=yes}} {{Notability|date=January 2016}} The '''Australian Under-16 Speedway Championship''' is a [[motorcycle speedway]] junior championship held each year in Australia since 1982 to determine the Australian Under-16 national champion. The event is organised by [[Motorcycling Australia]] (MA) and bikes are limited to 125cc engines, although there are also MA sanctioned 250cc and 350cc Australian U/16 Speedway Championships. The U/16's racing on the larger capacity bikes generally use full size speedways where the 125cc class race on the smaller (usually 110-150 metre) junior tracks. The first ever Australian Under-16 Speedway Championship, named for 1982, was actually held on 30 August 1981 at the [[Sidewinders Speedway]] in [[Adelaide]], [[South Australia]]. The championship was won by Adelaide youngster [[Paul Snadden]] from [[Western Australia]]ns [[Michael Carter (speedway rider)|Michael Carter]] and [[David Cheshire]]. [[Jason Hawkes]] ([[Victoria (Australia)|Victoria]]), [[Rusty Harrison]] (SA), [[Mark Jones (speedway rider)|Mark Jones]] (Vic) have two championship wins each. [[Arthur Sissis]] (SA) currently holds the record for most championship wins with three wins Riders who have won the championship and gone on to have successful senior careers include: triple [[Individual Speedway World Championship|Speedway World Champion]] and once [[Individual Speedway Junior World Championship|Under-21 World Champion]] [[Jason Crump]], 10 times [[Australian Individual Speedway Championship|Australian Solo Champion]] [[Leigh Adams]] (who also won the [[1992 Individual Speedway Junior World Championship|1992 World Under-21 Champion]]), national champions [[Ryan Sullivan]] and [[Troy Batchelor]], and multiple state champions [[Shane Parker (speedway rider)|Shane Parker]] (SA), [[Craig Hodgson]] (SA), [[Travis McGowan]] (Vic), [[Rusty Harrison]] (SA), and [[Rory Schlein]] (SA). The current (2015) Australian Under-16 Champion is second generation South Australian rider [[Jack Norman]] (the son of the 1990 and 1993 [[South Australian Individual Speedway Championship|South Australian Champion]] [[Scott Norman]]) who won the title at the [[Ipswich Junior Speedway]] on 24 January.
