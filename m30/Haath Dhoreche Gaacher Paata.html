Composed by [[Indraadip Das Gupta]], the song is sung by [[Shreya Ghoshal]], with lyrics penned by [[Srijato]]. == Release and reception== The song was released on July 8 2013 at the official YouTube channel of [[Shree Venkatesh Films]].{{cite news|title="Haath Dhoreche Gaacher Paata" video|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/videos/entertainment/regional/bangla/Proloy-Haath-Dhoreche-Gaacher-Paata/videoshow/21210999.cms|accessdate=1 May 2014|newspaper=[[The Times of India]]}} The music video of the song featured actors [[Mimi Chakraborty]] and [[Parambrata Chatterjee]]. The sequences were shot in a village of [[West Bengal]].{{cite web|title=Haath Dhoreche Gaacher Paata – Song from the Bengali Movie Proloy by Shreya Ghoshal|url=http://www.venkateshfilms.com/bengali-movie-songs-videos/videos/haath-dhoreche-gaacher-paata-song-bengali-movie-proloy-shreya-ghosal/|publisher=[[Shree Venkatesh Films]]|accessdate=1 May 2014}} The song was well received by the public as well as by critics and reviewers. Basically a love song, "Haath Dhoreche Gaacher Paata" involved the use of chiefly [[India]]n instruments. Both [[Shreya Ghoshal]]'s voice as well as [[Srijato]]'s lyrics were well appreciated.{{cite web|title=Proloy music review|url=http://www.gomolo.com/proloy-movie-music-review/45418/269426|publisher=[[Gomolo]]|accessdate=1 May 2014}} A video of the making of the song was also released later by [[Sangeet Bangla]]. Director [[Raj Chakraborty]] stated that the entire song was shot in a situation when the atmospheric temperature was about 35° Celsius, resulting in quite a hardship for the entire cast and crew.{{cite web|title=Haath Dhoreche Gaacher Paata- Making|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2ZnCt8zPjXc|publisher=[[Sangeet Bangla]]|accessdate=1 May 2014}} == Accolades == {| class="wikitable sortable" |- !
