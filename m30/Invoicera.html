It consists of several additional features like recurring invoices, late fee payment system, project [[time tracking]] and expense tracking solutions. The product helps enterprises to get customised billing solutions for their business. ==Overview== Invoicera is a cloud based invoicing system for freelancers, small businesses and enterprises to automate the invoicing process. Businesses can improve their invoicing, time tracking and task management processes and can implement custom invoices. It is also used as a simple accounting software. The invoices generated by Invoicera system can be customized, in order to reflect the business identity of the sender. This can be accomplished by using one of the several available templates (each with a range of color schemes) or by designing a personal template. The service may be configured for invoice scheduling (invoices delivered automatically at a set date and time), for late fee payment (charged automatically whenever there is a deferred payment) and for recurring billing. An invoice can be sent to multiple contacts at a time. A Magento extension permits to generate and send invoices in real time, as soon as an order is placed in a store Invoicera maintains an SSAE 16 certification, which is the online security standard for financial institutions. Work from both your computer and your smartphone is protected by TSL.
