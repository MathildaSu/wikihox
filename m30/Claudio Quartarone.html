He began playing classical music at age eight and he toured Italy with [[Enrico Rava]]. In 2008, he recorded three albums with major label [[Rai (record label)|Rai]].[http://www.jazzitalia.net/articoli/Int_ClaudioQuartarone.asp#.VEi2XSKsVDQ Jazz Italia] He began studying classical guitar at the age of 8. He also developed an interest for electronic music. At nineteen, he devoted himself to jazz that became his main channel of expression. "[[jazz]], the only way to learn about the music" interview for Jazzitalia of 2010 . He immediately draws attention from the jazz Sicily in 2006 before attending the Berklee School of Music in Perugia. In 2007 he won through the Siena Jazz Foundation, a scholarship to represent Italy at the international conference "IASJ". During the experience Siena is noticed by trumpeter [[Enrico Rava]], who decided to involve him in his band "Special Edition", he also said "A truly amazing guitarist, 20, 21, I think I have never heard such a one in Italy, it is called Claudio Quartarone, comes from Catania, as [[Cafiso]], that area is giving amazing musicians". During his career he played with some of the most important musicians of the national jazz scene including [[Enrico Rava]], Fabrizio Sferra, Giovanni Guidi, Mauro Ottolini, Stefano Senni, Dino Rubino, Dan Kinzelman, Joao Lobo, to name a few. The music of Claudio Quartarone is characterized by eclecticism, this feature makes it difficult to frame it in a genre exclusively.
