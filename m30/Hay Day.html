November 20, 2013 | genre = [[Strategy video game|Strategy]] | modes = | media = }} Hay Day is a farming game made by [[Supercell (video game company)|Supercell]]. This game is available for iPhone and android users. Hay Day released for android on 20th Nov 2013[http://forum.supercell.net/showthread.php/171112-Hay-Day-Available-for-Android!! Hay Day for Android] According to a report the company earns $30M every month from Hay Day and Clash of Clans(This game also made by Supercell)[http://www.ibtimes.co.uk/supercell-earns-30m-month-clash-clans-hay-day-1436122 Supercell earns $30M every month] ==Plot== In the game uncle of the game player owns a farm. He is not able to take care of his farm anymore and hands over the responsibility of farm to the player. The game starts with a [[scarecrows]] teaching the player about harvesting [[wheat]]. The players earns coins and Experience points(XP), coins used to buy production building and decoration items and XPs used to level up the game, and XP score reset to zero every time level is up. Eventually the scarecrows are reintroduced to the game. They revolt due to the farmers tyrannical rule. The farm becomes a centerplace for anarchy, ending with the player being burned at the stake. Player can gain coins and Xps by selling goods through roadside shop, truck deliveries and shipping orders.
