xml:space="preserve" bytes="9242"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Mark Tuan|timestamp=20141229064912|year=2014|month=December|day=29|substed=yes|help=off}} {{Infobox musical artist | name = Mark Tuan | image = | alt = "Mark Tuan" | caption = | image_size = | background = solo_singer | birth_name = Mark Yi-En Tuan | native_name = 段宜恩 (Traditional Chinese) | alias = Mark | birth_date = {{birth date and age|1993|09|04}} | birth_place = [[Southern California]] | genre = [[K-Pop]], [[Hip-Hop]] | occupation = [[Singer]], [[Rapper]], [[Dancer]] | associated_acts = [[GOT7]] | years_active = 2014-present | label = [[JYP Entertainment]] }} '''Mark Tuan''' Yi-En (Traditional Chinese: 段宜恩), simply known as Mark (Korean: 마크) is a Taiwanese American rapper of [[South Korea]]n boy group '''[[Got7]]''' (Korean: 갓세븐) formed in 2014 under [[JYP Entertainment]]. ==Early life== Mark was born in Los Angeles, California on September 4, 1993, but grew up in Brazil and Paraguay before moving back to Los Angeles. He comes from a Christian family and has two sisters (Tammy and Grace) and one brother (Joey). Mark completed his 10th grade in Arcadia High School and was scouted by a JYP Entertainment representative where he then went to South Korea and became a trainee in August 2010. He was trained for 3.5 years before his official debut as a member of [[Got7]]. ==Career== ===Pre-Debut=== Mark appeared in a KT Olleh CF in 2011 and had also made a brief appearance in [[Dream High 2]]{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CqpbaKuk2tU|title=[FYBK] JYP Trainees Brian Kang and Mark Tuan cuts in DH2 with JJ Project |publisher=youtube|accessdate=November 5, 2013 }} in 2012, which two fellow [[Got7]] members, [[JB (South Korean musician)|JB]] and [[Jr. (singer)|Jr.]], played as main roles. In September 2013, Mark, together with [[Jackson Wang|Jackson]], BamBam and Yugyeom appeared in Episode 4 (YG vs JYP){{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jx-ACrBWejo|title=[ WIN : WHO IS NEXT ] episode 4_ YG vs JYP ! 배틀의 결과는?! |publisher=youtube|accessdate=September 14, 2013 }} of "WIN: Who Is Next" and competed against the YG Entertainment trainees. ===Debut=== JYP Entertainment released Mark's image teasers on January 3, 2014. On Janaury 10, 2014,{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jXBqRdcpH-c|title=GOT7_Teaser Video #1 |publisher=youtube|accessdate=January 9, 2014 }} [[Got7]]'s first debut teaser video was released on YouTube, followed by a second debut teaser video on January 13, 2014.{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4fDJ54O2tcg|title=GOT7_Teaser Video #2 |publisher=youtube|accessdate=January 12, 2014 }} With '''"Girls, Girls, Girls"''',{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2sAoKmg7qPI|title=GOT7_Girls Girls Girls_M/V |publisher=youtube|accessdate=January 14, 2014 }} Mark made his official debut as a member of [[Got7]] on 15 January 2014. ===Notes=== Mark's main role in [[Got7]] is a rapper and he also does Martial Arts Tricking. Albeit being the "Hyung" in his group, Mark is described as having a "soft spoken" personality and was chosen as the "Visual" by his members.
