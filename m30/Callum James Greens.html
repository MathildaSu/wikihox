Greens is the founder and director of the former Warner Music UK record label DimeRock Records, now owned by Bangkok Media Group and renamed BKKMG Records. ==Music and Radio== In August 2007, Greens founded the DimeRock Records record label and the Calzo Houdini studio team.D'Ambrosio, Addolorata http://like-the-sound.blogspot.com/2011/03/dime-radio-is-now-live-on-dimerock.html – Like The Sound In 2009 Greens became a member of [[The Music Producers Guild]] in the UK. In June 2012, Greens moved the DimeRock Records record label over to The Bangkok Media Group and subsequently became a director of the company. Greens along with Bangkok Media Group founded BKKMG Radio Network which partners with stations and online networks throughout South East Asia, UK, Europe, Australia and SiriusXM in the US.BKKMG http://bkkmg.com/bkkmg-acquires-dimerock-records-and-affiliated-companies/ – Press Release ==Personal life== Callum James Greens lives in [[Bangkok]], Thailand and [[Edinburgh]], Scotland. Greens is an Atheist.
