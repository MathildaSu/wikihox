Based in a derelict garage in the hart of [[New Cross Gate]], founders [https://www.linkedin.com/in/cristiana-bottigella-b686a41a Cristiana Bottigella], [http://www.sigrunsverrisdottir.com Sigrun Sverrisdottir] and [http://www.tisna.com Tisna Westerhof], aim to break down barriers between art college and gallery and make the art world more transparent and accessible both for artists and the community. Inviting critical and cultural engagement by honest dialogue around work, they create opportunities for artists to exhibit, explore, curate and commission without compromising the integrity of the artist.
