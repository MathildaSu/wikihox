xml:space="preserve" bytes="2675"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Los Negrales|timestamp=20160813180800|year=2016|month=August|day=13|substed=yes|help=off}} {{refimprove|date=August 2015}} [[File:Los-negrales-tren.JPG|thumb|250x250px|The train station in Los Negrales]] '''Los Negrales''' is a pertaining residential zone to the municipal terms of [[Collado Villalba]], [[Alpedrete]], [[San Lorenzo de El Escorial|Saint Lorenzo de El Escorial]], [[Guadarrama]] and [[Galapagar]] (municipalities of the northwest of the [[Community of Madrid]], Spain). Although it was divided in three administrative zones, Los Negrales configures a unitary residential area, constituted in the majority by individual chalets. The most ancient houses date to the first-half of the 20th century, and the most modern were built in contemporary times. A significant part of the houses is that they are sometimes the second residence of his owners, therefore, in summer and in holidays the population duplicates. Los Negrales is situated near of the "Sierra" of Guadarrama and near of the centre of [[Collado Villalba]], what does that it was a good place to spend the holidays. This residential zone was formed little by little based upon principles of the 20th century. Los Negrales has of a considerable public facilities (parish Church, Public School of childish and primary, sports center, municipal Park, pharmacy). This enclave divided in 2 parts: Los Negrales (neighbourhood) and the Colony Fuentellana (where the station of Los Negrales is located), and both zones are divided by the avenue of Reina Victoria (ancient N-SAW or road of The Coruña). Los Negrales has public bus service and has a train station of the line C-8b of Cercanías[[Cercanías Madrid| Madrid]] called Los Negrales,{{Cite web|url = http://www.ferropedia.es/wiki/Estaci%C3%B3n_de_Los_Negrales|title = Estación de Los Negrales|date = |accessdate = |website = |publisher = |last = |first = }} situated in the municipal term of [[Alpedrete]]. The neighbourhood is situated near of the urban centre of Collado Villalba, what facilitates a lot the shopping and leisure.
