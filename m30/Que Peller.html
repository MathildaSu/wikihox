Que is a son to the late legendary [[Professor Peller]] who has been acclaimed as the greatest magician to have ever emerged from West Africa. Que graduated from Lead City University, Ibadan in 2012 and started singing professionally soon afterwards, before getting signed to Aquila Records [https://www.channelstv.com/2014/09/12/shina-peller-floats-new-label-signs-tilla/] in 2014 as one of the label’s pioneer artistes alongside Tillaman. == History == Abisoye Abiola- Peller, also known as, Que Peller, was born on November 17, 1989, to late Professor Peller and Alhaja Peller. His father was the late Professor Peller a legendary African magician who is said to be the greatest magician to have ever emerged from West Africa. Que Peller grew up in Ibadan, Nigeria and spent most of his childhood indulging in various forms of art which mostly revolved around his father and his routine as a foremost magician. Que Peller learned the art of magic from his father, but his main interest was music. As a young boy, Que performed his songs, in the background during his father’s rehearsals and performances. Growing up Que was influenced by [[King Sunny Ade]], and [[Fela Anikulapo Kuti]] amongst other notable Nigerian artists. Although his family had a legacy in magic entertainment, Que’s interest were in the art of blending instruments and vocals, which would eventually lead him to peruse music. Prior to his emergence in the Nigerian music scene, Que attended Dalfred Nursery & Primary School Ibadan, O&A Academy Ibadan and subsequently Government College Ibadan, where he made his Senior School Leaving Certificate. He was eventually admitted into Lead City University Ibadan, where he graduated with a B.S. in Mass Communications and Media Technology in 2012. === Musical Beginnings === After graduating from college Que moved to Lagos to pursue his love for music. He spent time moving around studios, working on his craft and creating a unique sound and style. He met Base One around this time and they soon became good friends. After a year of creating his sound, Que he presented what he had to his brother, Shina Peller, who was in the stages of launching a record label and signing the artist Tillaman. Shina was impressed with Que’s music and decided to sign his brother to his label. Que signed his first record deal in 2014, to his brother’s record label, Aquila Records. Due to his father’s legacy as a musician, he decided to fuse music and magic as a way to honor his late father who paved the way for him, in entertainment. === Single Releases === Que signed to Aquila Records in early 2015 and under the label’s artiste development program, spent several months working on production, and recording vocals for his debut project. He released his first single “ Juru” [http://notjustok.com/2015/06/11/que-peller-juru-ft-base-one/] in June 2015. The song featured label mate and friend, Base One, and was produced by [[Young John (producer)|Young John]]. He subsequently followed up his first release with his second critically acclaimed single, Eleniyan [http://notjustok.com/2015/11/02/que-peller-eleniyan/], produced by Reelz, in November 2015. Both songs received heavy airplay, and videos for both singles were simultaneously released and directed by Frizzle n Bizzle Films. After the success of his two first singles, Que followed up his success with the single “Carolina” [http://tooxclusive.com/download-mp3/que-peller-carolina-ft-olamide/] in August 2016.
