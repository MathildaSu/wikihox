[[filmmaker]] }} '''David Wilcock''' is a [[New Age]] [[lecturer]], [[author]], and [[filmmaker]] based in [[Los Angeles]], [[California]].[http://www.consciouslifeexpo.com/program2010/workshops/sunworkshops/Wilcock.html 8th Annual Conscious Life Expo] ==Career== Wilcock co-authored (along with Wynn Free) the book ''The Reincarnation of Edgar Cayce?''[http://books.google.ca/books/about/The_Reincarnation_of_Edgar_Cayce.html?id=kWhHEjB1oDkC&redir_esc=y ''The Reincarnation of Edgar Cayce?'' at Google books] in 2004.
