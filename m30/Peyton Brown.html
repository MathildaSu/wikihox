[[Miss USA 2016]] | birth_name = | birth_date = {{Birth based on age as of date|21|2015|11|08}} | birth_place = | eye_color = | hair_color = | height = | education = [[University of Alabama]] | homepage = }} '''Peyton Brown''' is an American [[beauty pageant]] [[title of honor|titleholder]] from [[Eufaula, Alabama]], who was crowned [[Miss Alabama Teen USA]] 2012 and [[Miss Alabama USA]] 2016. She will compete for the [[Miss USA 2016]] title. ==Pageant career== ===Miss Alabama Teen USA 2012=== On January 28, 2012, Brown won the [[Miss Alabama Teen USA]] 2012 title. She had entered the pageant as Miss Barbour County Teen USA, representing [[Barbour County, Alabama|Barbour County]] for the state title. She competed in the [[Miss Teen USA]] 2012 pageant and placed in the Top 16. ===Miss Alabama USA 2016=== She entered the [[Miss Alabama USA]] pageant in November 2015 as one of 43 qualifiers for the state title. Brown won the competition on Saturday, November 7, 2015, when she received her crown from outgoing Miss Alabama USA titleholder [[Madison Guthrie]]. She earned thousands of dollars in scholarship money and other prizes from the state pageant. As Miss Alabama USA, her activities include public appearances across the state of Alabama. Her platform is to raise awareness of [[skin cancer]] and the dangers of sun overexposure and [[tanning bed]]s. Brown will be Alabama's representative at the [[Miss USA 2016]] pageant in [[Las Vegas, Nevada]], in summer 2016. ==Early life and education== Brown is a native of [[Eufaula, Alabama]], and a 2012 graduate of The Lakeside School in Eufaula. Her parents are Johna and Russell Cotton and the late Steve Brown. Brown has an older sister, Alexis, and a younger brother, Copeland.
