xml:space="preserve" bytes="2046"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Lenin Gani|timestamp=20140305062812|year=2014|month=March|day=5|substed=yes|help=off}} {{Infobox person |name=Lenin Gani |organization=Sports Editor, BDNews24 |other_names=Asif |birth_place= United Kingdom}} '''Lenin Gani''' ({{lang-bn|'''লেনিন গনি'''}}) (December, 1967) was a highly respected [[Bangladesh]]i sports [[journalist]]. He was the sports editor of ''[[BDNews24.com]]'' and a senior member of the Bangladesh Sports Journalists Association. == Career == Lenin worked for a number of major Newspapers of [[Bangladesh]] and contributed immensely to the country's journalism. In 2001 Lenin won the prestigious Best Sports Report award from Dhaka Reporters Unity. == Death == In 2008 Lenin was diagnosed with a rare terminal illness [[Pulmonary Hypertension]]. He moved to the UK to receive treatment, and continued to contribute to [[Bangladesh]]i journalism in his debilitating state. On May 23, 2013 Lenin finally succumb to his condition.[http://www.thedailystar.net/beta2/news/youll-never-walk-alone/ You'll never walk alone] (2013). Print.
