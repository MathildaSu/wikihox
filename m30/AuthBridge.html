[[Risk Assessment]] | num_employees = 500 | homepage = http://www.authbridge.com }} '''AuthBridge''' is an India-based pre-employment [[background screening]] and [[risk assessment]] company in the background verification domain with its head office in [[Gurgaon]], [[Haryana]]. {{cite news|author=Nikhilesh Sinha|title=Starting Up showcases Authbridge - a research and background check company''|url= https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HB9-eFFQPD4}} http://www.worldhrdiary.com/2016/05/interview-ajay-trehan-founder-ceo.htmlhttps://www.peoplematters.in/article/background-verification/background-screening-necessity-recruiting-right-talent-13087 An empanelled background check company with National Skill Registry,{{cite news|title=List of entities empanelled to provide background checking services on National Skills Registry (NSR)'' |url=https://nationalskillsregistry.com/background-verification.htm}} an initiative of [[NASSCOM]]. AuthBridge is also a member of APAC chapter of NAPBS (National Association of Profession Background Screeners), which was started in the year 2005 by Mr. [[Ajay Trehan]],{{cite news|author=Ajay Trehan|title=Know Your Delivery Guy: The Importance Of Background Checks In An On-Demand Economy''|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.in/ajay-trehan/know-your-delivery-guy-th_b_9146892.html?utm_hp_ref=india}} Founder and CEO. ==Services== AuthBridge provides information services about candidates, individuals, organization. It provides screening services to organizations for existing employees, prospective hires, senior management and vendors and partners under the AuthBridge brand. AuthBridge customers include MNCs, Startups from diverse industry sectors. AuthBridge has created separate web based and automated platforms to offer instantaneous employee verification and education verification that can be used by other background screening companies and organizations. Spreading its wings to target the global markets,{{cite news|author=M Saraswathy|title=Firms go deeper in background verification for hiring at senior levels''|url=http://www.business-standard.com/article/companies/companies-go-deeper-in-background-verification-for-hiring-at-senior-levels-116021500320_1.html}} ==Awards and Recognitions== * In the year 2008, AuthBridge was one of the few organizations to have [[ISO 9001:2008]] and ISO/IEC 27001:20013 certifications and also recognized for its world class, technology driven processes and systems. * In 2012, [[Cornell University]] published a case study{{cite news|author=Vishal Gaur|title=Innovative HR Operations in India''|url=http://forum.johnson.cornell.edu/faculty/gaur/authbridge.pdf}} on AuthBridge Process Transitions, and incorporated the same into its operations management classes.
