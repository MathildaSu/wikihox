Canvas provides [[mobile app]]s to expand data collection and make sharing easier for organizations of all sizes. They create apps to gather information, and the apps can be used from [[expense report]]s to installing soda fountains.{{cite news|last=King|first=Rachel|title=How Pepsi Bottling Ventures IT Says Yes, Even When Strapped for Resource|url=http://blogs.wsj.com/cio/2012/09/05/how-pepsi-bottling-ventures-it-says-yes-even-when-strapped-for-resources/|work=Wall Street Journal|accessdate=16 September 2013|date=5 September 2012}} All submissions are stored in the cloud and accessible for the user. Canvas allows the user to customize their apps and can include [[GPS]] location capture, photos, signature capture, and [[reference data]]. Data can be exported in a [[comma-separated values]] spreadsheet or a [[pdf]]{{cite web|last=Gahran|first=Amy|title=Tracking Inventory Have You Stressed? Try These 5 Mobile Apps |url=http://www.entrepreneur.com/blog/224857#|work=Entrepreneur|accessdate=16 September 2013}} Canvas provides users a free month to try the product. After that, it relies on a subscription model for access to the app builder and to upload new data via the mobile app. == Origins == Founded in 2008 by Chris Morissette, James Quigley, and Matt Warner as a company specializing in [[Software as a Service]] (SaaS).{{cite news|last=Overly|first=Steven|title=The Download: Canvas goes international with Sydney office|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/business/capitalbusiness/the-download-canvas-goes-international-with-sydney-office/2012/11/30/06c69ac2-38aa-11e2-a263-f0ebffed2f15_story.html|work=Washington Post|accessdate=12 September 2013|date=7 December 2012}} == Operations == Canvas is headquartered in [[Reston, VA]]. A regional office covering Asia Pacific in [[Sydney, Australia]] was opened in 2013. It has apps in 4 languages including [[English language|English]], [[French language|French]], [[Spanish language|Spanish]], and [[Dutch language|Dutch]]. === Organizational structure === Canvas is run as a [[flat organization]] free of hierarchical structure. Rather, performance is judged based on metrics.{{cite web|last=Quigley|first=James|title=Getting to Know Canvas - Our Culture, Structure, How We Get Things Done (aka New Candidate Tips)|url=http://www.gocanvas.com/content/blog/post/want-to-work-at-canvas-you-need-to-know-these-5-things|accessdate=16 September 2013}} == Products & Services == Canvas services can be broken down into: The App Builder, The Application Store, Account Portal, Dispatch and MyCanvas === The App Builder === Allows users to customize their apps to include features such as GPS capture, signature, photo capture or use reference data. === The Application Store === A library full apps made by Canvas subscribers and partners from all over the world. Includes industries from [[construction]] to [[pet sitting]]. === Account Portal === Allows subscribers to access their submissions, edit previously submitted data, or export data to back end data systems. === Dispatch === This service allows administrators to pre-populate apps and send them to other users.{{cite web|title=Introducing Dispatch for Canvas|url=http://www.gocanvas.com/content/how-to/post/how-to-use-dispatch}} === MyCanvas === A new program from Canvas. Anyone who has work done by a subscriber, can now have that data stored in the cloud for them at no cost.{{cite web|last=Seo|first=Michael|title=MyCanvas Allows Homeowners To Access Their Personal Service Data History In The Cloud|url=http://techcrunch.com/2013/04/16/mycanvas-allows-homeowners-to-access-their-personal-service-data-history-in-the-cloud/?utm_source=dlvr.it&utm_medium=twitter|work=Tech Crunch|accessdate=12 September 2013}}{{cite web|title=Introducing MyCanvas!|url=http://www.gocanvas.com/content/tour_mycanvas/|accessdate=16 September 2013}} == Supported platforms == The Canvas smart client is available for Microsoft Windows, [[Android (operating system)|Android]], iOS (iPhone, iPad, iPod Touch), [[Windows Mobile]], [[Windows Phone]],and [[BlackBerry]]. Computers are used for accessing submissions, and the app builder. Filling out the apps occurs primarily on smart devices such as phones and tablets. There are some minor differences between operating systems. Still, all major functions are shared between platforms. == Ante Up == Rather than volunteer or donate money towards social causes, Canvas donates their product and employee time to nonprofit organizations.{{cite news|last=Small|first=Vanessa|title=Canvas selects nonprofits to get its app for free|url=http://articles.washingtonpost.com/2012-08-26/business/35491309_1_nonprofits-safety-checks-application-process|work=Washington Post|accessdate=13 September 2013|date=28 August 2012}} Every year, they award one nonprofit with whom they work the Ante Up Award.
