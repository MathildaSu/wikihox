xml:space="preserve" bytes="1400"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Heart of a Poet|timestamp=20170808235720|year=2017|month=August|day=8|substed=yes|help=off}} {{italic title}} '''''Heart of a Poet''''' is a Canadian [[television documentary]] series that premiered in April 2006, created by [[Maureen Judge]] and Tina Hahn and executive produced by Maureen Judge. The production is broadcast on [[Bravo!]]. Each episode of the series profiled the life, literature, and performances of a different working Canadian poet. The poets are introduced through samples of their writing, interviews, recitals, and the observational footage of the experiences that make up their daily lives and influence their poetry.
