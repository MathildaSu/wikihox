xml:space="preserve" bytes="2085"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Jericho Summer|timestamp=20160402151035|year=2016|month=April|day=2|substed=yes|help=off}} '''Jericho Summer''' are American Southern Country Rock Artists, a supergroup collation of musicians which were formed by Jay Zeffin and Vanessa Joy in 2015 to produce the Night Train Album.. Studio Musicians [[Richard Fortus]] (Thin Lizzy, Dead Dasies & Guns N' Roses), [[Albert Lee]] (Hogans Heroes, Emmy Lou Harris, Everly Brothers, Eric Clapton) and [[Marco Mendoza]] (Whitesnake, Thin Lizzy and Dead Daises) have been involved at various times of the recording of the debut album Night Train. The album also features other recognised musicians such as [[Alan Hawkshaw]] (Dusty Springfield, Tom Jones, The Shadows), Rodders Godders from Jigsaw and Bad Finger, and Brett Morgan (Freddie Mercury, Rod Stewart, George Michael, Robbie Williams). In 2016 [[John Marcangelo]] joined the line up and provided drums/piano alongside Guy Lancaster. Legendary Grammy Nominated Sound engineer Pete Mahyer also joined as mastering engineer in 2016. The album Night Train is due for release in 2016 was recorded between The Music Farm Studios, CTS Studios London, First Run Studios Nashville and Los Angeles AIR Consoles.
