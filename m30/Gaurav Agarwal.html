xml:space="preserve" bytes="2204"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Gaurav Agarwal|timestamp=20150620221437|year=2015|month=June|day=20|substed=yes|help=off}} {{multiple issues| {{autobiography|date=June 2015}} {{notability|Biographies|date=June 2015}} {{third-party|date=June 2015}} }} Gaurav Agarwal is a senior technocrat in Government of India. He is an IRSME(Indian Railways Service of Mechanical Engineer) officer of 1993 batch. He has done his Masters in Public Administration from Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy, National University of Singapore. He is also a Chevening Gurukul Fellow Scholar from King's College, London. He has worked in Eastern Railway, East Central Railway, Rail Coach Factory, Kapurthala and Railway Board within Indian Railways. These days he is working on deputation in Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports of Government of India. He has been awarded Railway Minister Award in the year 2006. He has written various books on various contemporary issues such as: High Speed Rail Technology, Maintenance of Diesel Locomotives He has also presented papers in various international seminars on issues like "Anti Climbing Device", "Personal Rapid Transit System", " Measuring Innovation: How Feasible it is ?"
