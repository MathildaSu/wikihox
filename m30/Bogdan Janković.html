xml:space="preserve" bytes="2590"> {{Article for deletion/dated|page=Bogdan Janković|timestamp=20170520060550|year=2017|month=May|day=20|substed=yes|help=off}} [[File:HK Vitez Bogdan Jankovic -4.jpg|thumb|HK Vitez Bogdan Jankovic −4]] {{Distinguish|Bojan Janković}} '''Bogdan Janković''' ({{lang-sr|Богдан Јанковић}}, born 1 September 1982) is a [[Serbs|Serbian]] hockey player who played for the [[Serbia national ice hockey team]]. Earlier in his career he played for the [[Yugoslavia national ice hockey team]].With Yugoslavia and Serbia ice hockey teams, as player he won bronze medal at IIHF European junior championship in 1999, Bronze medal at IIHF World Championship D2 in 2008, and Gold medal at World Championship D2 in 2009. He was member of HK Partizan Belgrade ice hockey club for more than a decade. Playing for HK Partizan he won multiple Serbian championship titles, and he competed in four Continental Cup tournaments of Europe. In 2010 he moved to HK Spartak Subotica and won Serbian cup title same season. Afterwards in 2012 he returned to Belgrade and joined HK Vitez Belgrade, where he soon became player-coach,and was elected as General Manager for Serbian national U18 and U20 teams for 2012–13 season. He currently continues his engagement as player-coach in HK Vitez Belgrade. ==Profile== Gender: Male
