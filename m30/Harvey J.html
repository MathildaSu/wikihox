The year 2015 spawned his commercial debut "Make It Bounce" with Trollphace, released under OWSLA.TSIS Premiere: http://thissongissick.com/blog/2014/tsis-premeire-trollphace-make-it-bounce-owsla-eggnog-compilation/#sthash.EACR4fnU.dpbs Thanks to cosigns from the likes of high profile DJ’s such as Skrillex and Chuckie, Harvey’s debut went on to chart #7 on iTunes Dance Charts and it remained in the Beatport Top 100 Dubstep Charts for 96 days.Beatport Charts: https://pro.beatport.com/track/make-it-bounce-feat-harvey-j-original-mix/6121319 Harvey has amassed a massive following from his self-promoted "Pancakes & Henny Tour" as well as releasing a string of songs and viral skits on his Facebook page.MXXNSHINE Tour Sheet: http://www.omgitsharveyj.com/#!live/c22tzHarvey’s Facebook Page: http://facebook.com/omgitsharveyj == Musical career == Harvey started his musical ambitions online, as he pursued recognition on his Facebook Page. His first success came upon the creation and release of the song "Durham Boiz", paired with the movement of 4 other rappers from his hometown they quickly gained regional attention getting over 500 spins of commercial airplay and acquiring hundreds of thousands of views on YouTube.Durham Boiz on Youtube: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K48mZVtryhQ On December 19, 2013 Durham Police filed a complaint to YouTube claiming that visibly marked vehicles are seen in the video uploaded. The same video was subsequently deleted and an alternate cut was uploaded the following day. Following numerous police shootings in the media Harvey uploaded a lyric video by the name of "FTP" to his Facebook page in August 2014.FTP on Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/harveyjlive/videos/10152599687571900/ This video went viral and went on to reach 1.2 million views and over 11,000 shares among the social media site.FTP Release: http://carolinaontherise.com/video-durham-boiz-fuck-the-police-2/ Following the success of the lyric video the song was released for sale on iTunes.
