[[Miss Universe 2012]] }} '''Lindsay Japal''' (born 1987) is a [[beauty pageant]] titleholder who was crowned Miss [[Cayman Islands]] 2011, was represented of the [[Cayman Islands]] in the 2011 [[Miss World]] and 2012 [[Miss Universe 2012|Miss Universe]] pageant.(26 Sept 2012). [http://www.compasscayman.com/caycompass/2012/09/26/Reigning-Miss-Cayman-Islands-maintains-active-role-in-training/ Reigning Miss Cayman Islands maintains active role in training ], ''Compass Cayman'' ==Early life== Japal was born in [[George Town, Cayman Islands]]. She is of mixed race - her mother is a full Caymanian (of [[White Caymanian|European]] ancestry), while her father is a Grenadian who immigrated to the Cayman Islands in the early 80s. She has an Associate degree in Business Administration from the University College of the Cayman Islands. She is currently working towards a Bachelors degree in Finance with a minor in Marketing from the University College of the Cayman Islands. Her motto is "As you grow older you will discover that you have two hands, one for helping yourself, one for helping others." ==Miss Cayman Islands== Japal was named as the grand winner of Miss Cayman Islands 2011 beauty pageant on 24 September 2011 at the Lion's Community Centre in George Town, Cayman Islands, by Cristin Alexander, Miss Cayman Islands 2010.{{cite web|url=http://www.timesofbeauty.com/2011/09/miss-cayman-islands-20112012.html |title=Lindsay Japal is Miss Cayman Islands 2011/2012 |publisher=The Time of Beauty |date= |accessdate=2011-09-25}} She continued to hold the title through 2014 because a successor pageant had not yet been held.Boushy, Lisa (5 Sept 2014).
