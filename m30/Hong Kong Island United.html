The team is nominally based in [[Hong Kong]] and was formed in 2016 to compete in the [[Hong Kong T20 Blitz]] League. The franchise is owned by Leonine Global Investments through its sports entity Leonine Global Sports. The team is captained by Pakistan's most successful test captain [[Misbah-ul-Haq]] and coached by former [[Australian cricket team|Australian]] player [[Ryan Campbell (cricketer)|Ryan Campbell]].
