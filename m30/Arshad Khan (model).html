He became popular online after Pakistani photographer Javeria (Jiah Ali) took his picture during a photowalk in the Itwar Bazaar area of Islamabad, and uploaded it on Instagram. He formerly worked as a fruit seller at the same Sunday market, but in mid 2016, he started working at the tea stall. He has 17 siblings.
