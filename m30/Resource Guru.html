Percy Stilwell | location_city = [[London]] | location_country = [[England]] | services = [[Web application]]s | products = resource scheduling, calendars | homepage = {{url|http://www.resourceguruapp.com/}} }} '''Resource Guru''' is a web based resource scheduling and calendar tool company located in [[London]], [[England]]. They specialise in online collaborative software that can be used for employment, project management, events, task management or resource planning. == History == Andrew Rogoff and Percy Stilwell started prototyping the application in 2009{{cite news|title=If You Want To Find Resource Scheduling Zen, I’ve Got Just The Guru|url=http://killerstartups.com/people/if-you-want-to-find-resource-scheduling-zen-ive-got-just-the-guru|publisher=killerstartups.com|accessdate=8 March 2016}} in Cape Town after years using spreadsheets for resource scheduling. They saw an opportunity the market wasn't satisfying and decided to build their own online resource scheduling tool. The company was founded in May 2011 in London{{cite news|title=Index Leads $870K Seed Round For Resource Guru’s Cloud-Based Team Scheduling App|url=http://techcrunch.com/2014/01/19/resource-guru-seed/|publisher=TechCrunch|accessdate=8 March 2016}} and currently employs over 20 staff.{{cite web|title=About Resource Guru|url=http://resourceguruapp.com/about-resource-guru|publisher=Resource Guru|accessdate=8 March 2016}} They have received over $1.4 million in funding since 2014. == Products == Resource Guru offers an online collaborative application for resource scheduling and planning. Instead of using spreadsheets to manage staff, equipment and other resources they offer a replacement with its cloud-based team scheduling software. Rather than offering a freemium version, they require all users to buy a subscription after a free trial. Packages range from $19 a month to $99 or more. Companies such as [[Vodafone]], [[Deloitte]], [[Cisco]], [[Yahoo!]] and [[VMware]] have used the online application.{{cite web|title=Resource Guru Pricing|url=https://secure.resourceguruapp.com/pricing-signup|publisher=Resource Guru|accessdate=8 March 2016}} == References == {{Reflist}} == External links == * {{official website|http://www.resourceguruapp.com/}} [[Category:Business software]] [[Category:Collaborative software]] [[Category:Administrative software]]
